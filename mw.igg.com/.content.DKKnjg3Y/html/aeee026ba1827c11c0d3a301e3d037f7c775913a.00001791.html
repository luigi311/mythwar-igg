Making Money in MW2

I will enclose ways i have found to make money in mw2 and i will keep adding as time goes on. the reason: skills cost a lot after awhile..

1) Go to an area capture pets and go back to sky passage and sell them to pet npc.
2) Survivor
3) Hurricanes - hope to get AoD
4) Grinding for money
5) thief quest - repetition(Chuck)

1) Capturing Pets

Sounds easy and it kind of is. There are two different ways/reasons to capture pets. One reason to capture pets is for the high level pets because not alot of the people can get them therefore they are worth a lot. Another reason is simply to start off the game get some money to buy some skills or maybe even because you are a collector. One way to catch pets is to be a human hurt the monster a bit put it to sleep and boom its pretty much yours. another way is to bring lots of mp pots have your pet chip away at it as you try to catch it ea round until its yours. The pets can then be brought to sky passage pet npc if they are a certain type or sold to people.

2) Survivor

For those of you who are veterans or played vival in mw1 you can skip this section. for those who havent. strategies for it change but the basic setup is typically 1 cent 1 human and the rest borgs and mages up to 2 mages 1 borg or vice versa. You fight waves of monsters ea getting progressivly harder and you get more money for ea wave. if you stop you get money from all waves if you die well too bad then no money for you. If you do very well in it you can make a fortune.

3) Hurricanes

Hurricanes are groups of monsters like humans that you defeat in order. If one is dead he appears dead just like you would. However he can be revived by his teammates just like you can. You must defeat hurricanes in order so 1 then 2, then 3 etc. The leader also has a chance at getting AoD (Armor of Dreams) which can sell for a lot and is a pretty nefty piece of armor. You can however go back and do 1-4 after you finish 4 if you wish if the hurricanes are too strong for you onwards.

4) Grinding for Money

Exactly how it sounds, fight and fight and fight will obtaining money ea battle you win.

5) Thief Quest

Start at bar waitress in wood. you follow what they tell you and boom your done get contribution points which can be redeemed at the redeem steward for money.

will add more later. as well as a section explainin each one in  detail. fill free to submit all your ideas : )
