Mythwar "2" Quest List

Note
This list is not complete. This list is probably inaccurate. This list was obtained through crude translation from the mighty Google (Systran wouldn't do it). Obviously some quests will traverse to a higher level (seal etc). Some quests you may recognise, some have been split into segments (Stonesmith) don't know why, it just sorta... happened. Soulstones have each changed required level, Hellion allegedly provides "great experience", all those monsters have moved, likewise with Bounty....

So, take it with a grain of salt, enjoy the moment and take a peek.
