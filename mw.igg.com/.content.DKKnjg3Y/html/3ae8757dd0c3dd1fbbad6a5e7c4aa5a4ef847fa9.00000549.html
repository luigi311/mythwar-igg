3# A A A Post at 2008-6-11 05:20 AM  Show author
i have never seen such posts.

for heal, i think max heal ii is roughly mokka max heal iv. if you don't care about maxing skills later on, there is almost no point maxing heal ii or iv as heal iv mokka max can get you by in most situations. max heal iv is costly and shouldn't be used carelessly.  

if you are going to max heal at some point, then it's wise to max ii first, at this point your ii and iv will heal the same amount with roughly same mp. so you use ii (because animation is shorter), and you can train iv to max, then you would have heal iv as an emergy heal option.

if you start by maxing iv first, then you won't have a cost-effective way to heal until you max ii.

for multi, personally think it's not worth to use either ii or iv, especially before you reborn. it costs a lot of mp and you aren't likely to get good damage worth the cost, and animation takes a long time. unless you are planning int cent, then maybe. but if you have to choose to train, then train multi ii. the cost is more acceptable. max multi iv, after one cast you might run the risk of not having enough mp to heal when you need.
