Killer Guides for Myth WarⅡ Collection

We have heard your complaints about how leveling seems to take ages and that you want to know the rewarding secrets possessed by many of the leading edge players that help them to figure out the shortcuts to completing the quests. Or you, yourself may have some special approaches to upgrading your equipment and lose no time getting your hands on high-level quality gear pieces. Now we want to share these secrets and strategy guides with everyone in our international gaming community. Some quite special awards can be won by submitting your one-of-a-kind game walkthroughs to us.

Event duration: March 24th 2009 to April 10th 2009

Rules:
1. Entrants must contain their accounts and character names (may be hidden) in their posts. Works must be tagged with “Guide” and posted in  the Event Zone.
2. Walkthroughs or strategy guides must be original works of authorship. If any similar posts are found, then only the first one posted will be credited with authorship.
3. Walkthroughs or strategy guides must be well written and easy to follow. Pictures are highly preferred.
4. There are no limits on the text length. Walkthroughs or strategy guides must be un-offensive (no profanity or derogatory comments), otherwise the entry will be instantly disqualified.
5. The winners of the work chosen by us will receive prizes. IGG will own any and all rights to use their work.

Judgment:
We will select the winners to be awarded with the 1st prize and 2nd prize. All the remaining participants will receive consolation prizes.
The pot is sweetened by:
The 1st Prize (One player): Capricornus Pet
The 2nd Prize (Three players): one Blue Chinchilla
Consolation Prizes: four kinds of the Saint SSP each



Welcome to Participate the Event!  Come ON!!!
