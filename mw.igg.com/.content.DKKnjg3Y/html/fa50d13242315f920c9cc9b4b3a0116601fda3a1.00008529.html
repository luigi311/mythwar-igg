  Game Guide
 

Armory

In the Armory you can buy any kind of armor, and weapon, but depending on your level you will need to buy that level's armor in different cities. In the following chart you will be able to see the armor's level that are been sold in each city.

Woodlingor - you can buy armor for level 0-29 here.

Blython - you can buy armor for level 30-39 here.

Demon Square - you can buy armor for level 40-59 here.

Desert City - you can buy armor for level 60 or more here.
(The name of the shop is Weaponry here, not Armory)

If it has (*) in the end it means that in that city you will have to go to Weaponry to buy weapons. The weapons will still be the same price as the armors.

Bank (Sometimes it is called MW Bank)

In the bank you can deposit gold or items, you can go withdraw them whenever you want to. There is a limit of gold you can carry and this limit includes the gold in bank. This limit of gold depends on your level, if you want to see the chart go to this link (http://www.focusplay.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=252) The bank is more useful for items, there is a limit for items in the bank too, but you can have more than twenty-four (24) items that is the limit on bag.

Bar

In bar you can take different kind of quests. In the next table you will see which quests you can get in each city.

Woodlingor - Thief Quest (talk to Bar Waitress) Mystical Fruits Quest (talk to Igor)

Blython - none

Demon Square - none

Desert City - none

Craftshop

In the craftshop you will be able to smith any part of your armor. This will add defense to the armor. At the moment the craftshop in Demon Square is not working. We are working to put it to work as soon as possible.


Grocery

In Grocery you will be able to buy stuff like teleporters and items used for resetting pet's status, attributes and loyalty. In the following table you will see the names of all the items you can buy in Grocery and what do they
work for.

Stone of Loyalty - brings your pet's loyalty back to 100. (4,000 gold)

Stone of Deams - brings your pet attributes (level) back to 0. (500 gold)

Devil's Tear - keeps monsters ten (10) levels lower than you away for a period of time. (500 gold)

Devil's Blood - needed to start a pk (player kill) battle. (2,000 gold) (Still not working)

Teleport Scroll - lets you set an exact location and later you can use it to automatically teleport into that location set before. (200 gold)

Wasteland Teleport Scroll - lets you teleport your whole team to Wastelands Maps. (4,000 gold)

Desolate Teleport Scroll - lets you teleport your whole team to Desolate Maps. (4,000 gold)

City Teleport Scroll - lets you teleport your whole team to City Maps. (4,000 gold)

Dungeons Teleport Scroll - lets you teleport your whole team to Dungeon Maps. (9,000 gold)

Gem Tracker - helps you track the 7 dragon balls for the weekly quest. (3,000 gold)

Myth Manual - shows you a few basics of the game. (200 gold)

Battery - used to replenish energy to your mercenary in the quest taken in Blython's City Hall. (2,000 gold)

Communicator - lets you know what is happening with your mercenary. (5,000
gold)

Rose - increases relationship. (10,000 gold)

Crystal Ball - increases friendship. (25,000 gold)


Herbal Shop

In the Herbal Shop you can buy anything you will need to replenish your mana points (mp) and/or health points (hp). In the following table you will be able to see which kind of potions you can buy in each city, and how much each heals.

Woodlingor
Apple - 200 hp (10 gold)
Banana - 500 hp (30 gold)
Lychee - 200 mp (10 gold)
Pear - 500 mp (50 gold)
Bid Red Potion - 60% hp (13,000 gold)
Big Blue Potion - 50% mp (15,000 gold)

Blython
Graded Apple - 2,000 hp (200 gold)
Graded Banana - 5,000 hp (600 gold)
Graded Lychee - 1,500 mp (220 gold)
Graded Pear - 4,000 mp (1,000 gold)
Bid Red Potion - 60% hp (13,000 gold)
Big Blue Potion - 50% mp (15,000 gold)

Demon Square
Red Salad - 8,000 hp (1,000 gold)
Wild Lychee - 7,000 mp (2,000 gold)
Bid Red Potion - 60% hp (13,000 gold)
Big Blue Potion - 50% mp (15,000 gold)


Desert City
Banana Sushi - 13,000 hp (1,800 gold)
Wild Pear - 12,000 mp (4,000 gold)
Bid Red Potion - 60% hp (13,000 gold)
Big Blue Potion - 50% mp (15,000 gold)


Stuff Shop

In the Stuff Shop you can buy a variety of jewels that will help you increase stuff like MP and HP. In the following chart you will see the level of jewels you can buy in each city. Bangles of Devil will increase hp, Bangles of Magic will increase mp and Bangles of Bowen will increase both hp and mp.

Woodlingor - jewels for level 0-19

Blython - jewels for level 20-49

Demon Square - jewels for level 50-79

Desert City - jewels for level 80 or more
