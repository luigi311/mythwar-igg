Halloween Crafts and Activities


It is once again time for our annual Halloween haunting entertainment. Spooky cats, black bats, Halloween vampires and all manner of things that go bump in the night are set to freak you out. So join us to celebrate in style by trick-or-treating, Jack O'Lanterns, and spooky magic performances. We are getting into the Halloween party mood by holding a Halloween Card Making Contest. Everyone who enters stands the chance to win some frighteningly good prizes!

Event duration: October 22 to November 3
Judgment: November 4 to November 7

Event details:
Players can take part in this event by creating amusing Halloween cards which are based on our game
(Image processing is allowed)

 Rules:
 1. Players can participate by replying to this thread.
2. Players may submit only one entry.
3. Participants must hide their accounts, servers, character names and IDs in their replies.
4. All participating works must be original. Any work violating this rule will be automatically disqualified. If two entries are judged to be similar or the same then only the first reply to our thread will be counted
5. Participating work is not allowed to contain any content that goes against our board rules. All such work will be deemed invalid and their owners will be automatically disqualified.
6. IGG reserves the final rights to interpret the details of this event and the property rights of the award-winning works.
Prizes:
We will pick out 6 participating players to be our award winners and another 5 participating players to be rewarded for their participation. :

The winner: one player will be rewarded with a Deity Beast
The runner-up: two players will be each rewarded with one Constellation pet or one Howling Beast
The bronze medalist: three players will each be rewarded with 300 integrals or 250 silver ingots apiece.
5 participating players will each be rewarded with 150 integrals or 100 silver ingots apiece.
