Aries Pack Released


Dear Players,

We are glad to inform you that Aries Pack has been released in our Item Mall. Aries Pet Egg and other pet-related items are only sold at 1997 Item Mall Points. Cut-throat price!!! You can't miss it.  Let's see what the pack contains.

Aries Pack: It contains a pet egg and 6 other articles. When you redeem it from NPC, there should be at least 7 blanks in your inventory.   
Aries
GrowthRate:1.250,Skill:Flash,Resistance:Drain/Poison/Frailty
Icy Crystal Pack: It contains 45 Icy Crystals and you could take them out when using it. It will increase your participating pet’s intimacy 200 points.      
Dragon Bone: It could be used directly and it will increase the in-battle pet's 6 points at random and 0.01 growth rate. Limit to 4 usages per pet. Note: Stone of Moon will reset it.    
  Dragon Eye: It could be used directly. Pet growth rate will be increased by 0.1 when used. Limit to 1 usage per pet. Note: Stone of Moon will reset it.
  Gold Card: It contains 10000000 gold, which can be withdraw in once or several times.
Stone of Dream: After giving it to Pet researcher (338,390) outside the City Hall at Blython, the pet's level will be reset to level 1. The grow rate will be changed (up or down at random).

Stone of Curses: It could be used directly. After using, it will delete the resistance effect of the pet in battle and renew to its nature resistance.

Total items: 7

Who is gonna be the first to own Aries Pack? Click here to the Item mall.
