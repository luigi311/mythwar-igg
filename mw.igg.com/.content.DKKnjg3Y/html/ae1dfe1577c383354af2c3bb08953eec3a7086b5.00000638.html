Censor

I think that a censor is a good start, stops a lot of stuff on world from getting out of hand. However, those that do use a bit of flavor in their speech, are often times even more frustrated to see it come up as ****s. For that reason, I feel that there should be a button that allows people to turn on and off the censor. To make matters easier of course, it could always start with on when a new character is created.

On a similar note. I have discovered that you can get around the censor in MW2 with a simple # and a space after the first letter. So the word ****, changed to f# uck, will come out as the word on chat. This works with all banned words. I don't know of any combination of such, but this is how I get around it when frustrated in party chat. I thought you mods/**s or whatever, would like to know about this little tidbit, and I hope it helps in sorting out how people manage to say censored words.
