New Year, New Activity

Introduction:

Happy New Year to you all! If you are looking for fun for the start of the year, then you are welcome to join in the New Activity in the Myth World!

Event Duration: From 0:00 1st Jan.to 23:59 5th, Jan.

Players above level 30 are qualified to participate in it

Activity Flow:
1. Get Ancient Egg: Go to Blython's Church and find the pretty New Year Angel at (165, 540), after talking with her, she'll give you an Ancient Egg which will later lead you to carry on a series of interesting quests. Each player can only get one Ancient egg which can neither be exchanged nor traded. It will be invalid after the activity has been completed.

2. Absorb magic power: Go to the Life Tree 1 and the Revive Arena to absorb magic power by challenging monsters. When the ancient eggs have absorbed enough magic power to grow, you will go onto next step of hatching. Group work is recommended for fast magic absorption. Leading new players to fight will contribute to the acceleration of the level of magic absorption.

3. Baptize: After enough magic has absorbed, go to Blython  find the New Year Spirit and give her one +8 Gem to receive blessing for the ancient egg. After that, go to Woodlingor(390.290) to find the New Year Fairy.

4. Receive blessing: Go to the New Year Fairy to receive 20 blessing points and get a chance to be rewarded one of the following four items: Fireworks, Dragon Stone, Solar Stone and Music Box.

Tips: 5 free ancient eggs are available for each player everyday. You must pay 1000000 gold to get another new one from the New Year Angel. After this activity is completed, you can click on the "Ancient Egg" to delete the eggs you are holding and the relevant quest records.

Send blessing: You also could go to find the Priest and leave blessings in the Blessing Pool to accumulate 10 blessing points every half hour and get the chance to be rewarded with one of the following four items: Fireworks, Dragon Stone, Solar Stone and Music Box.

Rewardsots of rewards will be given out. There are Christmas Pets, Deity Pet, Character and Pet Experience, Mystical Fruit, Gem, Essence and Armor of Dreams.

OK, wish you all will enjoy it! ^-^



Get what you are Event

Event Time: everyday from 4th Jan. to 6th Jan.

Instruction:
The GM will hide in one place and announce which map he is in and which particular pet in the System Channel he has. Players will then use the Shape shifting Potion for the announced pet and the first one to find the GM and joins in their party wins the named pet. So if the GM says "I'm looking for a Capilla in the Bone Desert", then the first player to find the GM, join his team and use the Capilla’s shape shifting potion will win the pet as a prize.  The Shape shifting Potion and location will change every time.

Note: Player must use the Shapeshifting Potion, not give the Shapeshifting Potion to the GM.

Rewards: The pet that the GM chose.

[quote:56cb594140="B1adez"][quote:56cb594140="corybanticfire"]What is a +8 gem and where does it come from?[/quote]

a +8 gem is a Grade 8 gem. (Level 8) whatever you want to refer to it as. (They give resists, or add critical, berserk, dodge etc)

There are a few ways of obtaining the "+8 gem"

Hurricane 7 = Drops +8 gems.
Mining/Miners (Drops +1 gems and +3 gems) (You'll need to combine them to get to +8)
Buying Bags of Magic/Venom etc from Item mall, and Combine them to +8
Guild war rewards if you are level 60-69 I believe.
Titans (The 3 big guys in desert city, Gusting Titan, Moss Titan and Blazing Titan) Defeating all 3 titans in under one minute will reward you with 15 +6 Gems, once you give them to the guild cleric, You'll need to combine them up to +8.
Lottery, Getting 1st place in the lottery can award you with a +8 gem.[/quote]

there are at least 2 more reasonable ways to get +8 gems:

1. lottery. one of the prizes.. i think one possible first prize.
2. spam wishing. this makes sense for the event. you have a chance to get wailing stone, which gives random gems from lvl 5 to 10. so 1/6 chance, i guess, to get +8 from one of those. make 50 alts and rotate login them to wish. sometimes you get something else like dragon stone, firework, etc. but with 50 alts every half an hour, it's likely to get at least one +8.

but if you can beat titans, it is much easier than anything else.
