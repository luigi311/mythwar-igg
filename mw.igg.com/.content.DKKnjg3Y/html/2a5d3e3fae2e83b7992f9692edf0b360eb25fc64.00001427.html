Coordinating Your Bounty Party

This is a quest for lvl 60-80. However, it is not popular mainly of the low exp it gives. But, this quest is still good for the GM 'Bounty Hunter' event. Therefore, you have to set the best record which requires good coordination of your party members and of course, your pets.

Information

Order of NPC to finish the quest

1) Bloodreaper - Life Tree 3/tol3 (155,190)

2) Firetroy - Flamingor (75,360)

3) Bonepoker - Devil's Gate (46,222)

4) Eyebasher - Evil Lair 2 (64,110) pet shop in Demon Square

5) Fist Scarer - Sunset Plains (99.195)

Recommended Party
You want to kill them fast? You need to play all out offense. So recommended party will be int mage/cent, borg, fhuman, any character with offensive spells and can do high damage.

- Mage Strategy
Pure int is the best for highest output spell damage. Wear HP boots or draconic necklace to increase amount of hp. If you want to deal the best damage you can, go all out int equips with glimmer gem incrusted. Try to get mages who can hit 5.

- Cent Strategy
Like the mage, pure int is the best for highest multishot/blizzard damage. Cent should be int mainly as they will need some HP to prevent 1 hit ko from occuring. Again, wear hp boots or draconic necklace to increase your hp if needed. If you want to deal the best damage you can, go all out int equips with glimmer gem incrusted. Getting the highest blizz/mulishot lvl cent will be good

- Borg Strategy
Borg only need to drain 1-2 times at most in each battle. So what they will need is critical or berserk equips. Necklace and wepons with them will be good. Sta borgs will get critical while str borgs can get either berserk or critical. Those who are mainly agi or int borgs will have to be more careful as they have low hp. So increase thier hp with draconic necklac or Hp boots again. Again higher drain lvl = more targets and more hp taken away

- Human Strategy
You dont need chaos, stun, hypno, frail because they will not help you with dealing damage. So, male human is not recommended for this quest.
Fhuman is good because of thier poison, although thier damage will not be as high as the other races, they can still kill most mobs in this quest. You can try having crit or berk wep/neck depending on how you build them like the borg.

Ideal Speed Order
Cent -&gt; Borg -&gt; Mage
Borg -&gt; Cent -&gt; Mage
Human can be fit nicely in any part if you have one.

PETS
You want to kill fast, you need to go all out offense. Pets are your best allies in this quest. You will need attack or offensive pets. Depending on how you build the pet, they will be mainly str/sta. Try not to use fast pets to attack. Let the mages kill first. If there is one target left due to overlapping of targets, slow pet attack. Less animation = less time.

Recommended pets resistance will be critical and berserk. This will greatly help in killing the quest mob fast. Critical is good for eyebasher and fist scarer beause of thie high hp. Remember you would want to wipe them out as soon as possible before they deal serious damage to your party and cause some to free tele. Try not to heal as much as you can because it does not help in killing the quest mobs. Berserk is useful for high attack pets and is good for all the quest mobs because most of them have little physical resistance and defense.  

It is optional to have a santa in a party if there is no cent or the cent do not want to heal in this quest. So it can save the members from free tele

Fighting With Time
Remember this as you will want to get the best record you can achieve, Take note of the following as they will affect the record set.

- Leader
He needs fast fingers to click monster text and tele scrolls. Also needed to start a battle fast

- Party lag
If you lag, you slow down the party.....

- Animation Skill
Some skills require longer time for animation like drain IV, fire IV, ice IV. So you should use skills with shorter animaton time in order to speed up the battle

- Teleport Scrolls
Buy flagbag from item mall and record them at firetroy,bonepoker, eyebaser and fist scarer. Once you are done with a battle, tele to the next so you dont have to play with luck and walk from a distance.

Hope you enjoy the guide
