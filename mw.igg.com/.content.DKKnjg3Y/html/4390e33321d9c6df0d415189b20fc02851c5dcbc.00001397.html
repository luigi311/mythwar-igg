Pets resist/boost and Items

Crystals are stones to give resistance to your pet.. This way your pet can be stronger, receive less damage or has a less chance to get affected by any human (or mages Death) skill..

There are 5 different Crystals which can be imbued on diffirent pet levels..
The blue Crystal (+1) at pet level 30
The red Crystal (+2) at pet level 50
The green Crystal (+3) at pet level 70
The purple Crystal (+4) at pet level 1-1
The black Crystal (+5) at pet level 1-1

IF the +3 crystal hasnt been added before reborn you have to wait untill your pet is lvl 1-70 to add the +3 +4 and +5 crystal

All Crystals can be bought at the Crystal Seller..

Blython City (260,460)

They are quite expensive;
+1 = 100.000 gold each
+2 = 150.000 gold each
+3 = 200.000 gold each
+4 = 300.000 gold each
+5 = 500.000 gold each

With a little effort you can go to Demon Square's Pet Shop; "Evil Lair" (460,160) or Desert City Pet Shop Demon Lair (340,190) to hunt for Crystals.. In the Lairs there are 4 different maps, Evil/Demon Lair 1, 2, 3 and 4.. In these battle maps you have to face Spirits.. Spirits are NPCs which appear ones every hour, announced by the System in chat.. In each level Lair are different kinds of Spirits with each their own resistance, level and skills.. The ones from Lair 2 are stronger then the one from Lair 1, and so on (but mostly depending on what skill you use and what resist u wear)
This one can be found in Lair 1
This one can be found in Lair 2
This one can be found in Lair 3
This one can be found in Lair 4
This one can also be found in Lair 4



From these so called Spirits you can also get Pet Eggs..


These Pet Eggs can be hatched at the Lair 1 entrance at the Pet Breeder.. (not the Pet Breeder at Space Tunnel, because thats a quest NPC)


There is a drop rate for both Crystals as for Pet Eggs.. One time you will get a lot, and the other time u get non..

With the Crystals you can go to Baia, Koradji and Nexo in Blython City (330,430).. From them you will get pet resistance if you hand over a Crystal..

Baia will give you Magic resistance
Koradji will give you Elemental resistance
Nexo will give you Physical resistance

Magical is Death, Flash, Evil, Fire, Ice and Anti-Physical resistance
Elemental is Chaos, Stun, Hypnotise, Poison and Frailty resistance
Physical is Combo, Counter, Pierce, Critical, Berserk boost and Hit/Dodge rate

Anti-Physical should of been given by Nexo since its a physical attribute

Combo : the amount of hits u make in 1 turn (following hit 50% less damage)..
Counter : hits back when enemy using physical attacks..
Pierce : Should be breaking enemies Anti-Physical resistance..
Critical : hits a % of enemies max HP
Berserk : % of base damage + base damage

Each Crystal can give you an amount of resistance or a boost..
For Magic and Elemental 13 resist per Crystal is the highest you can get..
For Physical its diffirent;
Berserk / Pierce / Hit/Dode 13/13 (Rate/Damage)
Critical 10/10 (Rate/Damage)
Combo / Counter 13/1 (Rate/Amount of hits)

Every resistance is put in Rate except for Magical resist which is put in %..
You do it as followed; hand over a Crystal to the NPC of your choice.. This NPC will give you a random offer.. You can chose to accept the offer or you can chose to refuse the offer.. If you chose to accept the offer, congratulations! Your pet got resistance! But if you refuse the offer, you can try another time, you will lose the Crystal you gave and have to give a new one.. This you can repeat as often as you want.. Ones you have the resistance you need, you can give a new Crystal of a higher grade and so on..

Deity pets and Saint Beasts have a EXTRA option.. The owners can go to Blython City (270,470) and talk to Defiler..

This NPC gives your pet 45 EXTRA resistance to the 2 which you prefer compaired to normal pets, 30 resistance for your first choice and 15 resistance for your second choice.. The previous resist will be gone so you could see it as an very nice EXTRA option instead of EXTRA resist.

Hit and Dodge rate wont be gone after selecting your new resist


Alteration is an option to change the initial resistance.. This option will transfer existing pet eggs resistance onto your current battle pet, thus changing your battle pets initial resistance.. Your old resistance will be gone! For this you can go to Liena in Blython City (240,470)..

If there are crystals added to this pet, you have to use a SoC first before Alteration


To change the resistance you added with Crystals you have to use a Stone of Curse.. These stones can be bought in the Item Mall on the website, by using real money to purchase them..



To reset your pet you need a Stone of Dreams.. These stones can be bought in the Item Mall on the website, by using real money to purchase them.. This will reset pets level and Growth.. Pet will be level 1 again, all stats will be resumed to normal and added resistance will be gone..

This can be done at Blython Citys Pet Researcher (340,390)..



To reset the stat points you added with each level up you have to use a Stone of Moon.. These stones can be bought in the Item Mall on the website, by using real money to purchase them.. This will also infect the Growth of the pet..

This can be done at Blython Citys Gado (280,430)



If a pet dies during battle its loyalty will decrease. If loyalty points become below 60, the pet can refuse to obey you. To deal with that, you can purchase the Stone Of Loyalty from the Groceror NPC and use it to increase the pets loyalty. It can be bought for a small amount of gold ingame..



There are also other Items in the Item Mall like Dragon Eye and Dragon Bone..
Dragon Eye will give your pet +0.1 growth and can be used only ones! Stone of Moon and Stone of Dreams resets it..



Dragon Bone will give your pet +0.01 growth +6 stat points will be added.. can be used 4 times.. Those 6 x 4 = 24 stat points will be re-addeble after reborn.. Stone of Moon and Stone of Dreams resets it..




Intimacy of a pet controls the pets physical boosts.. This means; the higher the intimacy, the better the boosts do their job.. This only counts for Counter, Combo and Berserk, although many of us believe that it also affects Hit/Dodge and Critical.. So it only counts for Physical boosts..

You can get intimacy by fighting monsters, PK, Icy Crystals and by giving grade 1 till 5 Gems in Guild HQ 3 at Blython City hall (280,410) to Lucretia..


Grade 1 gem gives 7 intimacy..
Grade 2 gem gives 14 intimacy..
Grade 3 gem gives 28 intimacy..
Grade 4 gem gives 56 intimacy..
Grade 5 gem gives 112 intimacy..

To check the intimacy of your pet you can also ask a Healer NPC aswell as Lucretia..


You need 50.000 intimacy to reborn your pet.. This is only possible if you have a reborn character, the pets level reached 100 and if u have 100 reputation (or more).. Your pet will be able to be level 1-120 after reborn and will also get some free stat points.. The name of the pet will change from green to orange..
The fastest way to get so much intimacy is to buy Gem bags, or even faster are Icy Crystals, both from Item Mall.. A Bag of Magic contains 50 grade 5 gems.. But to use the gems you need Service Rate from your guild.. 1 hour online = 1 Service Rate, and giving 1 gem cost 1 Service Rate.. Icy Crystals dont need Service rate, you can just use them, there are 45 Icy Crystals in 1 bag and each crystal gives your pet 200 intimacie.


If anything is wrong, or something is missing, post below or pm me, ty
