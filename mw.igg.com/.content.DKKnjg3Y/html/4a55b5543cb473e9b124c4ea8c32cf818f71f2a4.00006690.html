 May I save couple quotes from Economy concepts:
Quote:
A free price system or free price mechanism is an economic system where prices are not set by government or a central planning board but by the interchange of supply and demand, with the resulting prices being understood as signals that are communicated between producers and consumers which serve to guide the production and distribution of resources. Through the free price system, supplies are rationed, income is distributed, and resources are allocated.[/u].


In such case, the pics proof nothing (only scams) , since there's not a real demand on MCs, Mokkas, MFs, or low grade Gems .. (*)

The seller will try to get max profit from his good, and the buyer will try to get max income from his money/good. At this point will be an arrange or dismiss of trade. If the good is so preciuos for the buyer and so hard to get that only has one source, it will be a trade then. But if the seller pushes too much over the price and buyer's incoming capacity, probably wont be an arrange. So we all see here the "invisible hand".

This, the real demand only happens with Pets and valuable items from Mall. Not with the common items easy to get from game. (*)

As last quote:
Quote:
A free market is a market where the price of an item is arranged by the mutual consent of sellers and buyers, with the supply and demand of that item not being regulated by a government. However, while a free market necessitates that government does not regulate supply, demand, and prices, it also requires the traders themselves do not coerce or defraud each other, so that all trades are morally voluntary.
