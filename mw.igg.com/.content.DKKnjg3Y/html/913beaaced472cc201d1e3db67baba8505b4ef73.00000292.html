Recent Login Problems

Recently, our servers have been moved to a new location, which has caused problems for some players trying to log on. The servers have been moved in order to provide a more stable and reliable service in future, however, while the worldwide DNS servers automatically update across the internet, players in some areas may experience problems logging on. The delays are unfortunately unavoidable, but should be resolved within 1-2 days.

IGG apologizes for any inconvenience caused by this move, and we thank you for your patience during this upgrade period.
