  MythWar 2.0 Preview
Dear Players,

These days, all players have been expecting for the promotion of new system in Myth War. Myth War Team is working hard on it all the time. Here, we will announce some information about the Mythwar II. We will open a board for poll and advice on the forum, and we are looking forward to your participation.

1. New images of races: All the four races will take on brand new images.
2. 4 scenes added: Players will experience a completely different world for all MythWar maps changed.
3. New pet system: The function of “Pet Combination”, “Pet Longevity” and “Catch Pet” will be added.
4. New prentice system: Players will be able to recruit prentices.
5. New marriage system: Players will be able to marry in the game.
6. New mail system: Players will be able to send mail or items to others without meeting face to face.
7. New online item mall: Players will be able to purchase items anytime and anywhere.
8. New support items: Powerful support items will bring more convenience and fun for players to train.
9. New movement function: After clicking a destination on the minimap, the character will go there automatically.
10. New enchanting system: Players will be able to attach various special attributes to gears.
11. New server switch function: Players will be able to change server without login out the game.
12. New operation interface: The more humanized design will enable the operation to be easier
13. New skill system: More reasonable skill system will enable players to upgrade skills easier.
14. New auction system: The better auction system will present to players.
15. New military titles: New military titles will be added.
16. New forging system: Players will be able to forge and enhance gears.
17. New gear system: New gears will increase the hit rate and damage great deal.

PS: All the questions we all collected,we will answer them recently,thanks for support.
