Bored Manual Volume 1

For the newbies that actually bother to read the forum



In the main interface, the following keys are useful:
F1 to bring out the helpful info as shown above.
Tab to bring out minimap.
ESC to quit/log out
PrintScreen to print screenshot
F2 to switch between 4 views
_____Default: See other chars with spirite and title, name and rank
_____1* F2:   See other chars w/o spirite but with title, name and rank
_____2* F2:   See other chars with spirite but w/o title, name and rank
_____3* F2:   Hide all other chars.
_____4* F2:   Back to default.
_____Useful for hiding other chars to see ONLY NPCs to access teleporter,
        mokka etc and reduce lag.

Top left

Char Icon, Health bar and Mana bar
Clicking on Char Icon will bring up the char stats window.
Right Clicking on Char Icon will bring up the inventory window.
The Health and Mana bars are your char HP and MP, as u are damaged or cast some spells using your MP, part of the bar will be empty.
Right Clicking on these bars will fill them up instantly, using appropiate HP or MP potions from your inventory. Lowest Grade of potion will be chosen first, such as lychees instead of wild pears.

Below these

Claw Icon, Health bar, Exp bar and Mana Bar
These are for your pet.
Clicking on Claw Icon brings up the pet window to choose which pet to use, follow, add stats etc.
Right Clicking on Claw Icon will bring up the inventory window for pet usage, to feed them HP/MP potions, not stone of loyalty, mystical fruits.
Health bar and Mana bar work the same way as the Char Bars.
Exp Bar indicates the exp earned towards the next level. When the bar is full, the pet will level.

Below these

Party Char Icons
Sometimes there are somemore Char Icons.
This happens when u are in a party. The above pic illustrates a party of a female cent and a female borg.

Top Right

Map & Coordinates
Together tell u the exact location where u are.
Weather Icon
Indicates the current weather status.
For those who understand Traditional Chinese, the following link has detailed info on weather effects.
http://www.mythonline.com.tw/mod ... 0204/1107495030.htm
Fullscreen Button or ALT Enter
Will alternates between fullscreen mode and windows mode.
If u are multilogging, and switch from windows mode to fullscreen mode, all other mythwar clients will shut down.
World Map Button or ALT-D
Shows a world map
Mini Map Button or TAB
Shows the map of the current area, where a shining square indicates where u are on the map.

Bottom Left Panel of Buttons

Switch Button
There are 2 places where a chat window can be. One on the bottom left
corner of the screen. Another will be a separate chat window from the
Mythwar Client on the right side. Switch button alternates between the
2 chat window styles. The separate chat window will not be available in
fullscreen mode and for windows mode too if your windows display
settings are too small to see the extra chat window.

Record Button
This will brings up the message log history which contains the whole list
of messages in various chat channels. GM channel and Info channel are
exceptions.


Block Button
After selecting a target via the target's ID, pressing the Block Button will
block future messages from all channels from the target AFTER u relog.

Expression Button
Brings up a list of expressions to use in your messages, a max of 3
expressions per message. Typing #0 will have the same result as
choosing   from the list.

Scroll Button
Pressing once will stop the chat window for automatically scrolling down
when someone types anything. For u to scroll up and check what u want
if u dont wish to use record button fuction instead. Pressing a second
time will start the automatically scrolling down again.

Changes Button
For those using chat window on bottom left style. Pressing it will change
the length of the chat window.

Scroll Up Button
To scroll up the chat window. Use it after u stop the chat window for
automatically scrolling down or u will get frustrated.

Scroll Down Button
Dont think need to explain.

Bottom Right Panel of Icons

Inventory Icon, ALT I
Brings up the inventory window.

Top half of the inventory window is the equipments that u are wearing.
Bottom half is the items in your inventory bag.
The I, II, III, and IV are 4 different pages of your inventory. Only Page I is available for Non Reborned Chars. Page II is available for 1st Reborned Chars, while Page III and IV will be available for 2nd and 3rd Reborned Chars respectively.
Hovering the mouse over an item will show the item description.
To equip an equipment, check the requirements first, then click it to choose it, and select USE. Else just right click the equipment.
To unequip an equipment, right click it from the top half of inventory window.
DROP will drop an item totally from your inventory bag.
VENDOR is to open vendor stalls to sell your stuff to other players.
     For more details -&gt; Sparky's Guide [url]http://forum.igg.com/viewtopic.php?t=436&sid=4b465d85f5c4122cf11e23b03e4f6[/url]
The Gold Value is amount of Gold u are carrying, while the Deposit Value is the amount of Gold u deposit in bank. Gold is the only in-game curreny.
Amount of Gold u carry will be deducted by 5% or so when u die after level 15, while gold deposited will not be affected.
To switch the location of an item, click to select it, then right click a new spot. If the new spot contains another item, the 2 item locations will swap. If the new spot contains a similar stackable item, the 2 items will stack together into the new spot.
EG, Old spot 2   , New spot 5 =&gt; New spot 7 .

Party Icon ALT-T
When u are alone...
Click on this to bring up a flag icon.
Placing the flag on yourself will make you a leader of a new party.
Placing the flag on an existing party will attempt to join that party, whether the leader of that party approves or not is another thing.

When in a party...
Clicking it once will bring up the top party window in the screenshot which displays the party members in your party, including the name, race and level.

If u are the leader, u can kick a member out of the party by selecting his name in this window, then press KICK.
To leave the party, just press LEAVE. If u are the party leader and u leave, the whole party is disbanded.
Clicking DISPLAY will bring up the bottom DISPLAY window shown, which shows who is attempting to join the party.
CONFIRM means u accept
DELETE means u reject and delete his name from the list.
CLEAR means delete all.
When someone attempts to join, your party icon will blink as the leader. Clicking it when blinking will bring up the DISPLAY window directly.

Attack Icon ALT-A
Clicking it brings up a sword icon.
Place the sword icon on another guy and u will duel/pk with him.
Unless...
For normal duel, both u and your victim needs to have challenge option on in the system options. Else, when u try to duel him, will have error message.
For PK, u need to buy devil's blood from item mall and use it to force a duel against your victim, regardless of whether challenge is on or not. But restricted to battle zones only (outside town) and victim above lvl 50. Also, for party PK, the whole party must drink devil's blood.
Another use of this icon is to spectculate an ingoing battle. When another guy has dual swords above his big fat head, it means he is having a battle. ALT-A on him when u not in a party will allow u to view his battle, though there are exceptions ( guild war and prison escape battles)

Give Icon ALT-G
Allows u to give 1 to 3 different items to a guy or NPC.
This function is used commonly in quests where u need to give NPC X some quest item to complete the quest. Talking to the NPC wont give him the item automatically. U need to use GIVE to give him literally.
For giving to another guy, the receipent must have the trade option ON in the sytem options to receive the gift or in most cases, garbage   , and of course, his inventory not full...
Unable to give Pets using this function, eggs can though.

Trade Icon ALT-X
Similar to Give, except Trade means u give A something, A gives u something or nothing.
Trade does not work with NPCs.
Both parties must have trade option ON in system options.
Trade allows u to trade pets via the trade pet button
      But the pet cannot be in battle/follow mode
      And the receipent cannot be lower than the pet by more than 10
      levels.
Basic Concept:
      Put the items to trade in the 3 slots or type in the gold.
      Tick to allow trader to view what u gonna trade with him.
      After he does the same thing, u can view what he offer u.
      If u happy, click confirm and wait for him to do so too.
      Both confirm, trade done.

For more detailed info on Trade, Sparky's Guide again.
[url]http://forum.igg.com/viewtopic.php?t=436&sid=4b465d85f5c4122cf11e23b03e4f6[/url]

Quest Icon ALT-Q
Brings up the quest log, which shows the quests u are doing, and for some quests, if u forget what the NPC says, u can check for info in the quest log, such as location for seal quest.

Guild Icon ALT-B
Brings up the guild window IF u have a guild of cos.

In the guild window are info of the guild
Name: Obvious
Founder: The creator of the guild that spents 500k gold to make it. His title will be ObviousGuildmaster til he transfers the leadership to another guy. This wont be changed.
Leader: The current leader. If the founder never transfers leadership, this will be the same guy as the founder. Else, it will reflect the current leader of the guild, and his title will be ObviousLeader and not ObviousGuildmaster.
Gold: Guild Funds,  max 2 billion, donated by guild members with real in game gold or a guild function that converts service rate to guild funds.
Exp: Guild Exp, earned from guild quest. Once it reached a certain level, the guild will level up, increasing guild member capacity and more functions not implemented yet.
Members: Current number of members
Level: Guild level_____Required Exp______Max Members
__________1_____________0________________50
__________2___________20000_____________100
__________3___________40000_____________150
__________4___________80000_____________200
__________5__________160000_____________250
Reputation: Dunno
War Exp: Dunno
Message : The message your guild leader posted as welcome message etc
Manage Option: Pressing it will bring u to the next guild window which has all your guild members and other info, together with 5 more options, Accept, Remove, Reject, Demote, Quit Guild
Accept: Leader usable only. To accept someone attempting to join the guild.
Reject: Leader usable only. Opposite of Accept.
Remove: Leader usable only. Kick someone out.
Demote: Nothing
Quit Guild: Leader NON -usable only. All others can quit except the leader. Quiting the guild will delete all info of your service rate and gold contributions and even if u rejoin, those will reset to 0.

If u are the leader, and someone attempts to join the guild while u are ONLINE, the Guild Icon will blink.

System Icon ALT-S
Brings up the system options window.
Some useful ones:
Music and Sound Setting: Er, I bet u understand.
Challenge/PK: Disable this if u dont wish to be dueled by strangers.
Trade: Disable this and other people cannot attempt to trade with u, or give u stuff.
Receive Msg: Disable this if u are fed up with some friends and strangers and want to prevent them from sending u mails via the friend mail msg.
Reject Other Msg: Enable this will allow only players in your friend and guest list to send u friend mail msg.
Add Friend: Decides whether others can add u to their friend list.
Speech Bubble: When using local chat, this decides whether a speech bubble will appear on your shining bald head.
Record: Enables to start recording AFTER u relog. Disable to stop recording also AFTER u relog.

For all the settings, u tick to enable, no tick to disable. And u choose Save and Exit to save the settings, else it will go back to what it was before.

Chat Icon ALT-F

Brings up the buddy list, the one on the right named Chat System.
Three tabs there, 1 for Buddy, 1 for Guest and 1 for Block
Buddy is a person u added permanently into your friend list. U can chat with him through Friend Mail Msg system by double clicking on his nick in your buddy list and type whatever garbage u like. If he is offline when u send, he will receive it when he is online, so it is like MSN. Also, if he adds u in his buddy list as well, when he is online or goes offline, an info message will appear in your chat window informing u.
Guest is a temporary buddy that lasts til u log out. U can use Friend Mail Msg system as well. Your Guest list will be cleared when u log out.
Block list is a list of people that u have used the block function to prevent u from seeing any message from them in any chat channel. And if he blocks u as well, system will inform u just like buddy list whenever he comes online/offline which is dumb...

The bottom buttons of the Chat System Window
1st one No idea.

Search 2nd icon from the left, allows u to search for another player anywhere in mythwar.
Search by Nickname is case sensitive, Monkey is a different player from monkey for instance. But even if u type properly, it may still not work.
Search by ID works 100%, criteria is u must obviously know the player's ID if u want to find him.
Choose 1 search method and confirm.
If he is offline, u cant find him.
Else, his data will appear in the big box there.
Then u can...
Accquantaince to add to your guest list.
Add Friend to add to your buddy list.
PM to select him as your PM target.

Add Friend 3rd Icon
Clicking it will bring up a add friend icon just like clicking party icon will bring up a flag icon. Placing the add friend icon on a nearby player will allow u to add him to your Buddy List directly, so it is a convenient way of adding nearby players without having to use the search function.

Scroll 4th and 5th Icons
Well when u make many friends or have many retards in your block list, u need to scroll up n down the list. There is a maximum number of friends u can have in your list, which is around 100 or so.

By right clicking a nickname in the Chat System Window (buddy, guest or block), u will bring up the Status Check Window.
In this window u can find detailed info of that guy, including ID, level, nickname, title he wearing, guild he belong to and race.
Friendship point is earned when the 2 of u form a party and fight somewhere, or u give him/her a crystal ball or rose, or cupid quest and events.
Crystal ball is meant for 2 similar gender players, while rose is for 2 similar gender players.
Give to the nurse NPC in church to a maximum of 8k friendship points.
Friendship point determine the relationship between u 2.
Below 999         Acquantaince
1000 up                 Friend
2000 up              Close Friend
3000 up                 Buddy
4000 up                Brother
Currently only usage of friendship points is it decides whose nick is arranged higher among those online in your friend list.
Friendship points are meant for brother system and marriage system, but those 2 are not implemented yet so... whatever

The options there:
Acquantaince shifts the guy to your Guest List
Add Friend shifts the guy to your Buddy List
Block shifts the guy to your Block List
Delete deletes the guy from your whole Friend List
PM selects the guy as your PM target
Update updates the status window to refresh info
Mail History just like chat history, this one keeps record of your conversation between u and him via Friend Mail Msg

Above this bottom right panel of icons, u can see some green msgs. These are system generated msgs that u get when u gain exp, skill exp, skill or character level up, as well as your pet exp and level up.
A max of 10 lines will be there.

Below the left panel of buttons, u can see a Channel button that decides which channel u talking in, and a Chat Bar for u to type what u wanna say. This will be covered in another volume of Bored Manuel  

Finally at the lowest part of the screen, there is a partially complete green bar with roman numericals. This is your Character Exp Bar, which functions similar to the Pet Exp Bar available at the top left corner.
