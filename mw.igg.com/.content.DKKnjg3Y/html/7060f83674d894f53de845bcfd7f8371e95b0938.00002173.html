Coming Together


Missouri and Achernar servers will be combined into a new servers. If the integration is delayed due to certain reasons, we will inform you of the time in another announcement.
Time: 1:00 midnight -- 6:00am EDT on July 24th
  
Original Servers: Missouri and Achernar
New Server: Missouri

Notes:
Please read the following notes in case of any problems.   
The New Missouri server time will be GMT-5.

1.One Server just can keep 5 characters. If players have more than 5 characters in the Missouri and the Achernar servers, they should delete the ones they don't actually need and make sure that the number of characters they have is less than 5. If players have more than 5 characters, the system will temporarily reserve all their characters in the Missouri server.
2. All your equipment, items, and pets will be kept intact after the integration.
3. All your accepted quests will be kept intact.
4. All the Item Mall Points you have and the Item Mall itself won't be affected, so you can still buy items in the Item Mall.
5. Your account and password will be kept intact, however, if the system displays "Password Error" when you log in, visit www.igg.com to change your password.
6. The IDs of Players who are in the Achernar server will be changed, and their ID numbers will be added to the end of the ID numbers of the players in Missouri server in sequence.
7. After the integration, the friends in the friend list will be deleted, and the character names will be changed as well, so you'd better keep a list of your friends' contact methods in order to add them again.
8. If there are character names that are the same after the integration, we will add a number to the end of the character names. (For instance, .1--.9999999) players can also change their name in certain page. (the corresponding pages will be operational after the integration)
9. Guild data: All guilds will automatically disband after the integration. If the level 1+ guilds have more than 10000 contribution, their Leads need to contact our GMs from July 16th to July 24th to ask the GMs to check relative guild information. After the integration, Guild Leads can create new guilds. Our officials will help the Leads keep previous guild contributions and compensate the guild fund. However, guilds which are level 1 and have less than 10000 guild contribution are unable to obtain this compensation. We apologize for the inconvenience.
After the end of the integration on July 25th, all players will join a brand new family. There are more friends in the broader game world.
Meanwhile, we will hold a series of events after the integration. Let's make a concerted effort to perfect this "First Turn-based ATB Game in the World".
Thanks for your continued support. We hope you will have fun in Myth War.

Myth War Online Team, IGG
