A Moment to Remember—Screenshot Contest

Are you detail-oriented? Have you had any hard to forget moments? You can take screenshots to record these moments and take part in our screenshot contest by posting your works in our forum to win silver ingots. It's time to show your real self.  

Event duration:
At the  period  of  Myth war II Closed Beta
Players taking part in the Closed Beta are qualified to participate in this screenshot contest.

Rules:
1. Participants can repeat posting screenshots to reply. Trolling is forbidden. Or your qualifications will be cancelled.
2. Participants may make a brief description within 200 words for your screenshots.
3. Screenshots must conform to the contest requirements and topic. Authors whose works are most original and closely related to our game will get the game-related rewards.
4. We will release the winners' forum usernames after the end of event. Winners must send a private message to the appointed board manager to leave your account and contact information. We are not responsible for any losses resulting from incorrect contact information.
5. We reserve the right to final interpretation

Rewards
 Top 5 players will be given 500 silver ingots for each
Players ranked from No.6 to No.10 will be given 300 silver ingots
