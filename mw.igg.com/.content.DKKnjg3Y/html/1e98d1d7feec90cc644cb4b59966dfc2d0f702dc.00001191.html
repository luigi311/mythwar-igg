Re: i hate this topic

[quote:5e33ac2586="Chobbits"]honestly i think this wasnt that great of a help you spent more time talking about the humans then any other race......i mean i would like to know more about the mage not just the fact of they cast fireworks and are faggots [/quote]

Mage

Str
For AODs and nothing else

Agi
Extinct superb weapons require agi to use. Example will be 93 speed wpn
Else raising agi is solely for pk/war purpose, where u wish to move before your opponent

Sta/Int built
Full int gives you superb damage with miserable life. Of cos, it is the centaur role to keep faggot mage alive to cast his fireworks. Full Int is very prone to free tele though. There are mobs with auto repel, such as lizards in defender quest, fog spirits in bone desert. How nice it will be when u cast your flash IV, all the mobs die, and u die of repel. Or, when a poisoned drowcrusher managed to kill you and die of poison. Too bad, free tele. So some pple go for 1 sta/3 int built. Of cos, this gives you slightly more hp, but your damage can never be compared to full int built.

The more HP you have, the less damage you deal. Opportunity cost theory. Every point you put in sta means 1 point less in int and vice versa.

What more u wanna know ?
It's summed up well how stats would affect a mage.

str for wearing equips requirement.
agi if u wanna be faster mage or to use the extinct speed wands
int the more u put the more mana and more damage u can have
sta the more u put the more hp u get

int~sta.  are interconnected, u want more hp or more mp/damage. more u put into 1 the less the other gets. it's up to u to find out how much u want on which end.

if u have a specific question about mages, just ask it.
