FAQ

Since I've seen quite a few questions being asked alot, I decided to make a Frequently Asked Questions.

Q) I'm trying to do the first quest and I try to give my egg to the Breeder it tells me that I have another quest in progress is this a bug?

A) Yes, it is and hopefully it will get fixed but until then there is nothing we can do. (I've heard maybe going to Redeem Steward can fix this.)

Q) I've set up my Vender shop and sold the items I wanted to sell but it won't let me close my vender what can I do?

A) You will have to relog, there is nothing you can do about it, it is a bug that IGG is aware of.

Q) I can't talk in the world chat is something wrong?

A) Possibly, you are either not typing in the right chat, check the bottom left corner to make sure that "World" is selected. If it still does not work, maybe you are over level 50 and each message sent in world chat costs you 50 Mana be sure not to run out.

Q) I'm having troubles starting the game and getting it going what can I do?

A) You can check out this topic here http://www.mythwaronline.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=161 hopefully that will solve the problem you are having.

Q) What skill expereience do I need to level my skills up?

A) Here is the experience list you were looking for:
lv2 - 60
lv3 - 130
lv4 - 312
lv5 - 690
lv6 - 1372
lv7 - 2490
lv8 - 4200
lv9 - 6682
lv10 - 10140
lv11 - 14802 (Only if you are reborn.)

Q) How does everyone keep getting high rolls in crabs?

A) They know the trick, which is if your first roll is bad, click on exit and re-roll same goes for first and second if they are bad exit and re-roll.

Q) Where can I exchange the Redeem points I have gotten from Theif quest and crab catching?

A) You can find the Redeem Steward in the Teleport Arena where you can exchange the points you have for Expereience, Gold, or items! Here is a list of what you can get:

1 Redeem point = 200 gold, or 50 experience each

30 points for Small Iron Ore
50 points for Small Copper Ore
100 points for Gold Ore

Q) Where do I find pet eggs people keep talking about?

A) Simple, head to either Demon Square, or Desert City, find the pet shop and go in. Find the Spirits and defeat them for a chance at an egg or crystal.

Q) I can't connect to the game and I can't get the update can anyone help?

A) Yes, try either disabling your firewall(s) or adding mythwar to your allow list. If it is still not working some firewalls have a popup when something is trying to connect to the internet, just simply click Unblock/confirm which ever it may be.

Q) My characters are gone! What can I do?

A) Check all 3 servers incase you made it on a a different server, if that isn't it send an email to mythwargm@igg.com or mythwargm@igg.com and explain what happened give your account name and ID number.

Q) My pets Growth Rate has gone down why?

A) If you have used a Stone of Dream or Stone of Moon then it's because thats the effect the item does to your pet besides reset stats/level.


If you think anything else should be added please feel free to let me know and I will add it A.S.A.P. Thanks,
