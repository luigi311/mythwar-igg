Auction House



The AOD Auction House held last time has come to a successful end. Players had a lot of fun the last time so we have decided to bring it back again. This time, participants can bid not only for Armor of Dreams but also for Deity Beasts. Different classy items will be put to auction everyday. Just watch for them and place your bid.

 Auction Rules:

 1. Fortune Bombing Integrals are the only official currency used during auctions. The 'Reserve' price is 300 Fortune Bombing Integrals. Each bid increase must be at least 100 integrals. Participants can reply to this post during Event time.
2. The highest bidder will be the owner of the Item. But if the highest bidder does not have enough Fortune Bombing Integrals, the second highest bidder will then get it.
3. Bidders are not allowed to place bids with more Fortune Bombing Integrals than they have, or their bids will be invalid. Bid posts can't be edited, or bids will also be invalid.
4. We will delete all posts not related to the auction. Participant bidders must observe our forum's rules.
5. Fortune Bombing Integrals will be taken away from successful bidders' accounts after the auction is over. We will then send out nicknames for the new owners of the Item
. You can claim your items from GMs through Live Support.
6. All rights reserved by IGG

Auction Time: October 15th to October 19th. Every item will be at auction for 24 hours
Auction Item: AOD+5 Armor of Dreams*5, Deity Beast*4 and Howling Pet*1

Auction House is the eBay in Myth War I Online. With every click, you win a chance to get some fantastic goodies.
