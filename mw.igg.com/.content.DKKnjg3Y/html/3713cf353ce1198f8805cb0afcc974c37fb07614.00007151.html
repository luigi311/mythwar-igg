  White Day Cards
White Day originated from Rome in the third century. It was said that the Emperor of Rome saved a couple who disobeyed the love and marriage ban and were sentenced to death on February 14th. In order to commemorate this day, the Emperor of Rome called it Valentine’s Day. After being released for one month, this couple swore that they would love each other forever. And then, March 14th became known as White Day.
Well, we here at IGG are all about love and celebrations! We just love a good holiday because it means more activities and events! So enjoy White Day with your significant other in Myth War.

Location:MW Media Overview
Content: You received gifts from that special someone on Valentine’s Day. Now, White Day is almost here. Can you make a love card by yourself and send it back to them?

Rules:
1. All cards should be made with the theme of “love” in mind. The background pictures of these cards should be screenshots of Myth War.
2. We don’t appreciate cards bearing rude or impolite messages and we don’t think anyone else would either. Remember these kinds of messages are NOT allowed.
3. All cards should be JPG or GIF in the horizontal line or vertical line.
4. All cards should be titled “White Day”.
5. All cards should be original. If there are 2 of the same cards, the one sent first will count.

Card Upload Time: From March 5th to March 25th

Selection Time: From March 25th to March 28th

Rewards:
1st (1) Place: Any Deity Pet
2nd (1) Place: Any Chinchilla or Any Aod+3
3rd (2) Place: Any Christmas Pet or Any Aod+2
