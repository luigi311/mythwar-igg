3# A A A Post at 2008-4-7 14:20  Show author
there are 2 ways to reset stats.

1. bloodstone. one use, then your stat is as if you have leveled to that level without assigning any point.

2. reset gem (agi gem, sta gem, etc.) take off 5 points corresponding to the gem type. those 5 points go to free point pool. however, you can't have points in any stat lower than your level. so that's the limit.

note: you also get extra stat points for getting skills II, III and IV. or maybe you get those when you reach lvl 15 and 50. not exactly sure which is. but usually total stat points will be slightly higher than level x 8. these counts as free points and you don't lose them when reseting stats.
