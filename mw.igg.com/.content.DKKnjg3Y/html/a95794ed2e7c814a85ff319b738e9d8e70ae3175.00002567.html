Soulstone Quest

I have edited and formatted the original guides for easier and less confusing reading.
If you find any wrong information or mistakes, please tell me. Thanks.

Soulstone of Light (written by Mew)
Level Requirement: 5
Activate from: Elder Zone (Woodlingor) - Elder of Night
Quest NPC: Barkeeper, Drunkyard Fenny, Gambler John, Deserter
Quest Item:
Quest Reward: Soulstone of Light, Reputation, Gold, Exp
Title Received: Wanderer

01: Talk to Elder of Dawn
02: Talk to Elder of Sight
03: Get quest from Elder of Night
04: Find information from Barkeeper and pay him 150 gold
05: Fenny wants Wintermelon wine for information
06: Buy Wintermelon wine from Barkeeper for 1000 gold
07: Give wine to Fenny using Give function
08: Gamble with John for information - if u lose u have to get mushroom spore to retry
09: John has lost the Soulstone to Deserter
10: Find Deserter at Tree of Life 2 and prepare for boss battle
11: Win Deserter and get the Soulstone
12: Give the Soulstone to Elder of Night to claim your reward
13: Keep Soulstone in your bank for now

Soulstone of Dark (written by Mew)
Level Requirement: 20 + Completed Soulstone of Light
Activate from: Demon Square - Captain
Quest NPC: Wily Carlton, Cool Kewl, Braggard Noel, Micheal, Soldier Joe, Legoma, Vourk
Quest Item:
Quest Reward: Soulstone of Dark, Reputation, Gold, Exp
Title Received: Adventurer

01: Talk to Captain at City Hall (Demon Square), choose all 1st choice
02: Talk to Wily Carlton, choose all 1st choice
03: Talk to Soldier Joe, near Prison, choose 1st choice to avoid fight
04: Go back to Wily and Give the sword to him
05: Go talk to Cool Kewl, near Healer, select all 4 options one by one
06: Go talk to Copper Hans, around Craft Shop, select all 4 options one by one
07: Go talk to Micheal, near Armory, select all 4 options one by one
08: Go talk to Braggard Noel, near Bank, select all 3 options one by one
09: Go back and talk to the Captain
10: Go talk to Legoma, western Demon Square, choose 1st options
11: Go to Devil's Gate to fight Vourk
12: Go back to Legoma for the final boss fight, 2 rounds
13: Beat Legoma and get Soulstone of Dark
14: Go back and talk to Captain
15: Give the Soulstone to Elder of Night to claim your reward
16: Keep Soulstone in your bank for now

Soulstone of Fire (written by Mew)
Level Requirement: 40 + Completed Soulstone of Dark
Activate from: Desert City - Girl Miya
Quest NPC: Cursed Mage, Tree Elder, Girl Miya, Spirit Mage, Flame Devil
Quest Item: Dragon Scale, Mysterious Necklace, Phantasm, Book of the Ancients
Quest Reward: Soulstone of Fire, Gold, Exp
Title Received: Explorer

01: Girl Miya will give u a mysterious necklace
02: Equip the necklace
03: You'll get cursed and lose 100k experience points
04: Talk to Miya again about the curse and she tells you about Cursed Mage
05: Go to Badlands and talk to Cursed Mage
06: Cursed Mage will force you to complete 3 tasks for him
07: 1st task - Find Knight Shingo in Blython and ask him for dragon scale
08: Go back to Badlands and give Cursed Mage the dragon scale
09: 2nd task - Talk to Tree Elder inside woodlingor Elder Zone
10: Tree Elder will request you to find a Phantasm from Earth Wolves for him
11: Give Phantasm to Tree Elder in exchange for Book of Ancient
12: Go back to Badlands and give Cursed Mage the book
13: 3rd task - Find out about the Soulstone of Fire at Desert City
14: Go to Desert City and find Spirit Mage
15: Promise to help him gain back 100k exp and you will find out that Flame Devil has the Soulstone of Fire
16: Find Flame Devil in Desert City, Demon Lair 3
17: Beat Flame Devil to get the Soulstone of Fire
18: Bring the Soulstone of Fire back to the Cursed Mage and get ready for final boss battle
19. Give the Soulstone to Elder of Night to claim your reward
20: Keep Soulstone in your bank for now

Soulstone of Ice (written by Sparkblaze)
Level Requirement: 60 + Completed Soulstone of Fire
Activate from: Blython bar - Duke
Quest NPC: Lion Dragon, Hades, Duke Clarke, Kelina, Veteran Doctor
Quest Item:
Quest Reward: Soulstone of Ice, Gold, Exp
Title Received: Venturer

01: Talk to Duke Clarke at Blyton Bar
02: Have a battle with him to prove your worthiness, you will be rewarded 100k exp
03: He will ask you to find his daughter, Kelina and ask you to bring her home
04: Proceed to Flame Ruins and look for Kelina
05: Agree to let Andrew and her be together, you'll be rewarded 20k exp and some gold
06: Go back to Blyton and get a party ready
07: Talk to Duke Clarke again and prepare to battle him
08: The battle isn't overly difficult, however, they are resistant to most skills
09: If you defeat Duke Clarke with a full party you get 300k exp
10: Talk with Duke Clarke and agree to keep Kelina away from Hades
11: Head back to Flame Ruins again and talk to Kelina, she will tell you the secret of Hades' "longetivity."
12: Head over to Wasteland 3 and prepare to battle Lion Dragon
13: Talk to Lion Dragon and choose the non violent method
14: He will be reluctant to help you and ask you to talk to the Veteran Doctor at Blyton
15: The Veteran Doctor will sell you a bottle of Pain Killer for 10k gold
16: Return to the Lion Dragon and Give him the Pain Killer
17: He will moan about you scaring him...some more...
18: Buy the Carving Knife from any Grocer
19: Return to the Lion Dragon and Give him the Carving Knife
20: You will receive 150k gold, 200k experience, and Soulstone of Ice
21: Head over to Ashes Pit 2 with the Soulstone and a good party!
22: Talk to Sorceror Hades
23: Fight Sorceror Hades
24: You will recieve 500k experience
25: Return to the Bar in Blyton and talk to Duke Clarke and each party member will recieve 200k gold
26: Return to Flame Ruins and talk to Kelina
27: She will give you 20k gold
29: Give the Soulstone to Elder of Night to claim your reward
30: Keep Soulstone in your bank for now

Soulstone of Wind (written by SnowyWolf)
Level Requirement: 80 + Completed Soulstone of Ice
Activate from: Blython - Pollati
Quest NPC: Pollati, Lohan, Dark Shadow, Elder Losu, Kang Leader, Hendel, Inspector, Wanderer
Quest Item:
Quest Reward: Soulstone of Wind, Gold, Exp
Title Received: Voyager

01: Head to Blython and talk to Pollati
02: Pollati will ask you to retrieve a World Eye for him
03: Head to Ashes Pit 4 and find Dark Shadow
04: Beat him and head back to Pollati and give him the World Eye
05: He'll point you to the first Beast Chip from Elder Losu at Demon Square
06: Help the nice Elder Losu retrieve his Sword of Sage from the Kang family
07: Look for Kang Leader at Desert City
08: Kang Leader will tell you that he never stole the sword
09: Head back and talk to Elder Losu
10: Now go to Demon Square, Evil Lair 2 to look for Lohan
11: Speak to him and bribe him for the Emblem
12: Go look for Kang Leader again and you will learn about his death
13: The truth will be revealed when you talk to Lohan, prepare for fight (Physical Damage &gt; Magic Damage)
14: Beat him and he'll tell you about the Mech family, and his reason for doing what he did
15: Lohan will give the Sword of Sage to you, return it to Elder Losu
16: You'll receive the first piece of the Beast Chip
17: Talk to Kang Leader for the last time
18: Return to Blython and talk to Pollati to start searching for your second piece of Beast Chip
19: Now head to Desert City - City Hall and find the Inspector
20: Leave City Hall and walk a bit northwest to find the Wanderer
21: Help him get some clothes by going to Woodlingor and buy a Robe of Shadows +1 for him
22: Give Wanderer the Robe and talk to Inspector again
23: He'll give you the second Beast Chip
24: Return to Pollati yet again to start the search for the third and final piece of Beast Chip
25: Now travel to Sunset Plains and talk to Hendel
26: Visit Agony Spirit down at Burial 2, you will find him at the entrance to Burial 3
27: Tell him I MUST FIND father's bone
28: Head down to Burial 3 and find Skeletor, prepare for fight
29: Beat him and you will receive the bone
30: Go back to Sunset Plains and give Hendel the bone
31: You will receive experience, gold, and the last peice of Beast Chip
32: Head back to Blython and talk to Pollati, he will give you the Soulstone Map
33: Gather the strongest party you can find
34: Now go down to Sewers 3 and find the coordinates (38, 38)
35: Use the Map and let the fight begin!!!
36: Win and you will get the Soulstone of Wind
37: Give the Soulstone to Elder of Night to claim your reward

You have finished the Soulstone Quests!
When you want to start the Reborn Quest, bring your 5 Soulstones with you and talk to the Reborn NPC at Woodlingor.
