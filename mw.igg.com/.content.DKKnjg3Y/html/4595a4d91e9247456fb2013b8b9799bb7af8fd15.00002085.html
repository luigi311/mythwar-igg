Dreams and Destiny

Dreams and Destiny  

Kung Fu Panda is a famous animated film about a panda who aspires to be a Dragon Warrior. News of a tournament to select a new Dragon Warrior is sent out from Jade Temple, and the whole city goes to watch, along with Po. The competition is between the Furious Five: Tigress, Viper, Crane, Monkey, and Mantis. The Panda accidentally enters the contest and is then named the Dragon Warrior. He must work hard to learn the secrets of Kung Fu so he can fulfill his destiny and fight the coming evil.  
Villains have attacked the peaceful Myth War Online City!. We cannot stand by and do nothing! Come on! Who will prove themselves as brave as the Kung Fu Panda?

Event Duration:  3:00pm-5:00pm from July 5th to July 6th (Server Time)

Event Description:
 After the GM announces the start of the event, participating players can go to the Church with their Chinchilla in tow. The GM will then organize a pet PVP competition. Characters are not allowed to take part in it. While pets are fighting, any healing potions or mana potions can't be used to recover pets' health or mana. There are five matches with each one being composed of 20 knockout rounds. The players whose pets win the 20th round will be the winner in a match. Who will be the Kung Fu Panda in Myth War II. Let's wait and see.

Reward: 50 Fortune Bombing Integrals and 3 Dragon Scrolls
