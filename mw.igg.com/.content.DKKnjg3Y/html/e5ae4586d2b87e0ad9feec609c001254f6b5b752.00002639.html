Overview

      IGG (Sky Union Co., Ltd.) is a rising MMORPG developer and operator in the world. We are aiming at creating a brand-new online entertainment living style for global audiences. Established in June 2005, the high-tech IT company has set up branches in America, Canada, Mainland China and Hong Kong. Currently, IGG's business is primarily focused on operating free MMORPGs which would allow thousands of players to concurrently log on to network servers to "role-play" characters in a virtual world.


      Within just one year, the registered users of IGG have reached 1,500,000 and Skyunion has developed into a professional company that covers the business range of online game development, operation, publication, distribution and service.


      IGG is dedicated to develop itself into an interactive entertainment platform bringing as much fun as possible to all its registered members, to ensure all members can interact with thousands of others and enjoy the interactive entertainment content that IGG.com provides.


Our History
June 2005 Our corporation, Sky Unioin Co., Ltd. was founded.
May 2006 Sky Union started to launch and operate MMORPG---Myth War Online.
Oct 2006 Sky Union owned JoyConnect Studio was established, commencing to develop its first 3D MMORPG---Gods' War Online.
Dec 2006 Sky Union continued to operate the second MMORPG---Voyage Century Online
Mar 2007 The third MMORPG under Sky Union’s operation---Tales of Pirates began its open beta.
