47# A A A Post at 2008-1-15 17:07  Show author
Not all companies will. It depends on their infrastructure and their customer service - not tech support - to some extents...

A small company is likely to care about the opinions, because it needs those clients as it were to keep it going...

A large company will need to care to an extent because it needs to keep its brand power  and it helps them ultimately expand that brand power to bring them more money.

A medium company would sit somewhere in between, it's likely to not have a brand which is well known, or the company itself isn't well known, but at that same time, it doesn't fully rely on but a collection of individuals.

When you don't work for a company, it's very hard to comment on what they actually do - even more so when it's IT based.

It's very easy for people to say I do nothing, some days, I have done nothing and thus read stuff for most of the day. The thing that generally really gets IT people is their work nearly always goes unrecognised - maybe not at christmas, but then it's cheap since you know it's just for that "spirit".

I'm not going to definitively say that they try and do something about them, but I won't hold myself to saying they do nothing about them.

Feature Requests for other games I've played are often just ignored, not replied to, never implemented etc. There are plenty of levels of what happens when you make a suggestion... it's not as simple as: Good suggestion let's do it...

it's usually something like

Suggest &gt; Like/Dislike? &gt; Like &gt; suggest to dev team &gt; they like &gt; they do

Developer team don't have to like the idea or maybe they already have plans for it? perhaps it comes at a later date? perhaps it doesn't fit into their style and target audience.

How do you make a suggestion for a feature to a company who wants to run a revised version of what they made previously, but you want it in the older version?

can you even categorically say that IGG didn't suggest any of these ideas to Developers who may have liked the ideas and plan on taking it into MW2 one day?


I'm not asking you to believe that they might be doing something, that would be wrong and megalomaniacal of me to do so, I just get tired of the whole slam van constantly repeating the same drivel in every stupid topic that exists.

Yes there are good suggestions out there, there are also "questionable" suggestions and in between both of those are the ones which are already "pipelined" in for one day...
