  Is Myth War still fun for you?
The fact is that MW kinda stopped being fun after a certain point. In essence this is because the game is just easy. The only hard part about the game is getting the knowledge and the required party to complete a task, Knowledge is pretty much abundant, parties not so much since people are quitting more and more or playing less and less.

IMO the only real fun thing in the game is GW, that's where you can prove yourself and it can be harder than anything the game has to offer. But that's once a week and it's still a lottery because most people get whitewashed by the older guilds and the fun is reserved for a select few.

As far as quests are concerned since everything in the game has been beaten need I say any more? People have started soloing them out of boredom and lack of challenges that the game has to offer.

If it's easy, it's dull and if it's dull, it's no fun.

P.S. I'm only referring about the game itself and not the people playing, as they sometimes can more than make-up for the game limitations, but since they can always do that with other games and have more fun with them it's not really relevant.
