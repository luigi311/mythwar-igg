Notice: Character Migration For Myth War Online

Knowing that Myth War Online has one of the closest and most passionate communities of players, we've had an extremely hard time making the decision to stop publishing it. But we feel that with the success of Myth War II, the time has come. We also know that convincing players of the classic version would be difficult, so we are willing to do something to make it up to them. On January 8, the game's development team will launch a new server in Myth War II Online. Classic Myth War fans will have their characters data moved to this new server.

The character migration sheet must be filled out completely. Players can apply for up to 5 characters in each of the Myth War Online accounts migrating to the new server. Players can also create new characters in Myth War II Online with the account they used in the classic Myth War. We will transfer character data involved in the submitted character migration sheet to the new server and give compensation based on the submitted sheet. Make sure you fill the sheet with complete and accurate details. We are not responsible for losses because of incorrect or unknown information offered to us.(We will post another notice to catch players up with detailed migration sheet info)

Special Notice: we will collect data about your character, gears and pets after maintenance performed on January 8. All transfers will be based on these instantaneous data. So all items you want to move to the new server must be kept in the inventory of characters that will be transferred to the new server as well. We will not help you move your virtual properties acquired after maintenance to the new server!

Character Migration:
1.  Characters Level 10 to Level 30 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 15 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 30,000 skill points, 3,000 game gold and 100 silver ingots. Common pets will not be transferred.
2.  Characters Level 31 to Level 50 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 35 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 50,000 skill points, 30,000 game gold and 200 silver ingots. Common pets will not be transferred.
3.  Characters Level 51 to Level 60 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 53 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 100,000 skill points, 100,000 game gold and 300 silver ingots. Common pets will not be transferred.
4.  Characters Level 61 to Level 70 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 60 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 200,000 skill points, 500,000 game gold and 300 silver ingots. Common pets will not be transferred.
5.  Characters Level 71 to Level 80 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 70 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 300,000 skill points, 5,000,000 game gold and 300 silver ingots. Common pets will not be transferred.
6.  Characters Level 81 to Level 90 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 82 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 500,000 skill points, 8,000,000 game gold and 400 silver ingots with one common pet being transferred to the new server.
7.  Characters Level 91 to Level 95 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 91 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 800,000 skill points, 10,000,000 game gold and 400 silver ingots with one common pet being transferred to the new server.
8.  Characters Level 96 to Level 104 in the classic Myth War Online will receive enough experience to move up to Level 97 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 1,000,000 skill points, 50,000,000 game gold and 500 silver ingots with two common pet being transferred to the new server.
9.  Reborn characters Level 1 to Level 119 will receive enough experience to move up to Level 104 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 3,000,000 skill points, 80,000,000 game gold and 800 silver ingots with three common pet being transferred to the new server.
10.  Reborn 1 Characters at Level 120+ will receive enough experience to move up to Level 104 in Myth War II Online. They will also receive 6,000,000 skill points, 100,500,000 game gold and 1,000 silver ingots with three common pet being transferred to the new server.
11.  Common pets refer to all animal pets except for Saint Pet and Deity Beast. Each of common pets will receive 10 of the Stone of Moon as compensation to have their growth rate reset after transferred
12.  Classic Myth War Online players with characters such as those above will to create new character(s) in Myth War II Online, using the provided skill points and such to alter their new characters'level, attributes, items and in-game currency. These newly created characters and characters migrating to the new server are independent object choices and are not allowed to be equalized.

Virtual Property Migration:
1. All your AOD gear and gear Level 11+ in the classic Myth War Online will be transferred to players new accounts in the new server with additional attributes removed.
2. Non-reborn pets will get powerleveled in the same way as non-reborn characters progressed after transferred. They will receive Mystical Fruits as compensation. Reborn Pets will be transferred to the new server while hitting level 100. Besides, non reborn pets  level 80 to 100 will receive 10000 intimacy and pets with their first reborn complicated will be given 50000 intimacy.

3. Some pets in old Myth War Online that will not be featured in Myth War II Online, we will replace these pets with newly developed pets in Myth War II Online.

4. All Deity Beasts and Saint Pets will migrate to the new server with the potential loss of resistance and growth rate.
5. Unused Fortune Bombing Integrals will be converted into IGGems at a 1:1 ratio.
6. All unused mw points and mall items in the classic Myth War Online will be transferred to your new accounts in the new server.
7. VIP players will receive new pets we've specially designed for you.
8. There is compensation on the way for transferred characters losing their gear units' and pets' additional attributes during migrating process-they will win gems with high resistance to recover their additional resistance by being a part of events we will hold in the new server.
9. Guild leaders must create new guilds in the new server with their leader accounts in the classic Myth War Online. Guild migration applications will be conducted in the official forum. Please pay close attention.
