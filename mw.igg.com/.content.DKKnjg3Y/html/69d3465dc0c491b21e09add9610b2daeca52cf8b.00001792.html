List of bugs I found so far

Hi!

Since this is a beta, I figure I should list all the bugs I got in the 3 hours I played so far.

  • When looking at a details in the quest list, the text in the requirement section is often too long to see it entirely.
  • The help messages that appears when you open a window for the first time say I should click on "confirm" to apply the changes, but the button is called "Change status" instead.  I think it is the button's name that is wrong.
  • The help window always appears behind opened windows.
  • The quest from the battle instructor: "Help Battle Instructor to collect 5 Pine at Sky Passage".  It should say Sky Garden, not Sky Passage.
  • I get a "Decode Macro Error! (length)" when talking to the lansquenet official.  It seems to me that the name of a macro was translated and it is obvious that anything in english will contain more characters than the original chinese.
  • Quest details and item descriptions often contain weird characters, like "Duration£º200/200"
  • In revive arena, you occasionally battle no enemies.
  • The quest from the brave angel says to defeat 3 times someone in "Life Tree", but it is in "Revive Arena" instead.

That is all for now.
Bye!
