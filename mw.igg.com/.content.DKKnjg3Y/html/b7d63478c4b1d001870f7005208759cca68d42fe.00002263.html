9# A A A Post at 2008-9-26 01:05 PM  Show author
You answered your own question, just have to look at what you typed and read it for understanding

If the holy armor you are wearing says + 7 chaos, then it is adding 7 chaos resist to you.  So say your level 100 human you will have a natural 10 chaos resist.  put that armor on and you will have 17 chaos resist. (plus means adding)

If the holy armor says neglect 7 resist that in turn means you are taking resistance away from the opponent.  Say its a slower agility human that is also level 100.  That means they have the same 10 initial chaos resist.  Due to your neglect armor they will only have 3 chaos resist (neglect is a smarter word for forget)  

When you wear "chance skill" resists, as the person wearing the resists, you are in effect lowering the chance that that particular chance skill hits.  There is no "exact" formula to figure what resist is enough.  Proven only by the fact that you will see characters with 110+ chaos resist get hit by chaos, while characters with 42 chaos resist are missed.  Algorithms are a b***h to figure out .
