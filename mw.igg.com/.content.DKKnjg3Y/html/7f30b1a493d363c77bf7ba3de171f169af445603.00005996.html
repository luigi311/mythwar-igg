  IMHO...
Item mall shouldn't sell anything of practical value that doesn't drop in game. This makes the game seriously imbalanced and therefore unattractive but to the select few that can buy their way to the top. But guess what, with very few players, you have a very poor community despite 'rich' players and since a MMO can't last very long without a community... Well, the truth is becoming more and more obvious.

Suggestion number 1.

Make alternate ways for players to acquire intimacy for pets in the form of normal quests. And by that I mean either a specific quests for gaining intimacy for your pet or an alteration of the existing quests to reward above required level pets with the proportional amount of exp for the quest. Just like players get skill exp for being above the quests maximum level.

Suggestion number 2.

Modify the quest reward so that is dependent on the number of people in the party. You do solo, you do more work and you get a lot more for your efforts. Actually this exists already for Hellion quest, but only in the form of exp. If you solo, you get +50% more exp that with a party. You just need to extend this to the other quests, so that players will have an incentive to try a new approach to old quests.

Suggestion number 3.

Make item mall stuff drop in quests. Like Dragon Eyes and Dragon Bones dropping in Drow and Worn. This will also give players more incentive to do those quests more often.

Suggestion number 4.

Put Devil's blood and Stone of dream back in groceries. PKing shouldn't be reserved to mall users. Maybe that way you could reduce the staggering inflation and give players access to the player vs player part of MMO that make it fun to play. Stone of dreams shouldn't be in item mall in the first place. Its use is very limited and I think everyone that bought these with cash has been more than frustrated at the random results.

Suggestion number 5.

Modify the pet resists NPCs. And by that I mean get rid of pierce, hit rate/dodge and frailty. No one with a right mind has any use for these, with the exception of hit rate/dodge, but it doesn't work. All it does is suck your gold away when you try to get something that's actually useful for your pet.
