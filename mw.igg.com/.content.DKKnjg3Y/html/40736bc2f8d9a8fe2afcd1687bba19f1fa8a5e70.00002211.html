Wedding Party on Chinese Valentine


Are you still in awe of the grand wedding ceremony that we held during the closed beta period? All of you were so supportive and it was greatly appreciated.  And as it was such a wonderful time we have decided to hold a magnificent wedding party on Chinese Valentine's Day this year. It is our dream to create a really romantic virtual wedding ceremony which our players can use as a testimony to their loyal and beautiful love.

Event Details:

Players must sign up on our forum for their wedding parties. We will keep track of all participating players. Participants must take the test given by GM before becoming qualified to hold their own wedding parties. The 3 winning teams will have their own wedding parties with special wedding Rings and Home Upgraders gifted by the GM. The newlyweds' friends and relatives will also be invited to bless and cheer for them.

Register time:  From August 4th to 1:00am EDT (GMT-4),August 7th
Register link: http://forum.mw.igg.com/viewthread.php?tid=15967&page=1&extra=page%3D1

Event duration:
 Start at 10:00am EDT (GMT-4) on August 7th

Rules:

1. Participants must be online with their prospective marriage partners during the specific event time. If either one fails to be online, both will be disqualified.
2. The GM will be waiting for the players to take the tests at Luna's Palace in Woodlinger 10:00am EDT (GMT-4) on August 7th. Participants must team up with their marriage partners to take the tests.
3. The GM will let off firecrackers to announce the start, and the Players must then head for Blython's Cathedral. The three teams who are the first to reach the named destination will be announced the final winners. During the test, players are not allowed to use Devil's Tears and/or Teleport Scrolls. We will appoint one GM to join each participating team to confirm that they haven't violated this rule (the GM can't help the players during the test).
The test match route:
Luna's Palace in Woodlinger>>Revive Arena>>Snow Ridge>>Sunset Plain>>Cathedral in Blython

4. After the test is completed, the three winning teams may go to Blython's Cathedral to start their own wedding parties hosted by a GM.
5. All expenditures will be paid for by us. This includes the 10,000,000 gold rewarded for the weedding to the No.1 team, the 8,000,000 gold rewarded to the No.2 team, and the 5,000,000 gold rewarded to the No.3 team. The newlyweds will also be gifted with a pair of wedding rings and one Home Upgrader per pair.
6. Both the bride and the groom may invite their friends or relatives to attend their wedding parties to bless and cheer them on.
7. After the wedding party has come to an end, GM will make a server announcement to let everybody know the news of the marriages and share the happiness with newlyweds. Also any players attending the wedding party will be given the surprise gift of Romantic Sparks.
8. The three newlyweds must obey orders of the GM and them to keep order. Players attending the wedding party must also obey the GM's orders and conduct themselves in a dignified fashion.
Ahh!  It will be so beautiful!  Our eyes are becoming moist just at the thought of this wonderful event.  Just remember to get to the church on time!
Come and sign up for your own wedding party and end your long lonely search for true love.
