There r Talent quests u can start them by talking with Natural Talent NPC in Wood near teleport, he can give quests for lvl 1-30 and 30-60 and for lvl 90 by going in Blython City hall or Church (not sure) and talking with Natural Talent NPC there, is better to go in  groups of diff races cuz u can gain more bonuses, also there r item mall items that can double ur tp for a certain period of time, for example with a 2x tp scroll and doing ur coresponding lvl of quest u can get 8-9k tp at once per battle.

from my experience in the game is this, true u lvl hard cuz of low hours of 2x exp per week, but with mokka helping chars till 50 and giving 1.25k tp per swoord isn't a bad quest just is boring cuz of not beeing able to boot, but in couples of days by playing few hours a day u still can make a skill II decent and can start tp quest.
