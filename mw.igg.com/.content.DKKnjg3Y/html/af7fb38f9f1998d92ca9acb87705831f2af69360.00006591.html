 ihmo,

1. active members => raw power

2. good parties => turns raw power into effective power

at team level, a really really good team can equate 3 really really bad team, but not more. so choice 2 can be true, but it's unlikely. many guilds make effort to organize good parties from members they have. this requires organization (or smart members). but at the end, if you have three times fewer active members, it's not gonna happen.

3. strategies.... only matters in close wars

think what matters the most is still in-battle strategy, which makes a good team equates many bad teams. for other things like -- which team fights who, which team gets stone, run where, etc -- i don't think it matters much. if you have less effective power, you won't be able to pull off many of these strategies anyway. it probably matters in a close war, though.
