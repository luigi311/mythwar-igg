I just completed the soulstone of fire quest and I have a couple of thing to mention...

It says level 40+ recommended but I suggest you wait until you're level 50 at least (I'm lvl 53 and barely won).

When you form a party, I suggest you bring a high level human, 2 drain borgs and a Centaur. Drain works awesome against all bosses, especially the Cursed Mage and his Shadows. Poison works just as well, but if you can't then Choas the crap outta them.

Make sure you have good strong pets to keep you and your party members alive and their MP full... you're gonna need it!

Other than that HAVE FUN!
