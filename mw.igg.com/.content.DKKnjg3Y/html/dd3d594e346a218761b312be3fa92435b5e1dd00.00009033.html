New Round Sentinels

 

Dear players,

After waiting for a marathon selection, we have finally selected the new round sentinels. We sincerely hope these sentinels perform well in the future and become excellent sentinels. Our GMs will pay attention to their performance, and if sentinels don't perform well, we will cancel their qualifications. At the same time, players who lost the election don't need to feel upset, because they can still join the next round selection. We will give sentinels the Ghastly Dragon that only sentinels may possess after Dec 13th, and we have to mention that we will confiscate the Ghastly Dragon without compensation no matter whether you have trained the pet or not as soon as the sentinels' term is over. The Ghastly Dragon can't be traded, and if sentinels are caught trying to trade the pet, both parties' accounts will be frozen. We will confiscate all sentinel pets of last round in next server maintenance in Thursday, and sentinels who win a second term will obtain 200 Fortune Bombing credits as a reward.
