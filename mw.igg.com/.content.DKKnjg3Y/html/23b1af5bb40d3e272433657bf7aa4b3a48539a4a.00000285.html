How come a promotion topic has been hacked about a debate once again about the future of MW1?
Anyway,you have no doubt seen the "Veterens recruited" event. (I love how they call marketing strategies events btw)
This is a clear effort to attract players to MW2 that are playing MW1. There is no argument on this point, they admit it themselves. So why? What do you think the reason is?
So that players will play both? Spend twice as much? No, I don't think so. In practice very little players play both games. Those who come MW2 usually end up doing 1 of 2 things. They don't like it and return to MW1, or they enjoy it and gradually migrate thier time, effort and money to MW2.
However, its the former, rather than the latter that IGG need to target. Those pro's from MW1 that are for some reason struggling to adapt or enjoy the MW2 experience for what ever reason.
So we now have an event that helps gives them a leg-up into the game.
Theorising on the long term outlook, lets suppose in say, 12 months another 30_40% of MW1 players are now putting thier time and effort into MW2. If this 30_40 are a greater % of the mall players in MW1, then what do you think is the future?
In my humble opinion, this is just another marketing strategy to migrate the customer base from MW1 to MW2, with a view to one day deleting the MW1 product. I'm not saying this is a good or bad thing. All I'm saying is don't be deluded over the motives of IGG towrds MW1. They have a buisness to run and will run it how they see fit. But for me personally, as a mall spender and a promoter, I won't be throwing money and promoting a lost cause in the future.

oh yeah btw, back on topic. Be grateful for any % bonus from the mall. It attracts mall spenders to purchase more items which are then generated into the game for all. 20% is better than nothing.
