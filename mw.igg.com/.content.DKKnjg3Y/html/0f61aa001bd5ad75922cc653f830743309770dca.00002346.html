Myth War Online Official Blog Opened

What's so special about today? It's September 28th, the day when our official blog will be formally opened. With it comes a brand new way to interact and stay in touch with your fellow Myth War buddies.

So get ready for the future of communications.  And don't just listen to others express your opinion too!
