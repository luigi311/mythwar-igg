  Sentinels Recruited
After waiting for a long time, we will finally hold a new round of Sentinel Recruiting Activities! Want to be a sentinel?

Event Duration：
Nov 10th--Nov 12th（Application）
Nov 13th---Nov 15th（Selection）
Nov 16th—Nov 18th (Voting) (EST GMT-5)Qualification：
1、You should at least have 1 IGG account with level 30 or above.
2、The ideal candidate will be very familiar with Myth War, has a good command of in game player to player communication , be able to solve thorny issues in game and has no bad record in game such as using bots and using inappropriate swear words in game.
Sentinels’ Rights：
All sentinels can obtain the special pet Ghastly Dragon and will be able to contact GMs directly.

Sentinels’ Obligations：

(1)Help GMs to maintain the harmony of the Myth War World, stop quarrels or arguments in any channel and inform GMs in time.
(2)Help new players to play and answer their questions;
(3)Help the officials to search for players who are using bots, and persuade them to stop as well as alerting GMs in time
(4)Provide officials with useful information, such as game and website bugs, players’ interests, comments on the game, on IGG and on GMs, etc.
(5)Normally, sentinels should be online at least three times a week, and the time should not be less than 2 hours, if a sentinel can not go online for a period of 1 month, they should inform the GMs in advance.

Specific Flows:
1. We will announce the sentinel name list of from the last time in our forum, and players can vote for them according to their performance. [/color]Quit /Poor/Good, and please give your comments honestly. We will cancel those sentinels’ qualifications who don’t perform well and confiscate the pet---- Ghastly Dragon，however those who perform well can still be a sentinel and obtain a special gift. The result will also be announced in our forum.
2. You can log onto our forum and put a post with your server names, character names, ID and your “application speech” under the “Sentinel Application Activity” thread, with title being ”Sentinel Application” and the contents of application speech should contain 100 words or more. 1 IP address only allows you to apply for one account, so please don’t apply for accounts repeatedly. Otherwise we will cancel your qualifications. Please apply for an account in the right time and the right place.
3. After the application, we will begin to select those qualified candidates, with the comments of the GMs and the Sentinels mainly determining the results. We will announce the results in our forum within 3 days.
4. Once we announce the sentinel candidates, there will be three days for these candidates to encourage their friends in game to vote for them., at the same time we will open a new zone for them to vote and each candidate is supposed to post a speech in the right time and right place to tell players why they should vote for them. The results of the voting will probably determine who will be the next sentinels.
We will announce the results on our official website. Visit our official website for the latest information.
Want to be a respected sentinel? Wanna feel happier? Then sign up now. ^^

Myth War Online Team, IGG
2007-11-9
