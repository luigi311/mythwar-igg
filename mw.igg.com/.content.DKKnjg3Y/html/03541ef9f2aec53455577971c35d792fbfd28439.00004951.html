Sentinel Selection

Thought long and hard today about whether to comment on the way this has been run, or just go away quielty and just get on with the game istself.
But I have concerns over the way it has been run and the selections been made.
There are 3 points in particular I wish noted.
1; The format for nominations and selections etc was clearly stated on the forums at the beginning of this whole process, yet it clearly has not been followed. Why? I asked support, but no one could give me any clear answers.
2; On Achenar there has only been selected 7 sentinels, where as the rest have 8. Now I know in the initial postings it says "about 8 " , but again I have no answer to the reason for only 7 being selected.
3; As I understand, you had to nominate or propose yourself for selection in the 1st instance. That would then be followed up with a list of wannabe sentinels who have been selected from the nominations. And finally after the probation period, the sentinels would be selected. However, there is an irregularity with this again on Achenar in that 2 of the sentinels selected where not selected for the probation period. I am still trying to find their nominations, however, support has already admitted to me one of these had no nomination in the first place.
If this is the case, then can anyone explain what has gone on here. Support can't or won't because I've asked them.

To conclude I would ask that all current selections be suspended until such time this can be cleared up.
This sends out all the wrong messages to new players who join and who wish to get more involved in the running of a game they love so much. Whats the point when there is a hierachy  based on length of time on game, or id numbers even, or which guild you happen to be in.
I was asked to put forward for the second round of selections. Second round? Where did this come from? When? Why? Thankyou, but I think I'll give it a miss.
I help lots of people on my server where and when I can. I don't use bots, or 3rd party software, or manipulate the sw thats installed. Maybe thats where I went wrong.
Please try to see my comments as constructive rather than sour grapes at not being selected. I can take not being selected, but not over someone who wasn't even in the initial runnings.
