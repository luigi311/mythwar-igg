  PK event coming
Dear players:
Do you have perfect skills? Do you have excellent equipment? Do you have super pets? Do you wait for a chance to show yourself? Do you want to prove you are the best player in MW? The chance is coming, please join our PK activity.
Period: 2007.6.27——2007.6.30
Prize: Every participator will get one Shapeshifting Potion which can transforms you into a Ghost Warrior (Item level 3).
The party that enters 1/4 final will be rewarded two Trainer Device and one Super Bag of Renewal.
The party which enter the semifinal will be rewarded one bloodstone and one Shapeshifting Potion which can transforms you into a Mad Ogrewalker（item level 4）.
All champion party members will obtain item mall 200 points.

Content: All participators will be divided into two groups, LVL1 to LVL 124 for a group, LVL 1-14 to LVL1-104 for another group.
Each party should be just three persons. If you are interested, please come to forum event area to sign up. You have to provide your Server name, the three members’ character name and also ID, level, leader.
Procedures：
1. If you want to join, please be quick, because we only choose the first eight parties of LVL1 to LVL 124 group and, LVL 1-14 to LVL1-104 group each server to participate. The registration is from now to EST 0：00 June 28, 2007.
2. We will sort out and announce the list of all the qualified parties and competition arrangements on June 28. Here, we should notice you pay close attention to competition arrangements on the forum if you have registered in case you miss the competition time.
3. This competition will last three days from June 28 to June 30. The time is at 19:00 on each server. Please keep close eye on it. If you have registered but wouldn’t present, you would be regarded as giving up and you couldn’t obtain any rewards, moreover, your opponent would promote directly.
4. All of the prizes will be given out after every match except the prizes for champion, the champion’s prizes ( 200 item mall points) will be added into the winners’ account before July 3rd.

This activity is a tentative of IGG, so there are restrictions on the competition, only choose the first eight parties of each server to participate. Sorry for the inconvenience.
