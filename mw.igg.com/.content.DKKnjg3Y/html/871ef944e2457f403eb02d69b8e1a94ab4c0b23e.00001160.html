We will be holding the “Rock, Paper and Scissors” event again in the forum during server maintenance this week

Rock: Your hand is fisted.
Scissors: Unwind your first finger and middle finger. Others are fisted.
Paper: Unwind all your fingers.

Every gesture stands for a weapon. Rock defeats Scissors; Scissors defeat Paper and Paper defeats Rock. It’s a draw if you both pull the same weapon.

 We will write 10 posts in the forum. Every new post hides a Rock, Paper or Scissors. Players can reply to the post with a Rock, Paper or Scissors. You can see which weapon was used in the original post after your response.

Reward: 100  integrals

Notes:
 1. You are unable to rewrite your post.
2. Each account can only reply to a post once.
3. The player who replies the right gesture the most quickly will be the winner
