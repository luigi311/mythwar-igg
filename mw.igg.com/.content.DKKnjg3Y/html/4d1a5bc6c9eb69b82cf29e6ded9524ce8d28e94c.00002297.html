Veterans Recruited


Breath-taking Mine Wars, the awe-inspiring Siege War and four distinctive Weekly Events, each of which gives their participants large and surprisingly good rewards, are part of the new features in Myth War 2 online. Are you itching to play this great new game? Here is your chance. This is our invitation given to all of our veterans to join us in Myth War 2 Online.

The Duration of Sign-up application: September 20th to September 26th

The Duration of Character Making: September 27th to September 30th


Event Details:
All veterans whose characters have been reborn once in the original Myth War
can apply to set their new characters in Myth War 2
at level 50 and will be rewarded with 200,000 talent.
All veterans whose characters have reached level 60+ in Myth War
can apply to set their new characters in Myth War 2 at level 30 and will be rewarded with 100,000 talent.
Guild Leaders from guilds Level 2+ consisting of ten guild members can apply for stationing in Myth War 2 with their guilds at Level 2 and with 10,000,000 gold for guild funds .

Notes: Only one character can be created with one account. Participants' characters in Myth War
will be saved while they are playing Myth War 2.

How to enter:
1. Veterans must use their accounts in Myth War (there must be at least one character Level 60+, or reborn once in the account) to get started in Myth War 2 and create new characters.
2. Only one character can be created with one account.
3 Veterans must reply to our event thread in the forum and apply for the setting made upon their characters' levels.

Character's Level Setting Application Format:
Account: ***
MW1: server:***
character name:**** character ID:*** (qualified account info)
MW2: character name:*** Character ID:****(new character in Myth WarⅡ)

Guild's Level Setting Application Format
MW1:Server name:***
Guild Name:*** Guild leadeer:***

MW2:Guild Name:**** Guild Leader:***
 Any ten guild members' ID info:
 character name:**** character ID:***

The new features will blow your mind! Have fun in our game!
