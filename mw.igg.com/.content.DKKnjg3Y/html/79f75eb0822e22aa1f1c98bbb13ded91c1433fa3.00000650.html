Win a Saint pet and an Olympic ticket

Win a Saint pet and an Olympic ticket


We will soon be holding a special auction to celebrate the upcoming Beijing 2008 Olympic Games, where players stand to win a Saint pet and aticket to the Olympic Football Tournament!
 The event is expected to be held on 24 June 10:00 pm -25 June 10:00 pm (EDT GMT-4) and the auction will last for 24 hours. The exact time has not been confirmed yet, so please check back for more information.


Rules:
1.The reserve price for this auction event is 200 integrals. Each bid increase must be at least 100 integrals.


2. If no one offers a higher price for 3 hours after a player has bid for it, the prize will belong to the player offering highest price when the auction comes to a close. If the winning player does not have enough integrals to match their bid, we will offer the ticket to the second highest bidder.


3. Please remember, you must not place a bid higher than the number of integrals your character has. Doing so will invalidate your bid.


4. All participants should observe the forum rules.


5. After the event has closed, we will announce the winner's forum username. Winners please reply to our private messages including your character names and account; we are not responsible for any losses caused if you provide incorrect contact information.


6. IGG reserves all rights to final judgment of this event.  


Rewards: A Saint pet (winner can choose which saint who he/she want) and a ticket to the Olympic Football Tournament!
