Training Routes

Revive Arena

Monsters: Capilla (level 2), Green Capilla (level 4), Ant Eater ( level 6)

Leveling Suggestion: 1-6

The monsters are of low level and hence the damage done to players are lower. Therefore, suitable for new players.

  
Tree of Life 1

Monsters: Capilla ( level 2 ) , Green Capilla ( level 4 ) and Teethor (level 5 )

Leveling Suggestion: 1-6

The monsters here are of low level and uses mainly physical attack. Damages to players are very low and is suitable for new players to familiarize the game
Tree of Life 2

Monsters: Ant Eater (Level 6), Nepenthes (Level 8) and Evil Ant Eater (Level 10)

Leveling Suggestion: 6-12

The monster skill is slightly low. If the players have level 1 armour, they'll be able to up level easier.
Tree of Life 3

Monsters: Poison Teethor (Level 12), Earth Wolf (Level 13), Evil Nepenthes (Level 14), and Flower Spirit

Leveling Suggestion: 12-16

The monsters here are of slightly higher level and they are able to cast spell. Players who want to up level here needs to carry more HP portion. Please move in pair as traveling alone is very dangerous
Tree of Life 4

Monsters: Lizard Bandit (Level 46), Greater Wolf (Level 47), Mad Ogrewalker (Level 48)

Leveling Suggestion: 46-50

Due to the complicated maze, players are encouraged to use the magical scroll and travel in groups to train to up level
