Frequently Ask Questions[FAQs]

since no one decided to make a faqs of MW2,i decided to make one..

hope noobs out there read this and stop spamming world chat

1.Where is Tree of Life 1 and 2?
-NO such places have been seen in MW2..TOL 1 is basically in Revive Arena and TOL 2 in Snow Ridge


2.How can i capture pets?Do i need skill for this?
-No,u dun need any skill for this..ur a pure talented catcher born to catch pets and die by it..Just click the CTU button or ALT-W and click on the mob u want to catch..success is not guaranteed unless ur Chuck Norris


3.How do i get talent points(points to lvl up skills)?
-the famous Mokka quest or that natural mentor gives u TPs..
..There's a rumour that Mokka sometimes gives 10k Tps

4.Where the hell is Thief Boss and Theif Girl?!
-They r in revive arena and if u still got problems finding them,use the Tab button


5.Where do i get 2x exp?
-Master Dom in wood near tele or bly in Lansquenet League(what the hell is Lansquenet anyway? )


6.Where can i find collector?
-in bly near weaponry..


7.What do i do with dark blood?
-Collect 10 and give it to Collector for a decent amount of EXP


8.Why i cant equip my pets?!
-u need a specific title to use it..if system says"bla bla bla Wanderer bla bla bla"then u must be a Wanderer or higher rank to equip that pet..the stronger the pet,the higher the rank u need..well i say stick with Chincilla =p


9.Where do i get my awesome second skills?
-in class zone..talk to own race guardian and fight another race..you can talk to other races guardian ...if ur a mage,then ur gunna get raped by borg


10.How do i quick skill my awesome skills?
-click on the skill scroll or ALT-V(not in battle btw)and click on respective skill and press either F1-F8


11.I cant lvl up my skills II?system says something abut preposing skill
-Lvl up the corresponding skill I to lvl 25 then u can max skill II without preposing skill popping out..



12.Where in the blue hell is mercenary chief?
-not so sure but i think he is in Lasquenet League in Bly or he is Lansquenet Official in wood..70% i might be wrong



13.To whom should i give help letter?
-To the official in wood near bank..u should know this IF u r CLEVER and refer to the quest log


14.How do i get 'Wanderer' title?/How to get soulstones?
-by completing the Soulstone of Light quest..HERE


15.What is "mail of the blessing", and how do i capture it and give it to that borg in elder zone?*asked by sonqoqu*
-u dun capture 'Mail of Blessing',u buy it from armory..


16.There is no number 16 at the moment..will add if i find any FAGs ..u can also contribute by posting ur Faqs and maybe some answers here..also,correct me if im wrong..


Extras:
-U cant trade Sword of Stars
-When reaching lvl 10,20,30,40 and 50talk to mall promoter to get newbie rewards
-WiseChuck FTW!!
