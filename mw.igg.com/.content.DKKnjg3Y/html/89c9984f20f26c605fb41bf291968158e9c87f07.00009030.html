Promotion Activity Sign-up Ends

 

Ending Oct 25th,the sign-up period for the "Promote and Obtain Credits"activity is now over. Many players have signed up for the activity, and we are deeply impressed by their enthusiasm. Two clarifications need to be made though

1.The period October 23rd --- 25th was the time for players to sign up for the activity, not the time for recommending new players, which means the players you recommended during this period won' t count.

2. We have already verified that some players have registered more than one account by themselves. We want to clearly restate that the rules are, one player can only register one ID, and if players are caught cheating, their qualifications will be cancelled. Please don' make us be the bad guy and simply follow the rules.
