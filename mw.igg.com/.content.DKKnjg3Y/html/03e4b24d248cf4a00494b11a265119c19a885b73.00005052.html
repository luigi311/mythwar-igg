Week 3:  This one the fighting was rather smooth and fast.  Only about 4 or 5 teams were even entered, so it went very fast.  In general the fights were fairly bug free and such, and it started quicker too.  There were problems at the end though, with the GM(andy868) being very unprofessional.  He said he couldn't actually hand out the rewards, and the winners had to wait until tomorrow to claim them from a better gm.  Apparently the lead gm doesn't trust andy868 enough to allow him to spawn new items or pets.  Then he said that the winners could choose saint beasts, and he put one of each on follow behind him.  No other gm's would honor this when later asked about it.  Then he was talking with a player called liberation and said he was going to do drows with them on Missouri.  Basically showing favoritism, and not acting in the professionally manner I expect from customer support.

Week 4: This one was made horrible by poor gm management, and bugs, and players just acting terrible.  Oh yeah, and just plain all around gm power abuse.  In one of the first fights, which involved shinsei, they had a player who lost connection before the match.  The gm told them they had 5 minutes to get him back in, or find a replacement.  In the end the gm gave shinsei 30 minutes, because thats when the player finally showed up.  I don't know who runs a tournament like this and allows a team 30 minutes when they aren't ready or prepared.  I can understand 5 minutes, maybe as many as 10.  But after waiting 30 minutes a team should be disqualified long before then.  To make things worse, the shinsei guild(apparently not appreciative of people waiting patiently) had members logging alts in and out as spectators the entire fight(to try and lag the opponents).  I saw them bragging about this in local chat during the fight.  Then, I don't know what happened at the end of that match.  When the team who was winning had the other team all dead except for 1 pet, which was stunned.  Members of the team were suddenly coming back to life for no reason.  Myself, and everyone else who was watching, and I'm sure those playing on both sides, were really at a loss as to how people come back to life when there isn't anyone to heal or pot them.  I can only assume either some really weird lag bug I've never seen before, or some type of hack by a player or gm.

Week 4 continued:  Once the finals match was ready to be fought, this shinsei team was demanding a re-match.  And for no reason other than they thought they can win if they fight enough times.  The GM(andy868 again) actually backed them up, and made system announcements saying that this team which lost gets to fight again.  I don't know who runs a tournament where once you're out, you get to go back in.  They lost fair and square, and didn't really come close to winning either from what I saw.  I didn't see any basis for allowing a re-match.  Some of the members of both that team and shinsei guild kept crying that they lost and such, and that they wanted a rematch, and that they had the GM in their pocket.  But the leader of shinesi stood up for honesty and said they wouldn't fight again.  So the finals went on as they should have.  After it was all over the players from the team who won and beat that shinsei team had some harsh words for the GM.  The one who was trying to sneak in a rematch for his guild, and the one who promised them saint beasts the prior week.  Even though no curse words were said, and nothing was said on world chat either, the GM started to mute players of this team(unfairly by anyones standards).  Then the GM proceeded to poke fun of the muted players on world chat.  Clearly abusing his power.  As it turns out gm andy868 is Jay on live support, and liberation in game.  Who had an alt in shinsei during this event.
