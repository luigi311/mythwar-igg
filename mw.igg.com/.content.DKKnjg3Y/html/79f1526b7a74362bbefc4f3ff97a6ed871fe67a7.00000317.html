Mythwar II Closed Beta Coming Soon

After a period of frantic hard work, the Mythwar II Closed Beta is expected to start at 0:00 am on June 13(GMT-5) and last 7 days. Please pay attention to the following details to ensure everything goes smoothly during the test period:
1.The closed beta will begin at 0:00 am on June 13(GMT-5).One server group will be opened up to take part in the Closed Beta.
2. Once the Closed Beta comes to a close, we will delete all test information. Players taking part in the Beta should submit any game bugs they find to us.
3.Players will need to log into our official site and use an activation code to activate the game, and will then be able to start playing the game.  During the Closed Beta, we will also hold a series of online and offline events. Participants will have the chance to win rewards. Further details will be announced on the official site.
4.You can report all bugs found in the Closed Beta and any opinions or suggestions to the Closed Beta board in our forum or send them to the email address mwgm@igg.com. We will collate and address all bugs as part of the next phase of development. If we decide to adopt any of your suggestions, you will receive a reward for your great idea!
The mythwar II client is available to download now and its auto-update facility is operational - check the download page on our official site (http://mw2.igg.com/download/client.php)
We appreciate your help testing this game and look forward to hearing your comments and feedback.
