About Pets' Origins

Every creature in the world has its origin, and it’s the same to pets. In the MWW, according to the origins, there are 6 kinds of pets so far, they’re normal, Intelligent, Howling, Deity, Saint and Christmas pets.

Of course, they’re different in appearances, but there is much more than that. For instance:

Different origins affect greatly on the pets’ beginning GR (growth rate), normal ones are mostly under 1.25, while the relatively ones have higher GRs.

Intelligent pets are the elites of normal pets, though they’re the same in appearance, intelligent ones are much more advanced than their counterparts in many aspects.

Most pets can only reborn for once, and their appearance will not change; but the Deity Pets can reborn for twice and have their appearances changing for twice, too.

Their attributes are also quite different from one another which can not be completely listed here.
