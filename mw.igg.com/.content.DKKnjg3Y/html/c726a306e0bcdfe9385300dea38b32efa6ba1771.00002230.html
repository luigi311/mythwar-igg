My Pet, My Friend

My Pet, My Friend



Some say that the dog is our most loyal friend; however we are not so sure!
In Myth War every pet is worthy of the title "loyal friend". You have faced many perils and many setbacks with your loyal pet along side and never did they leave you; no matter what happened…So we are going to give you the chance to express your gratitude to your beloved pet by chronicling your adventures in a comic style story featuring you and your pet.

Event Location:  Event Zone in the official forum

Event Period:August 8th to August 31th
Selection Period: September 1st to September 3rd

Event Contents:

A four-box comic strip complete with descriptions of your adventures.

 Format:

Copy to clipboard
Code:
Picture
[hide=3000]account name ,character name,character ID[/hide]


Event Rules:
1. The four-box comic strip you make should be on the theme of  Myth War Online, the content of the story has no restrictions, The story you tell can be funny, touching, etc…
 2. You should submit your comic titled  "My Pet, My Friend" in the Event Zone of our forum.
3. Post your work with your server name and character name.
4. All work must be original and not in violation of any of the forum rules. If your work goes against this rule then you will be automatically disqualified.
5. Work containing any vulgar, offensive, or any inappropriate content will be automatically rejected.
6. IGG reserves the right of final interpretation and the ownership of the winners' work.

Reward:

First Place(1) Howling Beast
Second Place (2) Sand Giant
Third Place (3) 1.3 growth intelligent pet
In addition, we will pick 5 outstanding participants and give them 150 credits.
