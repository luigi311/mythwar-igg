12# A A A Post at 2007-1-19 03:44  Show author
couple things to clarify:

- only purple diamond and orange diamond (from phantasm gem) work on shoes. other diamonds won't work. Likewise, all other gems except purple and orange work on weapons, purple and orange diamonds won't. necklaces can take any gem and/or any diamond.

- to be exact about color ranks (above orange), you take each skill exp for that level and round it up to closest 100, then multiply by 16 to get the total exp needed for that color. e.g. skill lvl 9 is 6682 =&gt; round up 6700 =&gt; multiple by 16 =&gt; 107200, which is the total skill exp needed for green. skill 10 is 10140 =&gt; round up 10100 =&gt; multiply by 16 =&gt; 161600, which is total skill exp needed for light blue.

Assigning Skill
In battle mode, right click the skill u want to use, it will view in chat windows you assign a skill, then press CTRL +1 to assign a skill for your pet. ALT+1 to assign skill in your character.
So every battle you only press CTRL+1 and ALT+1 for easy skill Spamming

SHORTCUT KEYS OF PET

Shortcut key Function
&lt; Ctrl &gt; + A The pet attacks
&lt; Ctrl &gt; + D The pet defends
&lt; Ctrl &gt; + 1 The pet with default uses technical skill
&lt; Ctrl &gt; + E Pet command of protection
&lt; Ctrl &gt; + W Opening Skill windows of pet
&lt; Ctrl &gt; + Z Tool bag opening and closing of pet

ATTACK MODE ON BATTLE

Shortcut key Function

&lt; Alt &gt; + Q Character and pet automatic operation attack
&lt; Alt &gt; + R Character escape, the pet remaining, you defend
&lt; Alt &gt; + P Exchanging the pet
