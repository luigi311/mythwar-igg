  Support
FAQ

Q) Is it normal for a pet to level up faster than the main character?
A) Yes, this is very normal, as the main character is allowed to keep a few pets. Pets will always be able to level up faster than the main character.

Q) How many pets can a character keep?
A) Every main character can keep up to 8 pets and there will be an increment of 3 pets after every reincarnation. The advantages of keeping multiple pets are: a) being able to use different pets for different battles; b) being able to trade your pets with other players.

Q) How can I view my chat log / history?
A) Select one of your friends in your friend list and double click 'history' to view the chat log for that friend.

Q) I have other applications running while running Myth War, will this affect the game stability in any way?
A) Yes, there are a few programs that will cause clashes and system errors with Myth War, such as 'RealPlayer'. Please make sure that RealPlayer is not running while playing Myth War.

Q) Which server should I choose to play in?
A) When you launch the game you can choose anyone main server you would like to play in. Under each main server there will be various sub-servers (approx 20) in future, where players will be able to choose their desired server.

Q) If during downloading or updating of Myth War client, a disconnection occurs, what can I do?
A) Please check your internet connection and firewall settings and if it still doesn’t work, please check the Myth War website for the game server status, it might be turned down temporarily for maintenance.

Q) If during downloading or updating of Myth War client, there is insufficient hard disk space, what can I do?
A) Please free up hard disk space prior to installation to ensure that there is sufficient space to install Myth War.

Q) How can I keep my version of Myth War updated to the latest version?
A) Upon launch, the game will detect if there are any new patches available. If there is a new update, the patch will be downloaded automatically. Alternatively, you can download the patch manually from the Myth War website.

Q) After I entering the user name and password, there is no response from the server, what can I do?
A) Please check your internet connection and firewall settings to make sure they are correct. If you are still unable to launch the game; please check the Myth War Official website for the game server status.

Q How can I create a character?
A) 1) Login to the game;
2) Select “Create New Character”;
3) Select a character you like;
4) Select the character’s sex;
5) Create a name and press “Enter”.

Q) How many races/classes are there?
A) There are 4 main classes.
Human: Humans possess the deadliest physical attack skills.
Male Human: Wanderer.
Female: Thief.
Centaur: Centaurs possess superb ability and arrow attack skills.
Male: Archery.
Female: Healer.
Mage: Mages mainly use magical attacks as their form of offense.
Male: Wizard.
Female: Witch.
Cyborg: Their vast knowledge of technology is their main form of power.
Male: Destroyer.
Female: Destroyer.

Q) How many characters can we create with one account?
A) You can create up to 5 different characters with each account.

Q) After death, where will the character go?
A) The character will be reincarnated in Woodlingor.

Q) What is the highest level that a character can reach?
A) The highest level a character can reach is level 100.

Q) Can I use symbols when naming the characters?
A) No.

Q) Can we change the name of in-game characters?
A) No, in-game characters’ names are not allowed to be changed.

Q) Are there any restrictions on how many alphabets can be used to name the characters?
A) The maximum is 14 characters.

Q) How can I delete characters?
A) Log into the game; go to the sky passage and find the Grimreaper.

Q) What can I do if I accidentally delete my character?
A) Players are able to retrieve their deleted characters within 7 days. You may do this from the website.

Q) How much gold can a character carry?
A) How much gold a character can carry depends on the experience level of the character. Level 1: 10000 gold. After leveling up, the amount of gold will gradually increase. Level 100: 1010000000 gold.
Q) How can I make my character run?
A) Right click at the destination on screen to make your character run to that location.

Q) How can I select skills to use in attacks?
A) During battles, click on Skill (open up skill list) and select the skill you want for attack and click on the enemy.

Q) How can I trade with other characters?
A) Click on the trade button or use “Alt+X” and left-click on the players you wish to trade with.

Q) How can I give an item to another player?
A) Click on the give button or use “Alt+G” and right-click on the person you would like to give your item to.

Q) How can I form or join a party?
A) In order to form or join a party, a leader must first exist. If there is currently no leader, you can click on the party button to have yourself to become a leader for the team. To join a party, click the party button and select the leader and wait for approval. The short-cut key is “Alt+T”.

Q) How to make friends in the game?
A) Click on the chat button to open up the chat window. Click on the buddy
button and drag the icon to the character you wish to make friends with.

Q) How to dismiss my pet?
A) Click on the pet icon, on the top left hand corner of the screen to activate the pet window. Then select a pet from the list and click on the dismiss button.

Q) How to replenish your health or MANNA point?
A) To quickly replenish your health or MANNA, open up your inventory, select a fruit or potion and click the use button.

Q) How to drop item?
A) Open your equipment window, select the item which you want to discard and press the drop button.

Q How to view a battle?
A) Click “Alt+A”, and select the player in the battle.

Q How to chat?
A) Type in whatever you wish to say in the chat box and then press enter to send your message. By pressing the up arrow key, the previous message will be loaded into the chat box.

Q) About the chat channel?
A) There are several modes of chatting in Myth War:
World: Talk to everyone in Myth War game.
Market: Talk to people on the same server.
Guild: Talk to people on the same guild/ country.
Private message: To the designated characters/players.
Party: Talk to the same group of people.
Local: Talk to people on the same screen.
Q) How to use emoticons?
A) Click on the smiling button just above the chat box to pop up the window of emoticons and click on the icon you want. Alternatively, press # + emoticons number, for example: #50.

Q) How to find a buddy?
A) Click the magnifying icon at the bottom of the chat system pop-up box. Type in either the nickname or the I.D. of your buddy and click “confirm”.

Q) How to engage in a private conversation with my buddy?
A) Right-click on your friend to mark him as a PM target, change the chat channel mode to PM.

Q) How to locate my exact location?
A) Press TAB button and a map will appear. Move your mouse towards the mini map, and the coordinates will be shown on top of your mouse.

Q) Why can’t I see the other players?
A) Your display view mode can be changed. Press F2 to toggle between “view player” and “hide player” modes.

Q) How can I teleport my character to other lands?
A) Use the mini map and locate the harbor, station or teleport arena.

Q) Where are the merchants?
A) The mini-map will show all the merchants.

Q) Where can we sell items?
A) You have to visit the trading zone to sell items.

Q) How can I acquire new skills?
A) When a new character has just been created, they will possess standard low level skills. The more you train your skills in the game, the higher your skill levels will be.

Q) How can I use skills in the battle?
A) During battle select the skill from the skills window, and click on the target. Some skills are for attacking, some are for healing teammates.
Q) How can I train and advance my skill level?
A) The more frequent you use a particular skill, the faster it rises and upgrades to another skill level.

Q) Why am I unable to use the skills?
A) This could happen if you MANNA is used up, or if your target is dead.
Q) Why should I complete the newbie quest?
A) The newbie quest is kind of a training simulation to familiarize you with different aspects of the game such as acquiring armor, swords or pets, etc.

Q) What is so special about the main quests?
A) The Main quests fully unleash the potential of Myth War and test the limits of the player’s strengths. Main quests can only be played once.
Q) What’s the maximum number of people allowed in a party/team?
A) 5 People.

Q) How can I form a party/team?
A) Click on the main map “Party” button or “Alt+T”, the cursor will become a flag. When the Player clicks on his/her character, a flag will be shown on the top of the character, this signifies that player has become a leader and a party is formed.

Q) How can I request to add myself to a party?
A) Click on the main map “Party” button or “Alt+T”, mouse will change to group icon during the period of time. Highlight the player who is the leader. You will be informed with the message on the screen “XXX has requested to party”, hold on for a while. Once a party leader approves, you will be in the party.

Q) How can I leave a party?
A) Players can highlight the lower right corner “Party” icon. A party window will appear, with the “leave party” icon. After you click on the icon, close the window. Players will resume their original status and will be free to control their characters once again.

Q) Why does my character automatically follow the leader?
A) Once you’ve joined a party your character will automatically follow the team leaders wherever they go.

Q) How to add friends?
A) Click on the add friend button and type in the players’ name.

Q) How many friends can I add?
A) The maximum is 100 friends.

Q) How can I know my friends are online or not?
A) When your friends go online or offline the server will prompt you.
Q) How can I send mail to my friends?
A) Select the players you want to send mail to. Then a message window will pop up, type in the message you want to send and press send.
Q) How can I search players?
A) Open the friend list, click on the search list and add in the players ID to search.
Q) Can I leave message if the other players are not online?
A) Yes. Once the players go online he will be able to see the message.

Q) How can I know that my good friends are online?
A) If the players are online, their name in your friend list will be in green colour.

Q) How to delete friends?
A) In the chat list, right click on the players’ name to delete the players.
Q) Where are the places you can buy heal or mana pots and armor?
A) In every town there is a medical store and armor shop or weapon shop you will be able to buy your required armor, weapon, healing or mana pots.

Q) Why I can’t wear my armor?
A) Myth War armor is classified by different types of races, gender and required levels. You need to fit the specified requirement to be able to wear an item.
Q) Can I use other races’ item?
A) No.

Q) How to redeem the first pet?
A) In sky passage find the pet redemption NPC. Talk to the NPC and he will randomly give you a pet. If you are not satisfied with the given pet you are able to release the pet and re-pick until you are satisfied with the given pet.

Q) How to let my pet join the battle?
A) Open your pet stat window and click on the battle button and the pet will join you when you are in battle.
Q) How can I let my pet follow me?
A) Open your pet stat window click on the follow button and the pet will follow you.

Q) How to dismiss my pet?
A) Click on the pet icon, on the top left hand corner of the screen to activate the pet window. Then select a pet from the list and click on the dismiss button.
Q) Why can't I dismiss my pet?
A) If your pet is following you, you can’t dismiss it.

Q) Does pet have skill ability?
A) Yes.

Q) How to play the point accumulation quest?
A) You can look for NPCs in Woodlingor. By completing a simple task assigned by these NPCs, you can accumulate points and these points can be exchanged for experience and rewards.

Q) How to use the teleport scroll?
A) The teleport scroll can be use to record the coordinates and teleport to another place. Players who are lost in the maze can use the teleport scroll to teleport to previous saved position.

Q) How to trade quest items with my instructor?
A) Click on the give icon in game or press “alt + G” then click the mouse on the instructor. After the selection of the quest item, press “confirm”.

Q) How to spread rumors? If the rumors become true will I get rewards?
A) Give the rumor scroll to the whisperer and select the way you would like to spread the rumor. For a rumor to become true, a certain number of players must click on the Messenger. Once sufficient players have read the message, it becomes true and you will get your reward.

Q) How to sell unwanted items?
A) You can go to the trading centre and sell the unwanted item to the various buyers there.
