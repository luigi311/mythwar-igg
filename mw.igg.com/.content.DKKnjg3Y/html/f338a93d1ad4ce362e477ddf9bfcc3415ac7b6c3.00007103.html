  MSN and IGG Promotion
Event Duration: Nov 28th -Dec 10th
Only IGG promoters are qualified to participate in the activity

Purpose: In order to recommend msn promotion method to our promoters.
Event Rules:
1. On Nov 28th, our administrator will post an activity announcement in the promoter forum.
Activity announcement: http://www.igg.com/forum/viewthread.php?tid=444
2. If players have both an IGG promoter account and an MSN account, they can change their personal message to a Player Promotion System link.
3. After setting the message, they should post the screenshots (below) with their game names and game accounts under the activity announcement.

4. After the end of the activity, the administrator will transfer 10 credits to the participants' accounts.

Rewards:
Every participant can obtain 10 credits.

Note:
1. Participations should keep their personal information on their MSN until Dec 10th, otherwise they won't obtain rewards.
2. IGG reserves all the rights for final judgment.
