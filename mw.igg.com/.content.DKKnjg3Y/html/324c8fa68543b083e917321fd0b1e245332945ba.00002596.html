Forum Rules

The In-zone MythWarOnline.com Rules

     Introduction
          Welcome to the MythWars forums. This is a short guide to help maintain an organized and friendly forum. Please follow these rules as outlined below.
     General Posting
~ Post in the forums that match your topic the closest.
~ Use search as often as possible to find topics that already discuss your area of interest.
~ Read all the stickies in the forum you are about to post into.
~ If you have questions about a specific forum, send a PM to the moderator that is running it.
~ Treat other forum members with respect
~ Some forums have specific posting and voting rules, please read the all the stickies.
     Topics to avoid
~ Do not make pointless posts. Responding with "Yea" or "lol" is considered spamming, double and triple or multiple posting is also considered spamming. See the forum specific rules for more information as to what constitutes spam. Trolling, flaming and spamming are not allowed.
~ Do not post offensive text, images or videos. If you aren't sure if its offensive or not then do not post it. Keep in mind that there are many young members in this forum. Offensive content will be taken extremely seriously.
~ Do not talk about 3rd party programs. These include, but are not limited to: maphacks, cd cracks, pirated software, etc.
~ If you have a problem with a member, don't post in the forums about it. Instead, send a PM to a moderator directly. If you have a problem with a moderator, send a PM directly to an Administrator. Flaming in forums won't be allowed.
~ Do not advertise any other products, games, maps or websites on this forum without direct permission from an administrator
~ All signatures should not exceed 500 pixels wide and 300 pixels tall. This includes both images and text.
~ All these rules apply to both posts and content posted in signatures.
~ The flaming of or trash-talking of any legitimate MythWar player or guild will not be tolerated. While we do not directly support or sponsor any of these players/guilds, they are still a part of our community as a whole and should be shown a due amount of respect.
~ When the server is going through a maintainance look on the webpage on when it will be finished and calculate on your own time zone. Don't spam the forum asking when will it be on. (Cat_girl2006)
~ Members are not allowed to post private conversations via Live Support without the express permission of GMs. Such posts will be deleted.


Above all else, use common sense when posting on the board.
