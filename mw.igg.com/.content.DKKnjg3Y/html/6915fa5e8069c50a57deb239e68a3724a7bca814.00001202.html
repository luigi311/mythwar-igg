26# A A A Post at 2008-6-11 05:26 AM  Show author
We all start as little white ranked recruits aka newbies or noobs, so we pursue the paths to gaining levels and most importantly, skills. From the start we see most goto revive to mokka max our skills then we become orange rank. Lev 50 we usually start wood defender and usually you see parties asking for people to join but they sometimes add orange or red rank to the request. Many times from experience i was rejected just because my color was not as required so i understand why the poster thinks its racist.

We progress further and are able to do other level based quests such as bly defender then seal etc, note that the system tells us that we are able to do these higher quests by player level and not by player skills for that level, even so most higher quest parties require that each party member have at least the minimum needed skills to be of any real use. The only real exception to that would be a weaker party member is either your own alt character or your friend etc.

I myself have been in parties of good players or bad players and the only thing that let the rest down was 1-2 party members had poor skills or were simply built wrong. There are many very experienced players and they really from experience alone know whats good or bad for them to beat the mobs, so in a way they aren't being racist or discriminating against the weaker player, its called being selective to give most damage to whatever they face and in the shortest time possible.

Nobody likes spamming skills much but if it's what is needed to get into good parties then thats the route to take.
