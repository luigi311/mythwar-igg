Myth War: Review of Works in 2007
A New Year. A New Start! Many things happened during 2007. Now it is the high time to conclude all the significant achievements we all achieved last year.
There were tears of joy and tears of sadness in 2007. We have held various celebration activities whenever there was a festival. Now we want to produce a review of all the works from all the fabulous activities we held. Moreover, we will give participating players special rewards. You can vote either for your favorite works which were selectively chosen or the players you like. Your comments and suggestions are greatly welcomed.
We have the “Most Creative Work” awards, “Participation” awards, the “Most Welcomed New Works” awards and the “Most Impressive Works” awards. In addition, we have set prizes for excellent board managers.
Your constant support helps us improve everyday. We welcome your participation.
Rewards:
Most Creative Work (1)Oceanic Stone Pack
Participation(1) Dream Flyer Pack+Chocolate
Most Welcomed New Works(1) Dream Dragon Pack+Chocolate
Most Impressive Works(1) Icy Crystal Pack+Peculiar Whammy Egg
Excellent board manager(1) Devil Egg

Please vote for them!
