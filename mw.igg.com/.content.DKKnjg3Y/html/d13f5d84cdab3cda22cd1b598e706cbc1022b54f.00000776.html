Shopping for Big Surprises

July is one of the hottest months of the year for good reason. Get 300 silver ingots and more surprises for shopping in the Item Mall from July 12th to July 27th. It doesn't matter if you are buying something in the Item Mall for the first time or if it's your first time that day, you will be given 300 silver ingots and obtain partial points to return.
New players (players who have never bought anything in the Item Mall)
Players who charge at least 100 points into their accounts for the first time from July 12th to July 18th will get 10% of these total points charged paid back. Players who charge another 200 points or more into their accounts from July 19th to July 27th will get 20% of the total points charged paid back.
Players should make sure they charge at least 100 points into their account from July 12th to July 18th or they won't be eligible for the point back policy from July 19th to July 27th.

Actual Consumption

Gift Amount

From July 12th to July 18th

100 points or more

300 silver ingots and 10 % of the total points charged paid back

From July 19th to July 27th

200 points or more

20 % of the total points charged paid back


Veteran Players: (players who have used the Item Mall at least once before July 12th 12：00AM(EDT GMT-4))
Players who charge 500 points into their accounts will get 10% of the total points charged paid back within 3 working days.

Actual Consumption

Gift Amount

500 points or more

300 silver ingots and 10 % of the total points charged paid back

Notes:
1. Event Time: From July 12th to July 27th (EDT GMT-4)
2. Only points charged within the event duration will be effective. Players must use the official way to charge their accounts.
3. Point totals that new players charge from July 12th to July 18th will be collected and worked out on July 19th. Point totals that new players charge from July 19th to July 27th and that veteran players charge during the event period will be collected and worked out on July 28th. All points that must be paid back will be charged into players'accounts within 3 working days based on the period that they turned them in.
