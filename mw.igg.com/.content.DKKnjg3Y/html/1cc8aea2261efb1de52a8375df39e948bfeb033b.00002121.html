I know what you mean dude. But I haven't actually lost out here fortunately for once.
What I was trying to do is to generalise on is this.
Each country has its own trading standard rules and all companies must abide by them to trade. Also there is a code of practice in place for trading on the net that companies are abliged to abide by, otherwise they may be in breach of a domestic trading law which then negates thier right to sell in a particular country.
Rule of thumb is this.
A product or item sold, must be as described and fit for the purpose it is sold for..
I would therefore suggest that if an item bought from the mall with ingots/points paid for by cash is not able to be used then those points must be refunded no matter what the status of the player.
