An advanced guide to fast leveling useful alts (or newbies)

- Assume you have already at least 2-3 main chars with good levels and skills. Best if you have 4, so you don't have to find extra members.
- Assume you have reasonable amount of gold
- Assume you must have good coordination between all chars (i.e. everyone know what you are doing)
- Assume you don't care if they are going to shut down MWI (maybe some of these will apply in MW2?)

This guide is a compilation of many facts and guides. It basically tells you what to do in order to get a useful alt of any race in the shortest possible time. It's not meant to make a perfect alt, i.e. light blue ready to reborn and such. Just an alt with useful skills you can use to spam things with. It assumes you know basic stuff already, so it's not a beginner guide telling you exactly where to go and what skills to use etc.

Steps

Getting out of Sky Passage
- Create the new character(s), i.e. alts.
- Do NOT take quest in front of you to get skills I
- Bring one of your mains to sky passage, bring a group tele, party with alt, tele to Bly.
- Give them low level pets to train as well.
- Take 5 minutes (walk to sky passage)

Leveling to 57
- Get 4 triple-exp scrolls (easy to buy from players)
- Get full party. Go TOL1 -> Sunset -> Badlands -> Bone. Using scrolls all the way.
- When fighting mobs, only mage do ii/iv and cent multi/bliz ii/iv (if you can't kill enemies with one hit of spell, consider skill train your mains first). other things dodge.
- This should take 4 hours max. Less with good skills.

Wood and Bly
- Wood to 60, Bly to 71.
- Use this guide to powerlevel your new characters by repeating round 10s on them:
  http://forum.mw.igg.com/viewthread.php?tid=14375
- This is a matter of 1-2 days with a good party.

Get Skills
- Get skills II and mokka max these 4 skills.
- If alt is cent, get III/IV, mokka but don't max. stop at heal IV just hitting lvl 7.
- If alt is human/borg, get III/IV and mokka max.
- If alt is mage, don't get III/IV.
- Take less than 1 hour if you stock mokka before hand

Seal
- Slow r1-10 seals until 91.
- If alt is mage, fast seal til II are maxed.
- Train pets meanwhile
- If you have hard/slow time with seal, you might consider spam alt skills first (below) then seal, esp. drain IV will help considerably ...and use hypno IV (mokka max is fine) if you know how to use them well in seal.
- approximately 1 week (besides mage)

Spam skills that matter
- If alt is borg, train protect IV (female), drain IV to lvl 9 (both sexes). consider using trainer device for drain IV.
- If alt is human, train III/IV you want. consider using trainer device for poison III/IV. For poison, relog trick helps to not kill the trainers.
- Optionally, blood to full int + int gears (at least necklace). If you're lazy to pot, use good % pots.
- time varies depends on race.

At This Point, You Will Have a Useful ...
- Human with good IV you want
- Mage maxed skill II.
- Borg drain IV lvl 9 and protect IV maxed
- Cent with low-cost heal IV (2490).
--> now you can log in 5 and spam low round survival for money and other possibilities.

Papa
- In all honestly, 91 and 104 doesn't make much difference. So this part is not really needed, but since it can be done fast, why not
- Make melee armors (+8/9 gems is fine, even +5s, whatever you can find). +5 armors are ok. no stress on high armors.
- Follow this guide for papa (fast way) to 104:
  http://forum.mw.igg.com/viewthread.php?tid=15014

The Rest for Perfection (just for completeness)
- If you care about making them perfect. Otherwise, stop here. It's pretty useful already. From here on, it is generally not worth the time.
- Mage get III/IV, mokka, then spam one IV (fire/ice are good choice).
- Spam rest of the skills to max, reborn, etc. (if you want it to be your main)

Tweak it to your needs. Feel free to share your method.
