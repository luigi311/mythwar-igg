Mid-autumn Riddles Guessing

Mid-autumn Day is fast approaching. In order to celebrate this traditional Chinese festival and reward our players for their continued support, we will be holding a “Mid-autumn Riddles Guessing” event in game. This gives players a chance to obtain abundant rewards by guessing the answers to riddles and defeating the Crescent Wolves. Come on and join us.

Event Duration:
9:00am to 12:00pm, 2:00pm to 5:00pm and 7:00pm to 11:00pm（Server Time）every day from September 12th to September 16th.

Event Targets:
level 20 or above players

Event Flow :
1. Players can find the NPC “Moon Cake Seller” around the field on the 3rd server branch and guess the answers to his riddles. If they can get the right answer they will obtain exp, talent points, and even some “Moon Cakes” and Deity Beasts as prizes.
2. If they get a wrong answer then the players must challenge the Crescent Wolf. They can also obtain exp as a reward after they have defeated the Crescent Wolf.


Event Rewards:

Style Reward
Super Reward AOD
High-level Constellation Pet
Deity Beast
Medium Reward Moon Cake (can be used to reset 5 attribute points)
High-level Material
Level 2-7 Magical Crystal
Level 12-13 High-level Gem
Level 2-7 Gem
Valuable Material
(Players can get ONE of them as a reward.)
Common Reward Appointed exp and talent points according to players level

Note: Players can take part in this event alone. Should a team enter then only the team leader will receive the event rewards.

For more informaiton : http://mw2.igg.com/themes/template/event/mid/
