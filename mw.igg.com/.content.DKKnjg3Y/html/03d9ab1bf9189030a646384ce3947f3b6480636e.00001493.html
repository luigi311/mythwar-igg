Rock - Paper - Scissors


Event Duration: 3:00pm-5:00pm every Saturday and Sunday from May 17th to June 17th (Server Time)
Event Contents:
 Rock-Paper-Scissors is a classic children's game that is loved by everyone. Its rules are simple, "Stone beats scissors, scissors beat paper and paper beats stone". This weekend, relax and join us in playing this childhood game again and get the chance to win big rewards
How to play:
 GMs will put one among rock, paper and scissors in the system channel after the game starts. You can answer the GM with the winning word in the world channel. The first player to post the winning answer will be the winner.

Rewards:50 Fortune Bombing Integrals and 3 Mystical Fruit(1.2M Exp)
