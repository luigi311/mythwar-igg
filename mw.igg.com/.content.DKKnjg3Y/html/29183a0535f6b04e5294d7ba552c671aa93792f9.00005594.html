it's not really about color rank, not directly. if you only max, say fire i-iv, and leave the rest at level 1 - which makes you maybe only orange, then you get natural fire resist of 10 and nothing else after reborn. and like cat said, you can check it at the NPC. i assume that the resistance will be linear... say if you max all fire, that's 10140 x 4 = 40560 skill pionts, you get 10 fire resist. if your total skill points in fire is half that, then you get 5 fire resist, etc. so if you are green, it depends on how close you are to light blue. you can get anything from 6.6 to almost 10 (on average). that's my best guess.

if you get all skills to max tho, you get perfect reborn extra bonus (+max level), which depends on race. for mages, your attack is 5% stronger.
