  Christmas Card Show
The sky is whipped with freezing wind while snow continues to fall carelessly along the street. An old man with a long white beard approaches, his long cloak billowing out and around him as his feet shuffle slowly along the frozen ground. In the distance warm light caresses the bitterly cold stone and Holiday carols can be heard drifting merrily on the breeze…Yes, winter is here, and with it comes the Holiday Season, especially Christmas. Have you prepared your gifts and mailed your Christmas cards? The Christmas card, it is an indispensable part of Christmas. You can imagine that when your friends, relatives, and parents read your best wishes and hopes on these cards at this special time of the year, they will feel very happy.
So we will be holding a “Christmas Card” show and offering you the opportunity to spread your wishes and hopes to all your friends and family.

Event Rules：
1.Players should make the Christmas cards themselves using whatever software they prefer.
2.The content should be related to Christmas.
3.Plagiarism is strictly prohibited, otherwise your qualifications will be cancelled.
4.The formats of the pictures should be jpg or gif.
5.Your works should be titled “Christmas Card”
Upload Venue: MW Media Overview
Submit Your Cards Between: Dec 5th——Dec 31st
Selection Time：Jan 1 2008——Jan 7th 2008

Reward：
The first(1) Oceanic Stone Pack+ Bloodstone Gem
The second(1)Icy Crystal Pack+Dragon Eye
The third(2)Chocolate+ Dragon Bone
