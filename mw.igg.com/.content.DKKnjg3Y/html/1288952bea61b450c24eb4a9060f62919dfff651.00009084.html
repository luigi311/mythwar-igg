Wish Making

Starts from Dec 15th until Dec 24t

Content:
During this period, everyone of you could write down one wish as long as it is relevant with Myth War (for example: hope to get a pet) in the Wish Making section created by the forum administrators, the official will randomly pick out three lucky persons and announce their names on Dec 26th.

More:
1. Everyone can only make one wish, and it must have something to do with the game;
2. The title of the post should begin with [my wish];
3. You should provide your Character name and your server name in your post;
4. The announcement will be made on Dec 26th, and if your wish becomes true, please find GMs to fulfill it during Dec 26th to Dec 31st, after the deadline, it will be considered as disclamation.

Reward: the Lucky guy will have his/her wish fulfilled.
