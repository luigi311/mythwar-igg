Brilliant guide, and well put together. And gratz Cronny for geting your guide suggestion taken up on.
Query; Berserk Resistance from Nexo. To my understanding and from observing my own 'serk pets. Berserk dmg is based on the pets base dmg multiplied by the % of the dmg resistance and then pets base dmg added again.This differs to what is stated above. There are however factors which will affect the impact of the damage inflicted.
There are the obvious factors such as melee resist. But there are also pet species domination over another pet whether attacking or being attacked.
An example of this can be easily seen in papa when I use Lucie my Glitter Tiger. If I attack Wild Lizard at the start, it pwns him for over 300k everytime. Yet later on when there are only the Dragons left he barely she barely hits over her base damage. This is also true if I use her in drow. Now I'm not sure whether this is true for all Saint pets vs Dragons as I have just the one, but its a factor worth consider when choosing what pet to build to use for certain quests.
Also, I'm not sure whether the Dragons are actually under the Dragon species of the game or of the Special.
Again great guide dude.
