The main pain at the start of this fight is the drain that is inflicted by Wild Lizard. He needs to be taken out quick, so usually all pets target him at start unless someone is going to cast protect or enhance for any reason.
Here's the Strategy I use as a hummy. Dependant on the party resist. Skill levels preferred are c3= lvl 11, c4 lvl = lvl 10 h4 and h3 lvl 9.
If party has good chaos resist then you might wanna ignore Black for 1st rnd and cast either H4 or c4. H4 can hit black and sleep most of swamps including wild. C4 will usually hit all swamps, wild and Poision lizard.
Next assuming that wild was killed 1st rnd. Pot yourself with your pet 70% ideally, and then try and c3 Black. If it hits, you can then  decide whether you want tyo try to c3 Drow Lord or even winter, and also re-target your pet to a mob. If it misses, you need to decide whether to sleep him again, or try c3 on him again, and also whether you will need another pot for mp.
Do not ever run out of mp until Black is chaosed if you can help it. Party will go mad if Black is un-chaosed and you misss a turn cause you need to pot. If the party chaos resist is good, then the biggest problem will be if any speed pets get hit with his chaos.
Another way is to h3 Black at start then follow up with c3 till he's chaosed..
I find, good comunnication before start of Papa makes for a happy team. If you know the strengths and resists of your party, you can work your tactics out accordingly.

Anyway this is just some of my experience as hummy in party. Now if borg and cent and mage post, we can add it all together.
