I have a couple of suggestions for events, similar to others posted here and some not. Some are events I ran or tried to run as a Guild leader.
1: Pet war. Already run as an event and already posted here. But my suggestion to alter this slightly would be to give at least 2 weeks notice on which class of pet or type of pet. Use low class and common pets for the battles as well. This will give people wishing to participate a chance to aquire one and level it up to participate.

2: Hide and Seek. Again, already used and suggested. However, my suggestion here, and also what I did for a guild event is this. Make multiple npc's to find which must be found in order. On finding them the npc will ask a question. This can be a MW related question or some obscure fact which must be researched. On giving the correct answer, the npc gives the player an item. This item then identifies the player to the next npc which must be sought out and again a question answered. When all npc's have been found and the last question answered and the last item obtained the player returns the item to a designated GM/ npc. The 1st player back receives the reward. When I ran as a Guild event, I made 6 alts to hide and made players ask questions and receive answers via pm. I also had 1st,2nd and 3rd place winners. It seemed a popular event and lasted a few hours. Cost me a fortune in the mall for prizes, but was worth it.

3: Capture and rescue. The GM's make a party of 4 players and go into world at random and capture a player into thier team. Once captured the clock starts for 1 hour. The captured player must then be rescued by his fellow guild mates or friends. To rescue the player a party must battle the GM team and defeat it. Ok, a little twist. The captured player as part of the team can choose two option. 1 to do nothing and try help his rescuers, or fight on the side of the gm's. If a party beats the GM party then the player is deemed rescued and the opposing team receive a small reward. If however the captured player helps defeat his rescuers, then the GM's after the set period, may reward him alone instead. The battle must start before the clock runs out and then if no challenges have come the player is returned with no rewards and a new player is captured at random.
I like the aspect on this where the captured player has to choose whether to fight his/her friends or or fight on the side of the GM's.

4. Just added this after reading another post which gave me the idea. Costume event/rewards. How about adding some rewards to events or even quests that give you a costume change/upgrade. This would allow some player individualisation to thier respective characters.

Any way, just a couple of suggestions. Hope you get the idea.

Ohh nearly forgot.
We could have a ball event.
