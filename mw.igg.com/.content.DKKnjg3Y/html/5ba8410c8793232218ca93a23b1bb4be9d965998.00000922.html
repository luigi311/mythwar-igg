3D Credit Card Payment Bonus

Event duration: September 26th to September 30th 2008
Event details:
 Anyone who purchases more than 1000 points via 3D credit card will get points bonus.

1. In case that you purchase 1000 points via 3D credit card, you will be given 10% payback for points purchased during the event period.
2. In case that you purchase 2000 points via 3D credit card, you will be given 15% payback for points purchased during the event period.
Points given by us will be charged into payers' accounts on October 6th.
With more points purchased, you will get more payback points. It's now or never.
