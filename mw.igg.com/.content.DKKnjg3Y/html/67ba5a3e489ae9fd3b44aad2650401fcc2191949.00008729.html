Our Romantic Memory

 

Myth War Valentine's Day Event

Event Duration: From 31th January 2008 to 20th February 2008
All players are qualified to participate in the event

Event Contents:
Valentine's Day is the most important day of a year for lovers, so we will offer you the chance to do something special. You can make a short film with love as the topic, and submit your film link in our website. (The video should be original)

Event Rules:
1. Your works should be based on the in-game graphics based around the topic of love. Your character names can be displayed. The film should last from 1 min to 3 minutes. 
2. You can upload your works to http://www.youtube.com/  first and then post the link in the designated part of our website.
3. You can submit as many video clips as you want only if they are eligible.
4. We will verify all submitted video clips and announce the winner list 3 days after the end of the event.

Rewards:
The Most Creative Award (2 winners) Any Saint Pet
Requirements: Your work should be funny, creative and have good lines.
The Best Play Book (2 winners) Any Deity Pet
Requirements: Your video can demonstrate a complete romantic love story and make people touched.
The Most Popular Award (2 winners) Any Christmas Pet
Requirements: Your video should be popular with other players.
Excellent Award (5 winners) Any Titan Pet
Requirements:Clear graphics, unique contents and sincere feelings.

Notes:
1. If you are caught cheating or stealing other players’ works, your qualifications will be cancelled.
2. IGG reserves all the copyrights of the videos you submitted and all rights for final judgment of the event  

 

Cartoon Contest for Valentine's Day

We met unexpectedly. Not too late, not too early. And then we fell in love. It is God's blessing that led the two of us to meet, understand and fall in love with each other at first sight. It is a day when we must hold a special ceremony to celebrate our fantastic love. After many years have passed, when we look back, we can't help but to admit these amazing days will be cherished by us forever.

Register Website: MW Media Overview

Contents: Draw four pictures to describe your love story and how your relationship started and developed. If you are single, you can find a friend to cook up a love story.

Rules and regulations:
1. Players must draw four pictures to describe their love stories.
2. As for auto draw software, pictures saved in a computer are not allowed.
3. All work must be saved as name. jpg or gif
4. All work must be titled with "Cartoon for Valentines' Day"
5. All work must be original. Or qualifications will be cancelled.

Upload Works: From 31th January 2008 to 20th February 2008
Judgement: from 14th February 2008 to 21st February 2008

Prize
First place(1):     Oceanic Stone Pack+ Bloodstone Gem
Second place(1): Icy Crystal Pack+Dragon Eye
Third place(2):    Chocolate+ Dragon Bone
