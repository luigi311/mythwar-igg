Server integration Completed

Dear players,
After the maintenance, Missouri and California servers and the Achernar and Alps servers combined into 2 new servers already.
If there are character names that are the same after the integration, we will add a number to the end of the character names. (For instance, .1--.9999999) players please login first and then click this page to change your name.
All guilds will automatically disband after the integration. If the level 1+ guilds have more than 10000 contribution.Guild Leaders can create new guilds. Our officials will help the leaders keep previous guild contributions and compensate the guild fund before April. However, guilds which are level 1 and have less than 10000 guild contribution are unable to obtain this compensation. We apologize for the inconvenience.
Hope you will enjon the new server!


Mythwar Online Team, IGG
