 Values for base stat are fixed, that means the +1 on all stats you get on lvl up.

Hp/mp/atk are based on sta/int/str pools respectively. The more points you have, more hp/mp/atk you get. I got around 65 mp for each extra int point i put on my last lvl up, plus the base 340 mp or something from lvl up.

I'm not sure about agi, but I suppose spd works at the same way. Never tried humans, cant confirm that. There's a bonus on hp due to str, but its much, much lower than sta bonuses.
