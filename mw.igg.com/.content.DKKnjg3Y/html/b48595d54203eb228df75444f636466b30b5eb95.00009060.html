What’re pets?

Pets are very enjoyable participants of the Myth War World and also very useful for all characters in the game.

Every main character is allowed to keep a maximum of 20 pets. Pets will always be able to level up faster than the main characters. Therefore they are quite good assistants for characters.

Pets have skill abilities; therefore can supplement the master’s disadvantages during a battle.

So far in the MWW, categorized by features and origins, there already have about 100 kinds of pets, most of them have been found in game, and some are still not debut yet.

Attributes of the Pets

To really know the value of the pets, you should understand the meaning of their attributes, just read below:

Level: basic referenced measurement, increased by the accumulation of Exp.
Exp: gained through combat victories, every time reach the limitation shown on the right of the slash, get up 1 level and 4 extra Stats points, and the limitation will increase.

Loyalty: determines the pet’s obedience towards the player’s command. If a pet dies during battle its loyalty will decrease. If loyalty points become low the pet won’t be able to join in battle or may even run away from the players. To deal with that, you can purchase the stone of loyalty from the NPC and use it to increase the pet’s loyalty.

Health: decides the HP (health point) of a pet.

Mana: decides the MP (magic point) of a pet.

Attack: decides the AP (attack point) of a pet.

Speed: decides the SP (speed point) of a pet.

Stats points: When the pet grows up 1 level, it will get 4 points which can be freely assigned. Owner of the pet can add the stats to how he wants it to become; that is to say, player can choose different ways to raise the pet by deciding how its stats points are added.

Growth rate: the green number in the Exp box which is an important reference of a pet’s ability, it determines the increasing speed of several aspects of the pet, including Health, Mana, Attack and Speed.

For more details about the specific pets’ attribute, you can refer to the Specific Information section.
