Draw Your Olympics Ticket

　 Dear Promoters,

The Olympics are fast approaching. Do you want to be there to cheer on your favorite athletes? Are you anxious because you are unable to buy a ticket? Well, we here at IGG want to give something back to our hard working Promoters, so we will be presenting you with some Olympics tickets andOlympic Gold. Only IGG Promoters can their credits for an Olympic ticket or Olympic Gold. Come on and join us!

Event Content:
During the event, you can exchange 2,500 credits for a 2008 Beijing Olympics ticket or 1,500 credits for a 2008 Olympic Gold if you are an IGG promoter.

From now until July 20th, you can enter the Olympic ticket exchange page and exchange your credits for an Olympic present. There are only 5 tickets and 10 gold available so act now. Hurry up! We will announce detailed ticket information approx. 7 working days after the event ends (July 20th).

All gifts will be sent out after July 20th.

IGG reserves all the rights to the final judgment of this event.

IGG
