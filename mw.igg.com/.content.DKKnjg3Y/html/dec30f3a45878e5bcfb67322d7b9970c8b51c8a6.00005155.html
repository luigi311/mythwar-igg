April Fools' Day Quiz

April Fools' Day Quiz


Happy April Fools' Day! Don't worry. We would never play a trick on you. But we would like to give away some cool prizes to our players. So we are holding a quiz event in the forum to celebrate! We have posted 15 questions which players can reply to with their answers. The top five players with the most right answers will obtain 3 Drow Lord Rumor Scrolls as reward.

Notes: In order to avoid cheating, there will be a function to prevent participants' answers from being seen by other players (only the administrators are able to see everyone). To be fair, we will announce all participants' answers at the end of the event as well.

Format:
Copy to clipboard
Code:
[Hide=3000] Answer sheet [/hide]


If you are caught cheating, your qualifications will be cancelled.

Event Duration: From April 1st to April 3rd
Selection Duration: From April 4th to April 6th

True
False

France
Native American Indian

“Stole” her hair bow
Made her “Blind”

George Weasley
Dudley Dursley

True
False

Euthanasia
Cannabis

Donuts
Chips

Loki
Jotun
