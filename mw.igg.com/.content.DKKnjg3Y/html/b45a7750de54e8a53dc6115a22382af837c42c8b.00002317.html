Buzz Slogan Collection


During the Open Beta period, we are collecting Buzz Slogans for Myth War II Online to increase this game's popularity. If your slogans are selected by us, you will be greatly rewarded.

Collection Duration: September 25th to October 15th

Requirements:
 1. All buzz slogans must be on the theme of our game, go with the style of our game and aim to attract more players to play Myth War II Online.
2. All buzz slogans must be composed of 5 to 20 words max.
3. Slogans should rhyme, be concise and witty. Appealing and touching ones are preferred.
4. All participants must give explanations as to the meaning in your slogans.

How to enter:
 1. All participants must reply to this thread with your buzz slogans. Your contact details(the server and character ID), the buzz slogans you make up and relevant explanation must all be included in your post, Repeat posting is allowed.Editing posts is forbidden. Spamming will lose you your qualifications for the event.
2. If you want to give us your slogans via email, send all your contact details, the buzz slogans you make up and relevant explanation must all be included in your email with the subject called Myth War II Online: Buzz slogans. Send emails to the following address: mythwargm@igg.com.
3. If there is something similar or the same in two posts or emails, the first reply to our thread or the first email sent will be counted.
4. You are allowed to take part in our collection with the same ID many times. If you come up with any good ideas for slogans, post them to get a prize.

We will send out the winner's name after collection has closed and final verification is complete. Winning players must confirm to us all their required information via Live Support. Any resulting losses will be your responsibility if you fail to give us the correct contact information.

Judgments:
 We will pick out 10 excellent buzz slogans from October 16th to October 20th and start a new thread in our forum to vote for the best one from the 10 selected slogans.

Prizes:
 Players whose slogans are picked out by us as excellent buzz slogans will be rewarded with 400 silver ingots each.
The player whose slogan is voted to be the best one will be rewarded with a Dream Constellation Pet.

By the way,We invite you to join our buzz team so you can make money by introducing people to our games. You can make easy money .Click here
