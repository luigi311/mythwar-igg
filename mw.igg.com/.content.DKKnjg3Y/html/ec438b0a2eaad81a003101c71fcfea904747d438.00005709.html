 Well, it is just so easy to make every pet a tanker. Actually, a borg can have a lot of variety of pets to play with and they all make excellent sparring partners for both one on one combat or party combats:

1. Fraily pet. This is excellent to go along with a borg, especially if it is a high level frail pet. I guess it does not need much explaination.

2. A shield. If you are a strength based borg with low HP and lots of damage, you will one a pet that can be used to protect you. As such, pets with high melee resist, high speed(in the case of party combats) to take the damage away from your borg so that it can remain Enhanced and cause loads of damage to the enemy.

3. Enhance pet. You may want a good pet with speed higher than your Strength based borg to enhance your borg so that it can do physical damage.

4. Speed pet. Remember, when you cast Enhance, it goes to the pet/character with the highest strength and when you cast Protect, it overwrites Enhance and vise versa. As such, you will want your Strength pets and characters with low HP to be protected from spells by higher speed pets which will be receive the Protect spell whereas making sure that the Enhanced pets remained Enhanced.
