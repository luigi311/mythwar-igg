let me light my cig 1st....ok we shall begin our journey of information

u smith the items at demon square at craft shop area use tab open map and find it.

u need the item ( armor or helm or shoe or weapon ) + ore if correct lvl to forge

benefit of making items is to prodeuce higher lvl items and also forged items are blank and therefore u can personalise it with ur choice of resistance or essence

ores are as in the order of lvl ~ small iron &gt; medium iron &gt; large iron &gt; small copper &gt; medium copper &gt; large copper &gt; small gold &gt; medium gold &gt; large gold. they correspond to the lvl u wanna forge ie. armor +1 forge to +2 takes small iron, armor +9 forge to +10 takes large gold, etc etc etc.

u need 3 ores of the lower grade to make an ore of the grade u need.
example : u need large gold ore, so u need to try combine 3 medium gold ores to try produce a large gold ore. 3 small copper ore combines to 1 medium copper ore. etc etc etc. the purpose of combining them, refer to above answer.

u can apply gems to armor helmet and necklace, 3 is the maximum on an item. but u cannot imbue gems on items tat has existing resistance.
example : u have an armor +5 with +8 evil resistance ( only 1 entry ) hence u may add 2 more gems to this particular armor, totalling to 3 resistance. best is if u have a blank armor, whereby u may add 3 resistances of ur liking. u may not overide any resistance that is already present in any piece of equipment. so if u have a helmet with +8 flash +7 frailty +9 fire, u may not alter this piece anymore. essences however, u may overide with other essences. once a piece has been imbued with gem it may not have an essence in it, same a piece with essence in it u may not imbue it with a gem.

for weapons with attack on it ( +190 attack ) they are inherent and will not be changed if u imbue it with diamonds ( altered gems ). same a shoe with +80 speed even if u add gems or essence it will not loose that +80 speed.

stardust u may get thru lottery event and guild war. theres also perfect stardust sold in the item mall.

( pause lights 2nd cig )

armors = sta essence, helmets = int essence, shoe = agi essence, weapon = str essence. the quality of essence u get from disenchanting these items is based on the original items lvl. example : a +10 armor disenchanted will yield a +30 sta essence, a +9 shoe will yield a +27 agi essence etc etc etc.

u may not override an item that already has essence with a gem. but u may incrust it with a lvl 1 - lvl 5 gem using guild function.  go to guild hq with an item with essence ( armor, helm or necklace part ) and talk to the npc at the bottom right side of map, cant recall wats the npc name. it will show u how to.

u may overide essences. example : u have armor with +27 sta on it, u may enchant it with essence again and try to make the sta higher or u may change it into int essence if u like. the result is dependant on the quality and type of essence used, with a slight random bonus. ie. +30 sta essence may give u +31 or +30 or +29 or +28 depends on ur luck.

weapons can only be imbued with diamonds. u may not put essence on a weapon. diamonds are gems that are converted.

combo rate is one of the diamonds, i cant remember which, but it is here somewhere on forum. go search it.

there, i done my deed for the day
