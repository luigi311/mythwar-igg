r17 - m.cent. multi iv most of the times. sometimes melee. low hp. resist to all human skills. likely to be faster than everything besides full agi builds.

F human poison works pretty good on those m cents, you can also land pet poison on them somewhat.  Mage spells can also take them down, but somewhat slowly, you can also crit them, but they have some dodge.  You can't chaos stuff reliably though, past like the snakes in r13 really.  I wouldn't worry about making those high damage parties for spamming survival.  My party is 2 cents, 1 borg, 1 mage, 1 human and it takes me about an hour flat to run a r15.  If you want to go fast, just get a reliable party.  2 cents means less time chaosed/stuned/dead and more time to kill.  F humans do more damage than mages do as well on certain rounds.  As far as pets go, the only thing you'll be using really past r15 are physical resist pets, and speed tankers.

27# A A A Post at 2008-5-10 12:21 AM  Show author
Yeah.  I wanted a screenshot of my white title staff sergeant cent beating r20, lol.   We would have easily done it too if it weren't for the bugs.  

Hrmm, I'll give a few r15 tips to contribute some more.

First round with your human, stun 3 the borg.  After that, cast poison or frail on the enemy humans, depend on gender.  One trick some people use is to frail away all the mana on a human, and leave them for after the borg.  It only takes a few rounds, and pet fraility also works.  It's also a good idea to use a phys resist poison pet or 5 here.  Basically, poison every human(unless saving 1), and have your borg drain 4.  The stun on the borg also means you can poison 4 and hit 3(pet), drain 4 and hit max number, and so on.  The round is a lot easier when you don't have to worry about the borg draining you or killing you with physical.  If you poison and kill everything, you just have to count your crits on the borg when killing it, or use death.  It takes 23 crits to kill.  I usually have the whole team doing suicide crits until we hit 20, then only our physical immune mage finishes off the last 3(using crit neck/weapon).  Also, crit pets with sg egg work as well.  You'll also want to let the leader get his/her pet out at 20 crits.
