I) Hellion Quest
To start the Hellion Quest, visit Howling Beast Located at Tree Of Life Level 1(130,194). It is on the same area as the entrance to Tree Of Life Level 2.

Players over level 50 will receive 50000 experience points Mystical Fruit for their pets.
Players below level 50 will receive 48000 experience points for themselves.

Now read your quest log, it should tell you to head to bat beast.

Head to Revival Arena in Woodlingor and find Bat Beast(103,146).

Players over level 50 will receive 50000 experience points Mystical Fruit for their pets.
Players below level 50 will receive 120000 experience points for themselves.

Now read your quest log, it should tell you to head to head to flower witch.

Head to Flamingor, the map after Revive Arena and find Flower Witch(71,148).

Players over level 50 will receive 50000 experience points Mystical Fruit for their pets.
Players below level 50 will receive 240000 experience points for themselves.

Now read your quest log, it should tell you to head to Flame Beast.

Still staying at Flamingor, head towards the Devil's Gate entrance to find Flame Beast(136,454).

Players over level 50 will receive 100000 experience points Mystical Fruit for their pets.
Players below level 50 will receive 480000 experience points for themselves.

Now read your quest log, it should tell you to head to Cave Clayman.

Heading to Devil's gate, head towards the coordinates 247,269 to find Cave Clayman.

Players over level 50 will receive 600000 experience points Mystical Fruit for their pets.
Players below level 50 will receive 720000 experience points for themselves.

Hellion Quest is repeatable unlimited times.

II)Bounty

Level Recommendation: 60+

To obtain this quest, visit BloodReaper (160,200) at Tree of Life 3

Players will obtain 10000 experience points after the fight.

Read your quest log, it should tell you to fight Firetroy.
Head to Flamingor and fight Firetroy (68,357)

Players will obtain 20000 experience points after the fight.

Read your quest log, it should tell you to fight bonepoker.
Head to Devil's Gate and fight BonePoker(280,366)


Players will obtain 40000 experience points after the fight.

Read your quest log, it should tell you to fight eyebasher.
Head to Evil Lair 2 and fight EyeBasher (65,110)

Players will obtain 100000 experience points after the fight.

Read your quest log, it should tell you to fight Fistscarer.
Head to Sunset Plain and fight Fistscarer (99,195)

Players will obtain 1.2m experience points after the fight.

III) DrowCrusher
Level Requirement: Level 50 for obtaining Skill III and IV

To obtain this quest for gaining Skill III and IV, first head to Woodlingor Skill Center and talk to your race guide.
Head to Demon Square and find DrowCrusher at 151,662.
He'll present you a list like the following asking for the items:

You're not required to bring him the whole list of items, but the item of your race only.
Male Human - Plate of Courage +5
Female Human - Plate of Oceanic +5
Male Centaur - Shoe of Angelic +5
Female Centaur - Shoe of Blessing +5
Male Mage - Robe of Shadow +5
Female Mage - Helm of Snow +5
Male Borg - Fist of Haze +5
Female Borg - Fist of Plague +5

The items can be found at Demon Square Armory.

Using the Give Function (ALT+G) will trigger a fight will Drowcrusher for the item he requested.
Note: DrowCrusher quest is not meant to be solo, it is strongly advised you have a party of borg for drain, human for status change, mage for damaging, and centaur for healing to complete this quest. Additional experience reward is 300k.

IV) Mokka Quest
To start this quest, head to Revive Arena (24,89) and talk to Disciple.
He'll tell you he lost the Sword of the Stars and ask you to help him retrieve it.
Accept to help him and fight around Revive Arena until you get the Sword of the Stars
After u obtain Sword of the Stars, use the give function (ALT+G), and give Sword of the Stars to Mokka.
You'll be rewarded 50 skill experience points for your lowest skill inside your skill list.

V) Defender Quest
Level Requirement / Target : 50-60
To start this quest, head to Woodlingor Elder Zone. The Defender(343,79) is located in front of the entrance.
Accept to help him to find the demon that's hiding in one of the merchant.
Defeat the demon and gain additional experience points.
Note: Players below level 50 and above 60 will not obtain any quest experience points.

Level Requirement / Target: 60-70
To start this quest, head to Blython City Hall. The Defender (307,381) is located in front of the entrance.
Accept to help him and find the demon that's hiding in one of the merchant.
Defeat the demon and gain additional experience points.
Note: Players below level 60 and above level 70 will not obtain any quest experience points.

VI) Golden Pet Keeper Quest

To start this quest, head to Woodlingor Bar (131,555) and talk to Igor.
He'll ask you to help find 3 Dragon tooth dagger and give to Nelson in Blython.
Head to Tree of Life 3 and fight until you have gathered up 3 Dragon tooth dagger

Go to Nelson (200,314) near Blython Trading Zone and use the give function (ALT+G) and give him the 3 Dragon Tooth dagger
He'll award you with 600000 experience points mystical fruit for your pet.
He'll then ask you to help him find the 3 lost Leather boots for Scott
Head towards Devil's Gate and fight until you've gathered 3 Leather boots

Visit Scott (329,189) near Desert City Pet shop and use the give function (ALT+G) and give him the 3 Leather boots.
He'll then reward you with a 1.2million experience points mystical fruit for your pet.

VII) Magical Crystal Quest

There is no starter NPC on this quest.
Head towards Demon Square Pet shop and fight Spirits
They're monsters that are clickable like NPC like the following:



All Spirits have a chance of dropping 9 different kinds of Pet eggs

Evil Lair 1 will drop Magical Crystal+1 for level 30+ pets to have their first time resistance forged.

Evil Lair 2 will drop Magical Crystal+2 for level 50+ pets to have their resistance forged for their second time.

Evil Lair 3 will drop Magical Crystal+3 for level 70+ pets to have their resistance forged for their third time.

To use the magical stones, visit Blython City Hall(312,437) in front of the fountion area.
Three NPC will be standing there with its own list, pick the one that has the resistance you want your pet to include.
A random resistance from the list will be chosen from that specific NPC list of resistance he can forge your pet into.
Choose yes if you wish to have that resistance forged into your pet, and no to pick again.
NOTE: The crystal will be discarded if you say no to the resistance the NPC gives you

IMPORTANT: Please decide wisely which resistance you want your pet to have as you can only forge once with each crystal, which means 3 times before your pet reborns. And another two times after your pet reborns.

VIII) Seal of Myth Quest

Level Requirement / Limitation : 70-90

To start this quest, visit Enderolth(225,192) inside Blython Bar.
Buy a scroll from him for 500 gold.
The scroll will look like this:

Head to the coordinates in your quest log to use your sealed scroll. An example below:

Press use on the scroll and beat the monsters
Experience points will be rewarded after the battle. The harder and further the scroll is, the more experience points you'll gain.
The quest consists of 10 rounds and can be restarted at any round you wish.
Head back to Enderolth and buy another scroll for 500 gold. Every round destination will get further and more challenging.

IX) Fire Defender Quest

Level Requirement: level 70+

To start this quest, visit Fire Defender(32,112) in Blython near Sunset Plain entrance.
The coordinates of the 4 bosses are random, so you must find them around the map.
Defeating each monster will be a piece of armor will pre set resistance.
Head down to Ashes pit 3 and find Sword Demon. Defeating him will receive a random weapon between +7-10 on all races.
Next find Blue Lich. Deafeating him will receive a random Helmet between +7-10 on all races.
Next find Ogre. Defeating him will receive a random Body Armor/ Robe between +7-10 on all races.
Next find Fire Giant. Defeating him will receive a random Shoe between +7-10 on all races.

X) Existent Quest

Level Recommendation: 30+
This quest consists of 20 rounds and is meant for players who needed some challenge to test their characters.
Every round of the quest will get harder, but gold will be awarded for each round that is completed.
Killer Bee and Phoenix eggs can be obtained from this quest.

To start this quest, visit Existent (293,348) in Sunset Plains.

XII) Stonesmith Quest
Function: This quest is intended for players level 10-30. Players that reach level 10 can go to
Woodlingor (456,157) to look for the Stonesmith. Depending on your level, the Stonesmith will
ask you to search for 6 different kinds of magical ingredients. You only need to find a certain
number of the same item that he asks and not all items. These items will drop from monsters in that map.
Mystical Fruits can be found at Tree of Life 3.
Molten Rocks can be found at Flamingor.
Carcass can be found at Sunset Plains.
Animal Scales can be found at Demon Lair.
Lava Crystals can be found at Devil's Gate and Lost Items can be found at Outcast City.
Players need only go to these maps to fight monsters and the items have a chance of dropping. Collect

these items and give them to the Stonesmith to receive your experience points.

Double Experience
Players will have 14 hours of double experience time every week. Players can collect it at Blython's
Skill Center. Players may only choose to use 1,2 or 4 hours. You may redeem it again after your time
has finished to up a maximum of 14 hours per week. All monsters fought with double exp will grant you,
well double experience :)
