Mage Country

The only dream I had which had to do with
Myth War, was about the
promised 'Mage Country'.
I was there with my reborned mage, in an
old-fashioned-village standing right beside
a shop which sold mage-accesoirs.
All players around me were busy and sold stuff.
Right over me was the entrance of the 'Bastion of cold dreams',
the main dungeon of Mage Country.
I was in M.C to wait for friends who helped me to fight
'Necrofear', the dungeonboss who gives out a Soulcrystal,
the item you need for the second rebirth.
I just get bored while waiting and wanted to pwn a
noob-party with my Ultima IV-skill when I awoke

what we learn from this:
Never drink too much alcohol at a party,
it ruins ur health ;D

Good wishes to all,
