Board Rules

Introduction
         
Welcome to the MythWars forums. This is a short guide to help maintain an organized and friendly forum. Please follow these rules as outlined below.


Zones and Threads

  • When creating a new thread, consider carefully the zone you post it in.
  • Use the search function to find previous topics which may have been posted, before posting yours.
  • Read the stickies in the zones to make sure your information isn't within one of these, and that there aren't rules which extend these.
  • Post any board suggestions into the Board Suggestions at: <url here>
  • Posting of Chat Transcripts and PM's is prohibited. Any topics which contain either will either be deleted or edited beyond recognition.


Posts

  • Do not make pointless posts. Wherever possible, consider one properly constructed sentence as a bare minimum. (except where exceptions are allowed)
  • Trolling, Flamming and Spamming are all discouraged and will be dealt with accordingly.
  • Avoid posting offensive text, images, videos and links.
  • Any discussion regarding 3rd party programs, beyond legitimate reason, will be removed.
  • Nobody is interested in your disagreements with members. If you have an issue with any member, PM either a moderator or an Administrator, depending on the severity of the situation.


Miscellaneous

  • All signatures ideally should not exceed 500 pixels wide and 300 pixels high. Excessively oversized signatures will be dealt with accordingly.
  • If you are unable to play, but have done so in the past, please check the website in case there is information available before posting on the forum. Check there isn't already a topic before posting on the forum about it.
  • Criticism is acceptable when done in an acceptable manor. Avoid poor language and above all, accept the opinion of those who do not agree with your criticism.


Discretion and Defiance

  • The ultimate fate of a post or topic is down to the discretion of the moderator who sees it first.
  • In cases where a topic is repeatedly sabotaged, the topic will be locked for a period of time, no longer than 7 days - unless the situation warrants a longer or permanent intervention
  • Feel free to "report" any posts you see as unfit, when doing so, include a reason why you have reported the post - Please do not use the reporting function to draw attention to any of your event posts.


Disclaimer

The messages express the views of the author of the message, not necessarily the views of this forum. Check back often to ensure that you are up-to-date, since ignorance of the rules will not be accepted as a valid explanation for breaking them.
