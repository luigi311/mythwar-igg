If it comes to that, you can get a 3rd party voice chat for your group. That is, assuming that you all have headphones the party leader could say, "Target back row, middle," or "Target front row, second column." Or you can even type it if you do not have chat. If you stick to similar patterns with your party, and you all get it down, such as always starting from the back and working your way up. Suggestion goes here !

I didn't want to start a new thread for a similar topic, so here it goes. I think it would be a good idea to see a customizable quick bar, which is located right above the auto fight option. That way, players can make their own quick notes. As an example, my reply above. A party leader, for example, can input into his quick bars. "Front row" and "Back row" and "First column" and click one, then the other, and then post, eliminating the need to type, and, hopefully, speeding up the battle for reaction times.
A different idea, but similar, would be still allowing customization, but allowing players to add or reduce the amount of quick notes. They can remove them entirely, or add a few more, all via the menu.
