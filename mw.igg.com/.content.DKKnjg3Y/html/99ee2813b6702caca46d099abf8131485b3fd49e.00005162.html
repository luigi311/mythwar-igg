You could also make an item that can allow you to remove the resistance added to a particular Armor, that could help players who want to add better resistances to a rare item like adding +13x3 Speed Diamonds to +12 or +13 Shoes which had +12x3 Dodge added to them... another nice item would be one with which you could reset a pet's stats without affecting its growth, it could be a little more expensive than Stone of Moon. Also, to counter the effects of this Gem, an extremely rare item could be introduced which has a low chance to negate the effects of the Gem on your opponent. For eg: if a GoD(Gem of Dreams) gives +50 Frailty res to your target, this item which I'm talking about could turn it into -50 Frail res instead. The chance of this happening should be low though, duels will be more fun and unpredictable.
