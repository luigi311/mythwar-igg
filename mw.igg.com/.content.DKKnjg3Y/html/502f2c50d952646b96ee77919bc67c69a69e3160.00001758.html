My Advice for the Mythwar Open Beta

Many events will be kicked off during the Mythwar 2.0 Open Beta. Now every player taking part in the closed beta will have a chance to give us their advice for what kinds of events should be held during the Open Beta
Event duration:
At the period of the Closed Beta

 Rules:
1. Post replies in our forum. Trolling is forbidden, or your qualifications will be cancelled.
2. Your ideas or proposals must be original
3. If posts from different forum users put forward the same proposal, the first post will be adopted. Don't repeat posting the same proposals. Editing posts is forbidden.
4. You can post replies to different proposals as many times as you'd like with the same ID. If you have good ideas, we will give you rewards.
5. We will release winners' usernames in our forum after this event comes to an end. Winners must send a private message to the appointed board manager to leave their account and contact information. We are not responsible for any losses resulting from incorrect contact information.
6. We reserve the right to final interpretation.

 Rewards:
Top five players will be given 500 silver ingots.
Players ranked from No.6 to No.10 will be given 300 silver ingots for each.
