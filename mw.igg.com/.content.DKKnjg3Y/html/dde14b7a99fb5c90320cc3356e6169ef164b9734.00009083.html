Xmas Carnival

Starts from Dec 24th until Dec 26th

During the activity time, you need to change your own appearances into Santa Claus, Santa Princess, Snow Bear or Snow Lady by talking with the NPC Santa Princess and then you will be given quests. After finishing the quests, you will be granted with lots of nice gifts.

Rewards:
During the Xmas activities, many amazing gifts will be given out, and among them there are Enjoyment Card, Blessing Card, Santa Hat, Santa Stocking, Santa Bell and Santa Candy.
