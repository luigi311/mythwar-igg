You Decide This...

Ok  just so to let you all know, It's not that my depression coming from losing 175k in chips on PokerRPG. Just to let you know on that. It's a completely different story.

Andyway... I'm loggeed on at this time, and I have been thinking. Just... everything on MythWar just isn't feeling right to me. Nothing at all...

I remember when I started this game, I was on at least once a day, and on for a good few hours. Now... I still come on once a day, but probably for not even an hour, unless I am reaaaallllllllllly bored. Even then, I rarely do stuff on MythWar.

So, you probably know where I am going with this. However, I really don't want to give up and quit when I am close to reborn... if level 91 is close... Though, my pain comes from a few reasons...

The number one reason is that I just don't feel like my friends, or my guild members were helping me. Just so I don't point fingers, I will not release names of my friends, nor will I announce what guild I was in. It was only a few days ago I left my guild, and now I was just by myself. I don't know how many times I've solo'ed round 6 survival. Not a lot, but a handful of times.

I'm not asking for friends. No, I'm not trying to be greedy, nor trying to get your attension. I don't even care if anyone posts on here. Though, I do want a suggestion, and a path. So, I have decided on four paths to take...

Path 1: Just take a break from the game. Maybe it's that I am getting over-worked up. Maybe I just need to calm myself down and just do something else for a little bit.

Path 2: Stay on the game, but work alone. I would not ask for any help, just go for reborn by myself. The number one way would probably be crabs, and take the exp rather than the gold.

Path 3: Stay on the game, and possibly try to find help. This might be the toughest idea. I am someone, and I will admit, someone who isn't really social 99.9% of the time. Though, with some support, I am willing to try.

I am also including a neutral vote. I understand if you do not know which path I should take, but use this vote fairly. Also, I have italicized (and I probably spelled that word wrong) my "vote" on what I should do. However, you do not have to agree with me. Go on what you feel is right for me.

So... I am leaving fate in your hands.

P.S. Please vote fairly. Your vote decides my next action.
You may know me as Inferno. I may be human, but that doesnt mean I can burn you. f18 Name: Inferno Sever: Cali Race: Male Human Level: 91

Quote:
You can only be satisfied with your own choice.
sounds good. but there are more and more psychological researches pointing out the opposite. there is this one conducted in photography class where students had to choose one of the two film-developed pictures they took at the end of the class, and give the other one away, never seen it again. half of the students has freedom to choose which picture to keep. the rest of the students don't; one picture is just simply taken from them randomly. turns out the latter is much happier on average. people tend to be happy if they don't have to make a choice. when you are forced to make a choice, we tend to look at and regret the decision, more or less regardless of the outcome (some outcome are hard to realize/quantify anyway)
