Event Contents?
Our GM will appear in Icicle Castle (1293,588) and wait for players. Players can find the GM and exchange some Christmas Lollipops (obtained from turkeys) for the Reindeer Summoning Scroll.

1. Our GM will appear in Icicle Castle (1293,588);
2. Players can find the GM and exchange 99 Christmas Lollipops (obtained from turkeys) for 1 Reindeer Summoning Scroll;

4. IGG reserves all rights for final judgment in the activity.
