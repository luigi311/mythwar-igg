15# A A A Post at 2008-7-24 07:42 AM  Show author
Ok, I just wanna try to help make a few things clear.

1. It's Gold Ingot items that can be traded, not Silver. I know this because I have seen people selling Gold Ingot exclusive items like Chamberlaine Invites, Magical Stones, Perfect Stardusts, and the Saint SSPs.

2. Both of the regular Sources of Life/Magic are obtainable with Silver Ingots.

3. I have bought Gold Ingots at the NPC in Blython before. twice, infact. You have to either place an order yourself, or wait for someone to sell theirs and buy from them. The only problem is that people severely overprice their Ingots. eg: they want 3.5 Mil for 1 Unit (100 Ingots) That just makes it even harder for us non-Item Mall Point buyers to use the Item Mall because we're getting ripped off. Here's my idea to improve the Gold Ingot NPC. There needs to be a reasonable limit on how much Gold you can charge for a Unit. That way, we'll still get our Ingots, and the sellers will still get a fair amount of Gold.

That's just my opinion. Hope it helps.
