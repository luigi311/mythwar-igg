Item Mall User Appreciation 2007

Event Contents:
Besides obtaining points, players can also be appointed rewards (listed as below) if their total consumption reaches the following requirements on 2007 (Midnight Jan 1stâ€”23:59 Dec 31st).

Rewards: Aurora Lion, Chocolate, Devil Egg, Armor+13 (according to character race and sex), AOD (Diadem of Serenity+3)

1 The players whose total consumption reaches 10000+ $ can get 1200 integrals and 5 appointed rewards.
2 The players whose total consumption is from 7000 $ to 9999 $ can get 1000 integrals and 4 appointed rewards.
3 The players whose total consumption is from 5000 $ to 6999 $ can get 800 integrals and 3 appointed rewards.
4 The players whose total consumption is from 3000 $ to 4999 $ can get 600 integrals and 2 appointed rewards.
5 The players whose total consumption is from 1000 $ to 2999 $ can get 400 integrals and 1 appointed rewards.

Event Rules:You can get integrals by knocking eggs over after EST Jan 1, 2008. To receive the appointed official rewards, you can contact our GMs and state what kind of rewards you want after EST Jan 5, 2008.The details will be posted on official website (mw.igg.com) on EST Jan 5, 2008. Don't miss out on this chance.

Event Duration:Time for exchanging integrals: The day after EST Jan 1, 2008. You can log into our official website on EST Jan 5, 2008 to find out when you can obtain the appointed rewards.

All players who meet requirements can participate in the activity.





Mythwar Online Team, IGG
