Exterminators Unite!

We have been fixing the bugs and flaws since the Closed Beta, and many of them have been fixed. However you should understand that operating a good online game is a work in progress, it takes time to make a game as perfect as possible. Our game is still in the Open Beta phase,so many flaws still need to be fixed. We want you to help us create a wonderful game world by finding the bugs and flaws in the game. Of course, who could forget about the great rewards available for the top bug hunters!  

Event Duration: During the Open Beta

 To Participate:
Post the bugs you find under the "Exterminators Unite!" post.
 Event Rules:
 1. Posts should be titled "bug" otherwise it won't count.
2. Describe the bugs you submit as specifically as possible. For instance, offer the related maps, coordinates, and quests.  If the bug is related to your computer, offer your computers configuration and the Operating System you are using. If the details about the bugs are not clear, your post may not count.   
3. If there are duplicates, the one sent first will count.
4. Submit as many bugs as you want as long as they are new bugs.
5. Submit during the Open Beta, and stay tuned for the event end time.
6. We will mark the bugs you submit according to their quantity and value. The top 20 players who get the highest marks will obtain the rewards.
7. We will announce the winners' forum nicknames after the event, winners should PM the appointed mods in our forum and offer their game account. Otherwise you may not receive our rewards, and we won't bear the responsibility.  
8. IGG reserves the right to final judgment of this event.
Event Rewards:
 1 available bug = 1 Tri-experience Roll
