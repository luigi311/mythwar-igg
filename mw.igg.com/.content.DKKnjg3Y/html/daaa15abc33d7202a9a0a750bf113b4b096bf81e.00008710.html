Mythwar Summer Holiday Carnival

 

Dear Players,

At this summer holidays, we have a series of events, you are interested in it? Ok, read the details and do not miss it. ^^

About this Quest

It is said that thousands of years ago there was a giant Elder Dragonkin, it was no threat to the humans at first. However, a greedy man attempted to obtain its fur and the shinning trinket on its forehead and thus disturbed it by repeatedly attacking. They tried to kill this kind giant Elder Dragonkin.

Finally one day the giant became evil, as if it had been cursed by the devil and became violent and deadly. It invaded the village and killed all of the men. Nobody survived this brutal attack.

The elders in the Myth War World couldn't kill the giant Dragon Beast, either. What they could do was to barricade it in a cage permanently and send ten warriors to stand constant vigil there. Unfortunately, due to carelessness, five of the warriors were swallowed by the Dragon Beast. The other five had to thoroughly bury the cage and made many traps to prevent the Dragon Beast from escaping its prison.

The holy weapons which were taken along by the warriors were all made by a mysterious blacksmith. Now that five of warriors have been swallowed by the monster, their holy weapons still lay inside the belly of the beast. The rest of the warriors didn’t have the energy to take back the weapons because they had been exhausted. Now this great task has been handed down to us.

The time arrangement:  From August 24th to September 15th

Event in the afternoon: 3:00 PM----5:00 PM

Event in the evening: 8:00 PM----10:00 PM

Steps:

 Woodlingor: you can accept the quest from the corresponding NPC in Woodlingor and be teleported to the 1st layer of Eternal Dungeon.

The First Layer（Ruined Gateway）: Quiz Contest

Guide: You should find NPC and answer his questions. You won't be teleported to the next layer until the answers are answered correctly.

The Second Layer（Foggy Jail）: Vision Contest

Guide: The guard NPC will happen to come out somewhere at this map. You will access to the nest layer after finding the NPC.

The Third Layer（Sealed Jail）: Search for the key

Guide: You have to fight with the field monster on this layer. Defeat it and submit the items and key obtained from the monster to the NPC, then you can go to the next layer.

The Fourth Layer（Eternal Well）: the Bitter Choice

Guide: You are required to find one of the chests which are scattered on the layer. The real chest is the one that contains the Passport Book which will teleport you to the fifth layer while the fake chests contain attractive rewards. It is really a bitter choice.

The Fifth Layer（Decisive Room）: PVP

Guide: You are required to PK with other players and only by defeating one player can you go to the next layer. If you have failed, you will return to winglingor to revive and be honorably rewarded. (To ensure your smooth playing, please do close the challenge window before entering this Layer.) 

The Sixth Layer（Rotten Pool）: Challenge Elder Dragonkin

Guide: You will compete with the ultimate Boss on this layer. After victory you can finish this quest and be rewarded and teleported back to Winglingor. If you failed, you also finish the quest and return back to winglingor and will obtain some honorable rewards.

Rules：
 Players can only pk freely with the others in the Eternal Well. There is no reward for the winners in PK while the losers have to return to Winglingor.
 No teleport rolls or items used for teleport are allowed to be used in this event.
 The object of the event is for players of level 20+
 You have to rest for three minutes before you join in the event again, whether you have finished the quest or been sent out due to failure in the PK area.
 Top 1 team  which killed the Elder Dragon fastest will be selected automatically by the system at the end of the event everyday. The winners will be rewarded with rich rewards and better odds to win Constellation Pets and Armor of Dream.
