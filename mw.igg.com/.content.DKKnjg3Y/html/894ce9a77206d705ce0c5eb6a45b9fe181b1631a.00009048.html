How to get pets?

There are several means to get pets:

Beginner
As a beginner, you start from Sky Passage, and you can get the quest from the Pet Keeper. After you finish it, he will give you an egg of pet. When you arrive level 15, the Pet Keeper will give you another egg of pet without any quest.

Pet Lair
You can go to the Pet Lair which is in Demon City or in Desert City. There are three kinds of Spirit in the Pet Lairs, find them and beat them. You will have opportunity to get the egg of pet.

Guild Quest
When you join a guild, you could go to the center of your Guild. You can see Harael in the center of your Guild, after finishing the quest from Harael. He will give you an egg of pet.

Market
Go to the market places; you could buy eggs of pets or just pets directly from other players.

Activities
There are many kinds of interesting activities held in the MWW every once in a while, and by taking part in them, you will have the chance to get pets.

Item Mall
Myth War Item Mall is also a direct source for you to get pets, you can click here to visit it.
