ok this is a very rough example. its not exact but it's the general idea

Example.
if str is 3.5 stars then every 1 stat u add in str it will grow by 3.5 multiplier
if it is 4.0 stars then every stat u add in str it will grow by 4.0 multiplier

also there are other factor like base growth rate, initial stat etc. the real formula nobody knows, but when u see the stars u get a general idea of how much it will grow if u add on that stat

this isnt exact but hope u get the general idea.
