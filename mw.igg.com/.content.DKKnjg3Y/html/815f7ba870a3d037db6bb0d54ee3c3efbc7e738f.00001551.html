Quote:
Fog spirits = Mage death if you dont pay attention .
Definitely. Be careful. They don't always reflect (repel) but when many of them happened to do at the same time, it's hard. Be specially wary with fog spirit bosses.  3-sta/1-int reborned cent multi iv 10-11 can kill fogs, and go before mage. It's good to have one in bone leveling.. if you can find. It's quite standard built cent but a bit hard to find. Otherwise 2 cents multi ii/iv work too. If all else fail, mages be careful not to kill all and die at the same time.

Cents can also switch to heal when enemies are mostly mud. So can run the fights nonstop. Carry a lot of cheap mp pots, and right click to fill mp to full while walking around.
