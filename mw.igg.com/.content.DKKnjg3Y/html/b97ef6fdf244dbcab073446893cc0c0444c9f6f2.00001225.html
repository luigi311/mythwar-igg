fadedpie's Leveling Guide LVLS 1-104

I have added a bit more specific information for those who got confused

Hi this is Osayo of missouri server. From what i know so far this is best way to level up. I dotn know about on archernar since there are reborns but this is what ive found so far.

lvl 1-6 - space tunnel
~Go farther in as your level gets closer to 6

lvl 6-9 - TOL 1 or revive arena
~preferrably revive for the mokkas

lvl 9-19 - sunset plains
~9-13 near entrance to blython
~13-16 mid way to badlands from blython
~16-19 near badlands entrance

lvl 19-32 - various spots in badlands
~19-24 near entrance from sunset
~24-27 north a bit from sunset entrance but not yet at hill/cliff
~27-32 near entrance to bone desert

lvl 32-50 - bone desert
~32-36 near entrance from badlands
~36-38 head north until wolves are replace by fog spirits
~38-50 head all the way north and fight mudrapers + spirits

lvl 50-55 - TOL 5 or wood defender*
~TOL is faster if you have double exp
~human tip for defender: snakes and wolves=hypno, lizards and spiders=chaos

lvl 55-60 - south west flame ruins (eye kickers and cyclopes) or wood defender*
~flame ruins is faster if you have double exp
~human tip for defender: snakes and wolves=hypno, lizards and spiders=chaos

lvl 60-70 - blython defender or ashes pit(defender is MUCH faster but requires full 60+ party)
~human tip for defender: snakes and wolves=hypno, lizards and spiders=chaos

lvl 70-91 - seal quest
~beware of belkers as they cast flash IV and hit for 12k dmg to multiple targets.
~an enhanced black warrior can do 33k on regular hit, be sure to dispatch them quickly.

lvl 91- 104 - worn diety quest or drow lord*
~See below for drowlord strategy-  thanks to maxfli  

Also at a certain point i guess guild quests are alright, i myself have never gotten to great exp from them.

and obviously you will train faster in a full party with double exp.*

Thats the info ive managed to gather so far, have fun and feel free to submit any corrections  

*submitted corrections

Drow Lord quest :

The mobs
Father Drow : vulnerable to Flash
Black Dragon : vulnerable to Fire/Ice
Winter Dragon : vulnerable to Flash/Evil
Wild Lizard : vulnerable to Fire/Ice
Swamp Lizards x3 : vulnerable to Fire/Ice
Poison Lizard* ( forgot mob name, looks like snake demon ) : vulnerable to Flash/Evil


In order of turn
Poison Lizard* : casts poison IV
Wild Lizard : casts Drain IV
Swamp Lizards : melee attack
Father Drow : casts Flash/Evil IV
Black Dragon : casts chaos IV
Winter Dragon : casts multishot IV

Chars
Mage : shoot mobs, in correct order
Borgs : drain away
Cents : heal, purge chaos
Humans : Chaos or hypno (or poison if u wanna do fast)

Pets
SnowBear,Santa,Snowie, Speed tanker pets, any tanker pets.
