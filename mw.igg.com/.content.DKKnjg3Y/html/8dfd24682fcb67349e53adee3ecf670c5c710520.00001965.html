Chill Out With MythWar II

Tired of the scorching weather and afraid of the pee-filled public pools? Well, crank up the A/C because IGG is starting the MythWar II Open Beta just in time to help you out! We've been working hard and are proud to announce it is scheduled to start at  10:00PM, June 26th，EDT GM-4.
As the development and improvement of MythWar II continues, we will hold various events during the open beta to reward our players for their commitment to the new version. At that time, our item mall will be open to players. And don't worry, all players taking part in our open beta will not lose their character information when it's finished.

Client download:  http://mw2.igg.com/download/client.php
To register:          http://www.igg.com/member/mw2_reg.php
To activate:         http://www.igg.com/member/actigame.php

Myth War Online Team,
