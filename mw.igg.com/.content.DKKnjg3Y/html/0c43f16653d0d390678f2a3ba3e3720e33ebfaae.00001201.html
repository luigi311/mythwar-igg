13# A A A Post at 2007-5-12 02:03 AM  Show author
There are many reasons why a leader reject a player from any party:

1. He/she already have a regular party. A lot of the parties have prearranged members who regularly seal or do other quests together. This party will more often than not reject other people even when they are more powerful or have more powerful skills and pets.

2. They simply do not like you. As an example, you used to be in one regular party and one day you decide you kick one of your regular party member in favour of a more powerful new friend. Well, next time you may never be invited again.

3. Simply irritating. Nobody like a party where members are irritating. When a quest go wrong and everybody get a free tele, more often than not, people start pointing fingers. People like players they can work together with and have fun with.

4. You are simply not good enough. Tell me something, will you accept a white ranked lvl 70 centaur to your seal party which have not even got his skill III and IV and heal II is at level 3? Some parties can afford to carry, but not all. Even if the party is capable of carrying, it is their choice whether or not to carry as frankly, you will hardly contribute.

5. They have too many in their party which is performing the role that you are. Try sealing with 5 centaurs which can only heal. It will take like an hour to finish 1 round of mobs.
