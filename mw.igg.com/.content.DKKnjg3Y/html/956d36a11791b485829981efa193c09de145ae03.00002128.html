Promoting Myth War II Online to Win Big Rewards

Promoting Myth War II Online to Win Big Rewards



It has been 2 week since the Open Beta started, and our players have been very busy exploring the game world.  In order to enable more players to experience the festive atmosphere in the game, we will be holding an event to obtain big rewards with ease.   


Event Duration:  July 12th to July 31st (EDT GMT-4)
Selection Duration: We will check at the at 10:00 pm (EDT GMT-4) every day and give out rewards.


All players are welcome to participate in the event.


Event Content:

Players should post in forums (other than IGG's) or blogs to promote Myth War II Online, and then post the link in thePromotion Zone

Notes:
1. The promotion posts must contain the Myth War II website address (http://mw2.igg.com) and any Myth War II pictures.
2. The promotion posts should be posted within the event duration.
3. The promotion posts should comply with the corresponding the rules of forum and blog.
4. Post the link to your promotion post with your character's name and the character’s ID at the end of the post title in the promotion section.

5.You can also join our Promotion System (http://rich.igg.com/) and show your Promotion link at the  post to win added Promotion rewards


Event Rewards:
1. Officials will randomly select 5000lucky participants (who meet the requirements), and each one of them will obtain 100 Silver Ingots)


Special Bonus:
The officials will select 2 extra lucky participants.
Both of them will obtain an armor of dream +1.   

PS:
1.The more you post, the higher your chance to obtain the rewards. Participants can obtain the rewards repeatedly.
2. The number of lucky participants we will pick also depends on how many participants are qualified after the event.
3. Participants caught cheating will be forced to quit the event. If your post violates the forum's rules, it won't count.
