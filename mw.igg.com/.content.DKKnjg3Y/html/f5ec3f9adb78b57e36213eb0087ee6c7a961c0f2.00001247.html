Oh, no no. I didn't scan anything in. I did it 100% on the computer.
I have a tablet (which is basically a mouse that is shaped like a pen + a pad which makes it easier to draw than with a mouse), so what I did was I roughed out an outline of the Chinchillas in Open Canvas as you can see here:



And with that, I made a new layer for the grassy Woodlingor background and used the rough outline as a sort of guideline as I digitally watercoloured over the grass. Mostly it was just... making balls of white and then detailing them =P After I had the main image, I did all the card designing and text in Photoshop.
