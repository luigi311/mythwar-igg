Hurricane Guide



Other rewards are the titel and exp for ur char, as well as ur pet

1.) Hurricanes spawn at exact timing. within this timing and the next, if someone whack say hurricane 1 and win, hurricane 1 will disappear til the next spawn time just like spirits.

2.) U cant do any hurricane u like. Have to start from H1, then H2, then H3, no skipping of any, but when you defeat (for example: hurricane 6, you can do 1,2,3,4,5,6 again and again (and again), but only when you lead the party.

3.) H1 to H4 is basically "weak" (for decent party, not asking u to solo at lvl 10). H5 onwards have 10 mobs each, where u start to see freaks like 3 drainers in 1 team, heal iv that heals 266k to each target, and superb human almost immune to chaos etc.

4.) Hurricane mobs are not normal mobs that die and vanish. They are like normal characters, when they die, they leave a cross tomb, and if the healer in hurricane party cast heal, these mobs are back alive and kicking.

5.) Only party leader gets the title for defeating H5, H10 and H14. Title disappear after you reborn, that means you have to do it again

6.) Gem drop randomly, anyone(not everyone) in party may get. Not like spirits where 1 egg drop all got the egg.
