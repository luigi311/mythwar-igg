How pet's skill works

Since not many people know how pet's skill works, I will write a guide on how it works, how much target it will affect and which target it will affect. Let's go about this one by one:

Poison

It is everybody's favourite skill, ain't it? Poison attack enemy with the highest maximum HP together with the enemy that is targeted. How much HP the enemy have left does not affect it being a target. The number of enemies that are affected are:

Poison I one target
Poison II two target
Poison III one target
Poison IV three target

Say there are 6 enemies.
A 60k HP
B 59k HP
C 58k HP
D 57k HP
E 56k HP
F 55k HP

If you aim Poison IV at F, you will attack F, A and B. If you aim at D, It will attack A, B and D. If you aim at A, it will affect A, B and C.

Frailty

Frailty attack enemy with the fastest speed. Throughout this article, there are more skills that affect enemies with the fastest speed. They have the same mechanism as this one, so, I would only cover this once.

Frailty attacks:

Frailty I one target
Frailty II two targets
Frailty III one target
Frailty IV three targets

In the case there are 6 enemies with:
A 500speed
B 450speed
C 400speed
D 350speed
E 300speed
F 250speed

If you use frail IV on F, you will attack A, B and F. If you use on B, you will attack A, B and C.

Ice

Ice is an attacking skill. It affect enemies according to speed, just like in the case of Frailty. As such, the order of attack is also the same as in the case of Frailty. Damage increases with pet's Intelligence.

Ice attacks:

Ice I one target
Ice II two targets
Ice III one target
Ice IV three targets

Fire

Fire is an attacking skill. It affects enemies according to speed.

Fire attacks:

Fire I one target
Fire II two targets
Fire III one target
Fire IV three targets

Flash

Flash is an attacking skill similar to Ice and Fire and attack enemies based on speed

Flash attacks:

Flash I one target
Flash II two targets
Flash III one target
Flash IV three targets

Evil

The same as all the other mage like attack skills, it affect enemies according to speed and Intelligent level affect damage done.

Evil attacks:

Evil I one target
Evil II two targets
Evil III one target
Evil IV three targets


We have now basically covered all attacking skills. Now it is the defensive skills or skills that affect your own party.

Multishot

Though it it not a mage like skill it has the same characteristic as one. It affect according to speed and causes physical damage.

Multishot attacks:

Multishot I one target
Multishot II two targets
Multishot III one target
Multishot IV three targets

Enhance

Enhance affects your own party members based on Strength. Meaning, the strongest in your party will be enhanced. Enhance increases your party members physical attack attributes.

Enhance affects:

Enhance I one target
Enhance II two targets
Enhance III one target
Enhance IV three targets

In the case your party have 6 members:
A 200Str
B 199Str
C 198Str
D 197Str
E 196Str
F 195Str

Enhance IV on E will affect A, B and E. Enhance IV on C will affect A, B and C. I believe you get the hang of it now.

Protect

Protect affect you own party members your own party members according to speed. Protect reduces damages done by enemy to your party members. The concept is the same as Frailty, only this time it affect you own party.

Protect affects:

Protect I one target
Protect II two targets
Protect III one target
Protect IV three targets

Speed

Speed affect your party members based on speed. It speed up those party members that are affected.

Speed affects:

Speed I one target
Speed II two targets
Speed III one target
SPeed IV three targets

Purge chaos

Purge chaos affect your party members based on speed. It purges chaos that is done by your enemy. As such, if three of your party members are chaosed, remember to always target the one with the lowest speed before you purge.

Purge chaos affects:

Purge chaos I one target
Purge chaos II two targets
Purge chaos III one target
Purge chaos IV three targets

Un-Stun

Un-stun affect your members based on speed. As such, the same advice apply as in purge chaos. It un-stun your party members stunned by the enemy.

Un-stun affects:

Un-stun I one target
Un-stun II two targets
Un-stun III one target
Un-stun IV three targets

Heal

Heal affect your party members based on the remaining HP on them. As such, even though one character may have full HP and lower than other characters in your party who is damaged, Heal will go to the one with lower remaining HP. Heal restore HP level of your characters.

Heal affects:

Heal I one target
Heal II two targets
Heal III one target
Heal IV three targets

If you do not understand the above, I will give you an example. SUppose there are 6 members in your party:
A HP 10k/60k
B HP 20k/20k
C HP 9k/9k
D HP 13k/20k
E HP 25k/50k
F HP50k/50k

If you cast Heal IV on D, it will affect C(Even though it has full HP), A and D. If you cast heal IV on F, it will affect C, A and F(even though it has full HP).


I also like to add that in most cases in pets with attacking skills, e.g. Evil and Flash, skill II actually causes more damage individually on targets than skill I.

Well, I hope it is clear. Do forward questions if you do not understand.

MB-grandmaster
