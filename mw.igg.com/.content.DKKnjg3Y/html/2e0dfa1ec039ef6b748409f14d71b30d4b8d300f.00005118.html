Lucky Number

Event Duration: March 29, 2008 to March 31, 2008


Rewards: Super Rose, Pets, Mystical Fruits and so on


Event Contents:

GM will randomly pick 3 lucky numbers (For example.1, 2 and 2). The player that has these 3 lucky numbers in his ID (for example ID 156422) is qualified to send a message to our GMs through live support chat or the GM channel to win a small gift.

Sometimes GM will change rules:

1. Pick 3 lucky numbers ((For example. 3, 5 and 6) and 1 unlucky number (0). So the player with the 3 lucky numbers but not the unlucky number 0 in his ID is qualified to contact our GMs to receive a small gift.

2. Pick 3 lucky numbers (for example1, 2 and 3). The 3 numbers must be in the players ID in same sequence. For instance, 102030 would win but 103020 would not.

  

Note: During the event, our GMs will announce the lucky numbers through the system channel, and winners need to contact the GMs to receive their rewards within 30 minutes, otherwise your qualifications will be cancelled. So pay attention to the messages from the system channel.
