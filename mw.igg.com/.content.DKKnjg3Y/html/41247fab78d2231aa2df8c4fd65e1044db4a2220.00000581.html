witch is the better one?

My personal opinion:

Mythwar 1:

this is the original mythwar game with it's special features, exploited gold but when has that hurt us?
the BEST battle system ever invented, good game for low rec comps.

Mythwar 2:

I know it's closed beta, but as soon as I started up the game I hated it:

battle system: the preparation time is taken away, exept in boss fights. and the graphics has turned into more childish. and the background is really bad.

Newbie: the newbie guide is harder to understand, worse map, graphics not any giant step forward...

Characters: cents&borgs = much uglier. Mages=almost same. humans=no change -.- .

other: item mall is a big deal int this game (what happened to FREE FOREVER?)...

suggestions: make it more like MW1 but still has that little change in it...make the battle system as in MW1.

that was mostly it...
