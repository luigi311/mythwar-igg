Skills

1. About Skills

  • Players can only use skills during battle. Players using special skills can injure the opponent directly, create chaos and even have the power to change the characteristic of opponents. All these special abilities increase the chances of victory for the skill-user.
  • Skill abilities varies from race to race. Each race has 2 different sexes and each sex has 4 different skills to learn from.
  • Human's corresponding skill is hypnotizing their enemies.
  • Mage's corresponding skill is using magical skill to injure enemy.
  • Centaur's corresponding skill is changing the enemy's or its own characteristics temporary.
  • Borg's corresponding skill is the ability to cause the enemy’s skill to vanish.
  • There are 4 levels for each skill.
  • At level 1, players can only learn their basic skills from the skill center. The skill will be acquired upon completion of the quest.
  • After acquiring a new skill at skill center, the players will then have to practice using their skills during battle. When the skill practiced to a certain level, the players can go back to the skill center to acquire even higher-level skills.

2. Obtaining Skills

  • New character will be able to choose from 4 different types of skill. Different races will have different skill abilities.
  • Every race can train up their skill levels by practicing during battle.
  • Through participating in a husband and wife / brotherhood quest, players can obtain special combined skills.
  • Through player's choice of weapon selection, players can obtain special skill depend on what weapon skills they possess.
  • By completing quests, players can obtain special skills.

3. About Practicing Skills

  • Skill training will have several limitations: title, practice level and introduction quest (only low level players will have this limitation).
  • Introduction quest: Every race has 3 professional skills. Every skill tree will correspond to one skill master to train. Players have to complete the introduction quest set by the skill master before the player can go up the skill tree.
  • Professional title: there are several players' title; the only title which will affect the player’s skill title is the skill title. By completing the skill title quest, the players can obtain the respective skill titles. Skill levels will determine the player’s title level, if the title level is enough; will have to look at the corresponding low level skill practice level.

Skill experience level: after obtaining one skill, it depends on the number of time use to level up the skill. Every level of skill requires experience. One has to level up the skill tree starting from the lowest level, to increase the experience in order to obtain the high level skill. After every successful use of the skill, the experience will increase by one point.

4. About skill level effect

Skill levels affects the power and area where it’s being unleashed. The higher the skill levels, the higher the power and the same goes for the MP needed.

6. About use of skill

Leveling up of skill relies on repeated usage of skill. The more you use your skill, the faster you level up.

7. About skill being used

Side effects from using the skills include: poison, chaos, lethargy, death notification, reincarnation, resistless, power, repercussion, etc‿these effects are experienced during current battle and will not be brought into the next battle.
