MW2 = botters paradise?

I bet this will cause some waves especially such a topic comes from a mod, but I have to load off this statement somewhere.

So, here's my lil story:
After several weeks I managed - for the 1st time since closed beta - to log into MW2 and start walking around, taking a look, enjoying the 2x weekend...
...and we all know what happens - somewhen you get bored and frustrated because for lvling you need good skill, for good skill you need money and for fast money you need decent lvl and nice skills - dead end.
In MW1 = no prob - even the most retarded jerk could "bot", just log mules, go sleep and wake up rich (not even talking about the possibilities of W** or C*...)
In MW2 this shouldn't work anymore, should it? I mean everything everything smells to be designed to make botters life tough...
...non-tradeable Mokka, sucky MFs, no skill exp from spam, ...
...looks pretty tough for bad for botters, or?

The answer is surprising (at least it was for me) : it's easier to set up bots in MW2 then in any other game I could imagine. Actually, I started to have to have the impression, that it was designed to make botting real easy.
Want proof? Sorry, but you have to trust my words:
Within few (meaning 2-3) h I managed to set up a bot able to do whole quests for me. Ok I'm not stupid, but my programming skill are at lower average level at most. I don't even want to imagine what a skilled programmer could do in that time.

For all you wannabee-script-kiddies: no chance that I will give you any hints how to cheat - don't even bother posting here or flooding my pm-box.
For all others who are around longer: did you notice the same? Is it even possible to be at the upper 10% in MW2 without cheating? [ok, I admit that was a rethoric question, we all know the answer].

...that's it from my side about MW2 after playing it for not even 2 days - back to ladder season 5 (you know what I mean^^)
