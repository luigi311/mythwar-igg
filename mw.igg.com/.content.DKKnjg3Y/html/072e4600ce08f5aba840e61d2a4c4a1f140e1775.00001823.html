Wisdom of the Masses Needed

Dear players,

IGG has been growing quickly since its inception two years ago. Without our players continued support, IGG would have faded into obscurity long ago. We want to hear from the people who matter most to us about how to promote and expand our reach.

Content:
Suggestions about the following content are welcome. You can also post your original suggestions.
1. Promotion methods
2. Commission proportion
3. Combining the Promoter System (PPS) and IGG games
4. Promoter System Improvements
5. Suggestions about new game's open beta's and operation duration's
6. How the Promoter System cooperates with the promotion cooperators?
7. Communication among promoters
8. Dividing the forum parts and managing the promoter forum

Please post with as much detailed information as you can about your suggestion. We will start with a post in the forum and players can reply to the post with their suggestions. Remember to write your account on the post so that we can contact you in time.

Rewards:
1st place: (1) 2000 promoter credits and 500 Silver Ingots
2nd place: (2) 1000 promoter credits and 300 Silver Ingots
3rd place: (2) 500 promoter credits and 200 Silver Ingots

On the last day of each month, we will select the winners. We will present all the selected players with certain rewards. Meanwhile, we will present the promoter whose suggestions are accepted with more rewards. All IGG games with released Promoter System's are available.

We will announce the winners on the official website.

For more information about this event visit: http://rich.igg.com
