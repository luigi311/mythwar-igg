Needle in the Haystack

Needle in the Haystack

Can you spot the difference between two almost-identical pictures? Come in an give it a go! We will be holding a Needle in the Haystack competition in the forum where players can win some great prizes. Here's how to compete: 2 pictures will be posted in the forum. All you need to do is reply to the post listing the number of differences that you can find. The first 7 players to post the correct answers will be awarded 50 silver ingots.  After the event ends, we will announce the winners name list and the correct answers.
