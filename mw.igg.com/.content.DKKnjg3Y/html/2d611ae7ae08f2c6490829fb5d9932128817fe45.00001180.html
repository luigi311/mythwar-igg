AOD Auction House


What 3 words, even spoken in hushed tones, makes every person in Myth War pay attention? The answer will inevitably be "Armor of Dreams", mythical elite armor possessed by only a few. The chance of getting one is slim. But take a deep breath and relax while I tell you the good news. Many Armor of Dreams will be put up for auction everyday until the end of this month.

The AOD Auction House, home of classic gear. Dig it.

 Auction Rules:
 1. Fortune Bombing Integrals are the only official currency used during auctions. The 'Reserve' price is 300 Fortune Bombing Integrals. Each bid increase must be at least 100 integrals
2. The highest bidder will be the owner of the Armor of Dreams. But if the highest bidder does not have enough Fortune Bombing Integrals, the second highest bidder will then get it.
3. Bidders are not allowed to place bids with more Fortune Bombing Integrals than they have, or their bids will be invalid. Bid posts can't be edited, or bids will also be invalid.
4. We will delete all posts not related to the auction. Participant bidders must observe our forum's rules.
5. Fortune Bombing Integrals will be taken away from successful bidders' accounts after the auction is over. We will then send out nicknames for the new owners of the Armor of Dreams. You can claim your items from GMs through Live Support.
6. All rights reserved by IGG.

Auction time: September 22th to September 26th. Each of the Armor of Dreams will be put up for auction for 24 hours.
