After a lot of killing and lvling a rb mule, that you might not have in the first place, you'll end up with a rb pet with 0 intimacy. Congrats to you.

Here is a good way to rb a pet if that's all you want, w/o buying any mp and and w/o wasting a whole lot of time in the process.

You have just reborned, but your lvl 100 pet lacks the need intimacy to lvl. What to do? Head down to revive arena, take the Sword of stars quests and begin autowalking all the way to mokka max. After you've done that, go and get your II skills then repeat the process. After you've mokka maxed your II skills, start saving the mokka for the III and IV for when you get them. After that's done, grab a GF ss pot and head down to Devil's gate and proceed to more autowalking, this time you're getting bee sspots which you will sell or use later and Nelson boots. After you're filled a mule with Nelson boots, use one of the bee sspot and go to ToL 3 and beging autowalking there for DTDs and EW ss pots. Once you've got the same number of DTDs as Nelsons, cash them in for MF for your future rb pet to save space. Just a note, when you get to 21 to avoid falling into fog cave, go and delvl your self, just set any pets of battle mode and proceed to killing yourself repeatedly. Keep doing Nels and DTDs runs till your lvl 100 pet gets 50k int and is ready to reborn. Don't use the MFs you got now, save them for later, or if you need cash, sell them together with the bee and EW sspots.
