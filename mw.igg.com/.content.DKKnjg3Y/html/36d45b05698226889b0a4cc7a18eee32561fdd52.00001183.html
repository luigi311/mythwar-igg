Author: didiao    Time: 2008-10-10 04:57 AM     Subject: The New Kid on the Block

All players probably remember the hard times they had when they had just started playing a new game. No money, no pets, nothing. So, when you see a rookie asking for questions and for help, can you really stand idly by and do nothing? We hope not. So, join us in this event for newbies and make friends with all the new players. Help them get through the hardest part, the beginning.

Event Duration: October 11th - October 19th

Play Flow:
1. Players with the title of  'New Horizon' can team up with players below Level 20. Then go to the Bar in Woodlingor at Server 1 and find a GM to register.
2. Players with the title of New Horizon must offer the GM the following details: ID and their teammates IDs. Registration will be complete after the GM finishes verifying the information offered. After that, participants can start playing game.
3. When players below Level 20 get to Level 30, the whole team can go to the Bar in Woodlingor at Server 1 and find the GM to claim their prizes.

Prizes:
1. Players honored to be New Horizon will get 500k Gold when their teammates reach Level 30.
2. Players below Level 20 reaching Level 30 will get a Tri-experience Roll.

Notes:
1. Participants must team up to register and reclaim prizes. If the GM is away from the keyboard, please contact the GM via Live Support.
2. Every New Horizon player can team up with four players. More players in one team will earn participants more rewards. Participants are allowed to take part in this event repeatedly.
3. Our GM will confirm if the participating players are cheating by checking their participating IP. Once cheating is detected, the whole team will end up with no prizes.
