Rules:
1. Entrants must contain their accounts and character names (may be hidden) in their posts. Works must be tagged with “Guide” and posted in  the Event Zone.

5. The winners of the work chosen by us will receive prizes. IGG will own any and all rights to use their work.

Judgment:
We will select the winners to be awarded with the 1st prize and 2nd prize. All the remaining participants will receive consolation prizes.
