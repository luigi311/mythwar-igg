 June 6th is IGG’s 2nd Anniversary, so we will be holding an event with big rewards to show our gratitude to all of our players continued support.

Event Content:

Make a birthday card for IGG’s 2nd Anniversary

Event Rules:
 1.Every player is qualified to participate in this event, and the submission should also be related to MW.
 2.We don’t appreciate cards bearing rude or impolite messages and we don’t think anyone else does either. Remember that these kinds of messages are NOT allowed.
 3.Your post should contain your server name, character ID and character name.
 4.After the event begins, participants can reply to this post with the cards that they have designed.
 5.All cards should be original ideas. However, should we receive 2 identical or mostly identical cards, the one sent first will be counted.
 6.All participants should obey the event rules. Otherwise, they will be disqualified from the event.

 Event Duration:June 5th – June 12th
 Selection Duration: June 12th – June 15th

Event Rewards:
First Place(1) Howling Beast
Second Place (2) Sand Giant
Third Place (3) 1.3 growth intelligent pet
In addition, we will pick 5 outstanding participants and give them 100 credits.
