Rescue

Rescue  

Are you frustrated because you've been imprisoned for PK? We want you to be happy while you are playing our games, so we have mapped out a plan to rescue all imprisoned players this weekend.

Event Duration: 3:00pm-5:00pm every Saturday and Sunday from June 20th to June 30th (Server Time)
Event Description:
If players are imprisoned for PK during the event period, they can send an application for a rescue to our online GMs through the GM channel or LIVE CHAT. Players must leave information about their characters' names, characters' IDs and servers with the GMs. Once the relevant confirmation work has been done, GMs will start to rescue players out of their prisons.
Players rescued by the GMs will get rewards as well. Everyone only has one chance.
Rewards:
50 Fortune Bombing Integrals and 3 Mystical Fruit (1.5M Exp)
                                            
Myth War Online Team, IGG
