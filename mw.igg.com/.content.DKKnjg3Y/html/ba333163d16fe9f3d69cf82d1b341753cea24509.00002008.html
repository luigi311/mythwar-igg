Transfer Pet Registration

Transfer Pet  Registration

Each account can only transfer one pet(two pets for VIP players) and the account should have a level 80+ character. Pets' growth in MW II are different from that in MW I. So we have made a menu about pets which can be transfered.We suggest that players can keep level 1 or level 1-1 pets so that they can grow with their pets together.

If you want to transfer your pets form MWI to MWII Please leave your account informaion(MW1 Account name,Server, Character name, Character ID ,Pet,Pet level; And MWII account name ,character name ,character ID)

NOTE: It won't keep Pets' intimacy and resistance.And Pet Growth will be randomly.GMS will collect players Registration Information from June 27th to  July 1st . And transfer the pets from July 2nd .

We suggest players hide your information
