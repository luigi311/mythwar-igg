Guide to being super powerful

Dear Players,

Welcome to the Experience Discussion Zone. grandmaster, kornelax, Spyder are your moderators here and if you have any problem regarding this Zone, do feel free to contact us.

If you look at the top of this zone, you will find an Article Index. In it are articles that we had gone through and highly suggest all players to read. We believe that they will be very helpful in making your game better. The link to the index is as below:

http://www.mythwaronline.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=1963

If you find an article you like and you feel that you will refer to it quite regularly, we suggest that you put that article in your Favourites by pressing Alt-A and then click Add to Favourites. This way, when you want to refer to that article again, you will not have to flip the pages around desperately and only need to click the Favorites icon on the top of your Internet Explorer Window and click the page you want to refer.

We would welcome all suggestions, view them and consider implementing them so that it will be much easier for you to browse through all the useful informations that are available here. If you have a problem that is not already written by any player, do feel free to create a new topic and we, together with other helpful and experienced players would be more than glad to be of your assistance. However, do give us some time to respond to your queries.

In the course of time, if we find more suitable articles, we will add it to the list in the index, so do look into it from time to time to see if there is anything new. For people who want to contribute articles and want them listed in this index, please let us know. We will review it and put it up if we feel that it is necessary. However, we would like to say, please refrain from using commital sentences, e.g. I have seen it with my very eyes or I know because I have one, unless you are very sure of what you have written

Thank you.
