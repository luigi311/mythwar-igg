  Worn Deity quest guide
Couldnt find it so made it by myself
As you know after lv 91 you don’t get anymore exp in Seal quest so question that you allways want to ask: where to lv up fast? Guild quests? Crusher (till lv 101)? Burial 4 (till lv 96)? Drowlord?
What can i say, all these places good but there is one more, yea, you got this right, Worn quest .
Of course a lot of you tried to do that and died but that was part of progress. After that you dont try anymore just doing lord . But, with good equipment deity can be much faster than lord so here written eq what you need and strategy for mobs.


Prince Zenith

Mage

Skill: Fire/Ice

Mob

Skill: Fire/Ice

Personal Note:

This one is not really hard you can do it without any special armour, best to stun them and kill one by one. Drain and melee hits them good so that’s shouldn’t be problem. Mage gets heavy dmg from melee attacks. (Of course if you want this to be faster and easier you should have Equipment with some ice and fire ress. 50 of each one and this will be piece of cake)

Borg Giant

Borg

Skill: none, melee only

Other Notes: Strong vs Melee / Weak vs Magic and hits 100-200k dmg but sometimes can hit even less

Mob

Skill: Repel/Melee

Other Notes: Weak vs Fire

Personal Note:

This one could be problem. All of them totally resistant to melee, so no use, minions casts repel, so mages should be kamikazes to attack them, borg hits with 160k physical and minions capable to hit with 28k dmg (physical). Drain, magic (fire does very good dmg to front row) and poison have no obstacle to kill them. Cast repel if you want but if borg stands last and repel on, that who will get hit, will get and free tele too.
You can make it without special eq, but i suggest at least 70+ melee resistance armour set, or high dodge set...


Dark Knight

Male Human

Skill: Chaos IV

Other Notes: Weak vs Melee and can counter with 6k dmg

Mob:

7 Felsworms

Skill: Chaos II

2 Snake demons

Skills: none, uses melee

Other Notes: Weak vs Melee and can hit 1500 physical

Personal Note:

We fought long with this one, because all party was chaosed. Mobs werent a problem, but i and my GW were...this one can be soloed by str borg (not need to be full or half str, at least should hit 10k on capilla), i mean soloed without pet. Mob: 2 monsters attacks with 1500 dmg (on chars hits lower because they have better armor than pets), 7 monsters are very fast and casts chaos II all the time and knight casts chaos IV . Sometimes they can cast stun II but very rarely. If party gonna kill this
one and has str borg, make sure that borg has really good chaos ress. All skills does good dmg on them.

Worn general

Male Centaur

Skill: Drain IV

Other Note: Weak vs Melee, imune to chaos

Mob

9 Aurora lions

Skill: Hypnotise

Other Note: Weak vs Melee and they can hit with 20k dmg

Personal Note:

All skill works on them, chaos lions and they will kill each other, melee works greatly (cent cant be chaosed). Good anti hypno set would be great if you don’t want to sleep during the fight.

Princess Agony

Female Human

Skill: Frailty IV

Other Note: Strong vs Chaos/Stun/Hypno

Mob

7 Skeletals:

Skill: Melee (about 70k dmg)

Other Note: Weak vs Stun/Hypno

2 Glimmer Fish:

Skill: Heal I (heals back 70k to skeles)

Other Note: Weak vs Stun/Hypno

Personal Note:

This one is hardest (my opinion), they stopped our victories ( free tele for everyone), but at least we killed that girl . Mob: princess (uses frailty IV and that was really effective, she cant be chaosed, stunned or hypnotised, at least all others spells works good), skeletals (can be stuned or hypnotised, not sure about chaos...All other spells works good. They hit with 60-70k physical) and 2 glimmer fishes (use heal I but that’s add back to skele 70k hp. Fishes can be stuned, not sure about chaos).
Stun as many skeles as you can and keep stunned, then kill princess and fishes and then somehow skeles.
Team should be: 2 humans (would be good if one of them female for poison), borg, cent and mage. Better female borg to cast protect but males good too. Melee and frailty ress set can save your lives here so don’t forget to take it with you.

So now you know about worns, less talking more doing, enjoy and don’t die.

This is my first post so tell me if i done something wrong. Any suggestions would be great.


Ty for those who died with me there (PostHuman, dukuso, Machis (he was our brave kamikaze) and Martin_Longbow) ty for eLAS too, without him this post wouldn’t appeared here.

 May as well go share the knowledge I got from completing it 100% :)

PS: Drowlord is faster, rewards take forever to get too....

Prince Zenith is easy enough, take 2 humans and both use Chaos IV 9 or 10 and you'll go off fine... Alternatively, if you prefer to take them 1 by 1, use Hypno, it's muc more flexible and lasts longer as memory serves from a while back...

Borg Giant is also relatively easy. His hits vary between 100k and 200k... I know cuz I survived once :)

You can poison them all which is fun, or pray that your cent has enough mp to heal the mage every round :) (you can use Critical to skip the physcial resist though too don't forget)

Dark Knight is a matter of taking it as you go, even 120 chaos resistance is enough to get chaosed by them, if you can get a female borg to cast protect you might be able to escape it... alternatively, get a FAST cent to cast a high level Blizzard/Multi shot - they're weak to physical.

General guy... again similar thing to Dark Knight weak to physical...

If you can avoid killing the general, his drain will remove hypno, alternatively, use a pet to attack your char... Even while hypno'd a pet can still pot a char... don't know why, doesn't really make sense..

Posion them if you can, it'll probably help you on your way etc...

Agony I agree is definately the hardest, especially without resistances.

The skele's can't be chaosed as memory serves, the fishies can be chaosed however.

Again, Hypno IV will be more assistance than Stun IV for the skele's.

Protect IV will help you so much in all 5 of these if really stupid :)

Personally, I recommend taking 2 humans for all 5 of them just because it'll help a lot.

Oh also, you can finish the first 4 listed by you without any resistances at all fairly easily... Agony is the one that'll take an eternity to manage... well... I never managed it the only reason I managed is cuz the rest of the party had resistance



Hope that's a bit more handy info for you :)
