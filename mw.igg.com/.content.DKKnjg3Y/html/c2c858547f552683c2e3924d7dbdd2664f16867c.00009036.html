Special Activities during Open Beta

 

Angel Leveling Match

In order to reward those good students, the Angel Lyceum has decided to give some gifts to them during the Christmas Holidays. Only those Angels who have a high ranking will be offered the big rewards.

Event Duration:

00:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 11th~00:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 18th

Event Rules:

1. The top three players in leveling match during the activity time will be offered a special prize. In addition, we will have some Lucky Numbers. The lucky players, whose places in this match are the same as the numbers, will get a prize.

2. GM will announce the name of the winners in the official website on Dec 19. The winners should contact the Live Support within three days to get the prize.

Event Price:

First Place: 25000 Angel Coins.

Second Place: 15000Angel Coins.

Third Place: 10000Angel Coins.

Lucky Number Winners: 6000 Angel Coins each.

 

 

 

Badge Collection:

Recently, we have heard that monsters knew Santa would come to Angel Lyceum and bring many gifts to the angels. These loathsome monsters are planning to rob Santa of those gifts. Angels, let's go save Christmas and Santa!

Event Duration:

21:00~22:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 11th

21:00~22:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 13th

Event Location:

Angel Lyceum A (175, 93)

Event Rules:

1. The Christmas Angel will appear in Angel Lyceum A (175, 93)

2. Angels should find the Christmas Angel, and exchange their badges for some rewards.

3. Angels can obtain badges from monster bosses.

Event Prize:

5 Copper Badges: 1000 Angel Coins

                                Common Red Potion*5

                                Common Blue Potion*5

5 Silver Badges:    5000 Angel Coins

                                 Double Exp Card *1

 

 

 


Hide and Seek

Christmas is coming. Christmas Angel wants to play "hide and seek" with the angels. If you can find him in Angel Lyceum A, or one of the other main cities, you will obtain big rewards!

Event Duration:

21:00~22:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 12th

21:00~22:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 17th

21:00~22:00 EST (GMT-5) Dec 18th

Event Location:

Angel Lyceum A and the four Main Cities

Event Contents:

1. Christmas Angel will hide in Angel Lyceum A or one of the four main cities.

2. The Lyceum Master will inform angels of some clues to find the Christmas Angel through system announcements. 

3. Angels need to find Christmas Angel with the announcement information.

4. Angels who can find Christmas Angel will be offered presents.
