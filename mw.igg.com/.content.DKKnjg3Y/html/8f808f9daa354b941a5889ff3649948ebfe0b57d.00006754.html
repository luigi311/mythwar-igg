 Theres no rule violation by multilogging. A player with a single char cant do anything on this game anyway, be prepared to level at least 2 of them.

Yes, you can use alt+tab to change between clients and use all of them to get mokkas. Theres no violation on this either.

You cant check MAC address of anything. Internet works via ip, the mac is device-only, and its replaced every time the info pass through a new device. Myth servers get their router/switch/whatever mac info, which receives all external data. The client limitation is normally made with a script on the own client, which verifies all process running. IF it finds a myth client running, it blocks a new session.

But, again, multilogging isnt forbidden here - its essential.
