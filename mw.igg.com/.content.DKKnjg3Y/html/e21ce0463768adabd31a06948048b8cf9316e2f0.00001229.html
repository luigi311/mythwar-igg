DC desert city

Rule-BL-SP is diff from SP-BL the left one means the map u haf to train the right one means near the xx tp

lvl1-6 train in tol1
lvl 6-10 fight monsters near SP-blython Very Happy big crab/bloodpede
lvl10-18 train in SP-BL on bloodpede/glimmer fish
lvl 18-23 fight near BL-SP ent flyer, bubble crab, big flyer
lvl23-24 go up further of BL of ur map n fight poisonpede n big flyer combination
lvl24-28 go up even futher near to bone desert tp n fight poisonpede n flying fish combo

lvl28-31 SW1

lvl38-42 guard n axewalker in CA near bottom left corner
lvl42-43 hellguard n axewalker near CA-BD
lvl43-48 mudraper n evil skeletal around BD-CA

Five ways for lvl 40s-68 #Last two ways r for 58-68

lvl49-51 WL1 from SP
lvl51-53 WL1 from DC
lvl53-55 WL2
lvl55-58 WL3

OR

lvl46-51 River Guardian n Hell guard in FR
lvl51-57 Cyclops n evil axewalker in FR
lvl57-60 Evil Cyclops n eyekicker combo near NPC kelina in FR
lvl60-68 Eyekicker n tree demon near right side of map. Warning: dun go bottom right or u meet with evil tree demon which chaos often. not recommended to fight

OR

lvl48-52 Spirit Wolf n evil skeletal TOP LEFT of BD
lvl52-60 Spirit wolf n giant slicer. jus go down a little from ur area
lvl60-68 go down more to bottom left corner to fight higher lvl monsters

OR

lvl58-60 AP1
lvl60-62 AP2
lvl62-64 AP3 Hint: for reaching AP3 always try left n top portal
lvl64-68 AP4

OR

lvl58-68 EL/DL4 easier to find than AP n ard the same exp as AP

Now its evil exp in BR. Recommended to go with a good party

lvl68-69 BU 1 Enter from SP
lvl69-70 BU Ruins Enter from BL
lvl70-74 BU2
lvl74-78 BU3
lvl78-lvlxxx BU4 only way for lvl 95 n above to train now
