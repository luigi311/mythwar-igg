Celebration with GMs

Content：
 During this day, GMs, assisted by sentinels will hold a series of online interactive activities with all players, including mutual blessing, firework play, quick-brain activity and online pet auction.

Details：
1. On the night of Dec 24th, GMs will send their greetings to all players and announce the beginning of the activity.
2. GMs will send out invitations to all players to join their activities held in specific places.
3. After announcing the rules, the activities will start.

Rewards：
 Plenty of Fireworks, Trainer Device, Devil’s Blood, Stone of Dream and Double-gold Rumor Scrolls, etc
