Myth War Weekend Blowout


Dear players,

With the weekend fast approaching, why not have a rest and enjoy the game after 5 days of working? We have prepared two special events that will absolutely blow your mind this weekend…ok, not really, but we do have 2 very cool events to help you pass the time. Enjoy!

MW In-game Olympic Torch Relay

Event Duration:From 3:00pm to 5:00pm on April 26th(server time)

Event Contents:
1. The GM will ask players some questions in the System channel. The first two players who answer correctly can take part in the In-game Olympic Torch Relay.
2. Go and find the GM in the appointed city and obtain 3 fireworks from the GM. Then ignite one of the fireworks.
3. Give the other two fireworks to one of your friends in another city and ask your friend to ignite one of the fireworks.
4. Your friend should go to the appointed city and give the third fireworks to another GM in the appointed city. Meanwhile, your friend should also correctly answer the GM’s questions.
5. The team who finishes the relay with the fastest time will be the winner.

Event Rewards: 200 Fortune Bombing Integrals and Super Roses.


Drowcrusher Quest

Event Duration：0:00 April 27,2008--23:59 April 27,2008 (GMT-5)

Event Contents: Each character is allowed to finish Drowcrusher quest 5 times per day, and there won't be a time limit for completing this quest during this period.

We await you in game! Enjoy and have fun!


Mythwar Online Team, IGG
