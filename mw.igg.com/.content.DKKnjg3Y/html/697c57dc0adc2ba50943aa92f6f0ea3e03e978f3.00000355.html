3# A A A Post at 2008-1-3 08:24 PM  Show author
feel free to experiment. but if you want a solid popular build, i would suggest full sta until lvl 70. at which you will be doing seal for quite a long time, keep adding sta until you think you feel comfortable with your hp (i.e. you don't have to keep a close look on your hp while doing the quest). then start adding int. more int means more mp and a bit higher multi damage. but the idea is you can train skills to high levels and can afford using them.

str is pretty useless. agi is not needed as cent is naturally fast.

many found that full sta all up to lvl 100 work wonderfully. this is generally the case if your skills aren't too high. for example, you can keep heal iv at low level for use instead of heal ii. but this won't work as well if you want to use max skills iv.
