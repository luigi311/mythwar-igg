Credits VS Dollars

Dear Promoters,

All promoters will be divided into several grades according to their credits. Meanwhile, the exchange rate for credits to dollars has gone down, so you now need fewer credits when exchanging for dollars. All promoters can experience the grade system in advance before it is formally released.

Details Introduction:

All promoters can reply to thesign_up post in the forum and take part in this event. After you sign up, the system will automatically start on your experience. When your credits change, the system will change your grade at the same time. Of course, it has no effect on your daily promotion. In addition, we will present you with extra credits as a reward when your grade changes.

Event Duration: From April 17th to May 7th

Event Contents:

Silver Promoters will obtain 800 extra credits when they become Gold Promoters.
Copper Promoters will obtain 600 extra credits when they become Silver Promoters.
General Promoters will obtain 400 extra credits when they become Copper Promoters.

During this event, all promoters can obtain extra credits several times. This means that even if you have obtained your reward when you became a Copper Promoter, you can obtain more rewards when you become a Silver Promoter. We wish to welcome all promoters to join in the fun with us!

Rules and Regulations:

 Promoters who meet the requirements above need to post the following personal information here before7th May.
