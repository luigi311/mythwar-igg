Item Mall Promotion Events

Item Mall Promotion Events  

Myth war II has held its Open Beta for one week now and so to thank you for your continued support we would like to announce the 2 special events that we are holding in your honor in Version 1.0
.
Event 1: Big Item Mall Promotion
Players will enjoy a 20% discount on all items for 3 days.

Event 2: Firecracker and Wishes for your Friends
Players can use only 2 points to buy firecrackers, and 4 points to buy a Jukebox.

Event Time: From July 4th to July 6th (EDT GMT-4)


Note: Myth War II also have special events. Some Myth War II Open Beta events are going on as well, so check them out!
