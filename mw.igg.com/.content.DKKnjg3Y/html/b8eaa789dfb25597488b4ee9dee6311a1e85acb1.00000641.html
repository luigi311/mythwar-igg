Advice for people leaving MW1 for MW2

I have noticed a few people recently leaving MW1 with the intention just to play MW2. Before you do I would like to make a few suggesstions.
Transfer your service points to another guild mate.
Share the wealth. Don't just reaper your character. Help some new players by giving them some of your stuff or pets. Don't let it go to waste.
Leave on a friendly note. Don't flame the world and try to go with a bang. Much better to say your proper goodbyes and stay friendly. Who knows, you might meet again in MW2.
Do not be tempted to sell or trade or give an account. Its against the rules.
Lastly, reconsider. Why not just play both? Even if you are not going to spend time in MW1 much anymore, your experience and knowledge is of value. So drop by and keep the MW1 community going.
Leave your balls in MW1 where they belong. MW2 will have its own balls.
