3# A A A Post at 2008-3-20 16:31  Show author
you can always go with a full party with that's available. everyone got same drop. i found the random encounters on each level are harder than the spirits itself in that level. so i would suggest you go hunt when you can use devil's tear to walk safely there (that's 10 levels above the mobs). killing spirits should be easy if you know their weakness

lair 1 weak to physical
lair 2 weak to non-physical
lair 3 - use drain, crit, poison
lair 4 - it has low hp, weaker than lair 3

note however that it's arguably not true that you got better eggs at deeper lairs. better crystals tho. but the drop rate also goes down substantially the deeper you go.
