Gifts for Pay by Cash Users


Duration: August 22nd - September 22nd

Eligibility: Purchase Myth War Online Item Mall Pointsvia Pay by Cash

 Details:
Players who purchase Myth War mall points via Pay by Cash from August 22nd until September 22nd  will receive special gifts from MWO. During the event, players can gain double Fortune Bombing Credits, while any player who purchases 2000 mall points at one time will also get  Lucky Egg.
 Notes:
1. Only players who purchase Myth War Online mall points via Pay by Cash during the event will receive gifts.
2. Players who purchase 2000 Myth War Online mall points at a time shall contact our GM via Live Support to claim the Lucky Egg.
3. Our GM will give eligible players a Lucky Egg by trading with the player.


To purchase mall points, visit http://mw2.igg.com/shop/paybycash.php.
Fortune Bombing page: http://mw.igg.com/sales/index.php.
