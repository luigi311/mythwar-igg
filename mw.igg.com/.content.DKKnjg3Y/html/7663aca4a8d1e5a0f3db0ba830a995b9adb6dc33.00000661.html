IGG considers FastCard

Looking for a easier way to get Item Mall points?  Something that doesn't require you to go on the internet?  How about just walking into a store and buying them?

After making a suggestion to a GM, I think IGG would actually consider this, but I'd like to know what the community thinks.

I was hoping IGG would distribute FastCards, which is done by Nexon, Gaia, Neopets, etc.

So, what is a FastCard?
It's a card, you can buy in a store, then go onto the offical game website and enter the code in (the code would usually be on the back of the card, and you scratch off something to see it).  After entering the code, you would get whatever amount you bought in Item Mall points.  The cards would usually be in stores like Target or CVS.





What does IGG think of this idea?


So tell me, what does the community feel about having FastCards?  Would you also like it to happen?  Considering most of us are under 18, I thought it'd be a good idea.
