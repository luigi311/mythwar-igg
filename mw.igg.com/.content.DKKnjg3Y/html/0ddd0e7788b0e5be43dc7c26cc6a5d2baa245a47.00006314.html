  Thanksgiving Forum avtivity
Do you want to hang out with your close friends in game on Thanksgiving Day? You can travel around the world of Mythwar with some friends and enjoy a delicious Thanksgiving meal.

How to participate in
1. Travel Map: You can arrange a travel route with instructions by yourself in terms of Myth War Maps.
2. Cook Thanksgiving Meal: You should put the meal pictures under your Travel Maps.
Cooking Materials: Turkey，Stuffing, Cranberry Sauce, Corn on the Cob, Pumpkin Pie, Bread and Rolls, and various other vegetables and fruit.
3. Spread thanks: The GM will select some lucky participants to help them spread their words of thanks and blessing on Thanksgiving Day.
(Format: XXX wants to thank XXX,)(ie.) Thanks for caring about me so much

Rules:
1. All participants need to finish the "Travel Route" and "Thanksgiving Meal".
2. The "Travel Route" should be described in your own words. The "Thanksgiving Meal" should be illustrated with pictures.
3. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited, once you are caught cheating, your participation qualifications will be cancelled.
4.Please post a real sign with your id and server along with the picture of food .

Event Duration: 2007.11.12——2007.11.30
Selection Duration: 2007.12.1-2007.12.3

Rewards:
The First Place(1): AOD+1 (draconic necklace)
The Second Place(2): Hell Pheonix Egg + Dragon Eye
The Third Place(2): Pheonix Egg + Dragon Bone
The Fourth(3): Lucky Egg

We hope our players can have fun in Myth War, especially on Thanksgiving Day. On behalf of IGG, we want to send our best wishes to you, and wish you all a Happy Thanksgiving Day and Holiday Season.

Myth War Online Team, IGG
