MythWar v2.0 Closed Beta Ends

Dear players,

The 7 day MythWar v2.0 Closed Beta will end at 5:30am EDT on June 20th. After the closed beta ends, all character information will be deleted.

The MythWar v2.0 Closed Beta has been very popular with many players since it began on June 13th. In order to return all players continued support and participation, all items in the item mall will be free from 5:30am EDT on June 19th to 5:30am EDT on June 20th. All players are welcome to try any item they want for free. Meanwhile, your suggestions and ideas are welcome as well.

Thanks for your participation and attention.

MythWar v2.0 Open Beta is coming soon, get ready!

Mythwar Online Team, IGG
