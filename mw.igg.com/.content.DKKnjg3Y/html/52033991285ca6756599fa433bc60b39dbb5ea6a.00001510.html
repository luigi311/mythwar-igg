Tips for Male Humans

Tips for people who wanna use Male Humans

This isnt some guide for what is the best build, what pets do i need etc,etc

This is just a few helpful hints Ive gained through my time in MW

I
First and foremost, realize your the most unneeded char in the game Sure its nice to have a good hummie in high lvl existent or in your

gw party. But when it comes to quests and lvling male humans for the most part arent needed and slow things down

II
It really helps you and speeds up your leveling if you take the initiative and time and learn to lead wood/bly and seals

Alot of parties dont need m humans but if you lead you will always have a party(especially seal alot of people dont like to lead)

III
Spamming is your best friend  any hummie is heavily reliant upon their skills. Spamming for m hummies is really easy and faster than any

other race. Hypno and Chaos IV 9 at least for wood/bly & seal. Hypno & Chaos III 10 for papa. Plus lvling frail is fun( its really fun to

frail then hear omgwtf I cant cast ). Plus finding a speed seal party for m hummies is few and far between. Unless you have good friends to do it with

IV
Humans are reliant upon there gears (esp. full agi 3ag1/1sta hummie. Your gonna need good chaos,melee sets. Fire is good for rd 10 and

higher lvl hurris and crit dont hurt for rd 15 and just making people mad  ,and it never hurts to have dodge and flash res for the cursed

belkers. If you want to be effective in gw or in high lvl existent you will need these gears.

V
Humans are very versatile dont get stuck on this build or that build just do what you want. If you stick with hummies can rock and be the main factor in many things. Just go with what you feel. After all its your char and you can do what you want with it. Always remeber have fun after all this is just a game


Thanks and hope it helps, feel free to add on or criticize. Any feedback is appreciated
