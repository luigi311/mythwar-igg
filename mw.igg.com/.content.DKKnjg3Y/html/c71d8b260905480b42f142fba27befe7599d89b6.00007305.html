Step 1: download Gigaget Installation Program

  1. click on “Gigaget Software”;

  2. a window will pop up, click on “save”;  

  3. choose a folder to save the installation program then click on “save”.



Step 2: install Gigaget Software
(1) after the download is finished, click on “Run”;


(2) click on “Next”;


(3) read over the Agreement carefully then choose “ I accept the agreement” and click on “Next”;


(4) click on “Browse…” to choose the folder that you want to install the program in then click on “Next”;


(5) click on “Next”;


(6) click on “Install” to install the program;


(7) Click on “Finish”.


Step 3: Use Gigaget to download Game Client
(1) Put your mouse on one Game Client link, right click your mouse, choose “Download by Gigaget”;


(2) A download window will pop up; choose a folder in which you want to save the file, then click on “OK”.


After the download is finished, you can close the Gigaget software and find the game client to install it.
