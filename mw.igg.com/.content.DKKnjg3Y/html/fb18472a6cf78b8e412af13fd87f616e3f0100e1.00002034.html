Duan (Help others) Quest

Ok here is a basic guid to this quest.
Rewards are gold and Super Dragon Stone which can be used for making +13 equips.
1st Step: Go pay Duan a visit in Demon Square. He will tell you which npc to visit next. From then on, its a csae of visit each npc and perform which ever task they set you.
All tasks are easy to do and involve either of the following:
Deliver a msg,
Deliver an item,
Fight a battle.
The items will be either given to you by the npc or will be a teleport scroll. The teleport scrolls are usually the team teleport scrolls and can be obtained from the grocer next to Duan in Demon. There are other grocers that sell them, but this one is more convenient as you will return here a few times.
The battles are fairly easy and can be easily done alone after lvl 20.
2nd, empty your inv except for the woodlinger scroll and go stock up on a few normal teleport scrolls and at least one of each team teleport. Don't worry if you can't afford all these as you can buy them as you progress through the quest.
The areas you will visit most will be Demon Square and Snow Ridge. Demon square costs 1500 from Wood tele, so it makes sense to log a teleport scroll to take you back there and always keep one logged. Snow ridge is a walk through revive, so again, to save time, keep a teleport scroll logged for that area. Other areas will usually be Southern Riv and sunset and Bly. But these are quick and easy to get to.
Also you might want to use tears for quick walking to certain npc's in SunSet and Southern. Thats upto you, but not much time is saved, cause battles are fairly quick to despatch.
ok so now you have your inv full of scrolls (save one empty slot) and your 1st destination. Off you go and do 100 help quests.
If at any time you get lost or forget what you are upto due to quest log not showing all the info or for whatever reason, then goto Duan's Apprentice next to him in Demon Square and she will give you the full coords and update.
It took me about 3 hrs to complete.
After wards I had made about 1 million gold and received the dragon stone. So for an early level quest, its quite rewarding.
Last tip. If you have a bad memory, make a note of where each npc is as you find them. This will help you when returning back later on.
Thought I'd add a bit more info. A lot of people are discussing this message as displayed below.

Other than the humourous side of it. You actually need to take the correct level of sex with you to obtain the reward for that particular trip. You can go without and get no reward and carry on the Duan quest as normal.
Unfortunately for me, at the time when I got these messages, the only gays in the village was Chuck and Cronny. I decided it was safer to carry on the quest alone
Good Luck
