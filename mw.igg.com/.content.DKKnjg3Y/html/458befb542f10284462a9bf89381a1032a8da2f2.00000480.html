China Earthquake Donation

One Global Community

--Disaster Relief from IGG is associated with the Red Cross Society of China

An earthquake measuring 7.9 on the Richter scale struck Wenchuan County in southwest China’s Sichuan Province on May 12th, 2008. The official death toll is more than 12,000, and looks set to rise sharply. (Click here for current news about the disaster)

We here at IGG call on all people, including our community of players, to help these people in the disaster-hit areas. You can help send them money via the Quake Card created by IGG. They need your help right now!

How can I get involved?
Quake Cards can be purchased with an additional 10% cost added to them, but 20% of the full purchase price will be sent directly to the Red Cross of China.

IGG will ensure that your contributions will be delivered to the disaster areas promptly and that the refugees will know that this aid is coming from all over the world. Let these poor people feel the love from all over the globe! Remember every little bit counts, no matter how small.

Let us work together as one global community to try and relieve the suffering and devastation, and help rebuild for the future.

Let’s come together, hand in hand, to eliminate this disaster with our love!

*Portions of the Quake Cards will be given to the Red Cross of China.
*Quake Cards are NOT refundable.
*All payment methods are available
