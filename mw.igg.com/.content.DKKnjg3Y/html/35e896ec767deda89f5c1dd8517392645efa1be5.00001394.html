yeah it's a matter of preference really. there is really no dominantly faster way to do r15. when i played i only spammed r14, going 15 only for fun, but i guess with increase in price of xp scrolls, going higher now makes a lot of sense. i guess maybe it depends on human/borg builds? agi builds probably tend to like being in safe stun then risking the mage attacks.

for the stun-borg strategy, only human and borgs need to be chaos/stun resist. or you can forget about any resist and use xmas pets combined appropriately with the cent, since the humans are slow. it takes only a couple of turns to kill 6 humans when your pets don't need to be melee resist. so chances of screwing up is pretty small (bigger if human/borg is agi build) then switch to melee resist one just before borg comes out. oh, and leave one human/mage alive just in case. i found it's easier to just hypno iii the last thing than trying to deplete its mp.

wait-in-stun strategy has something i don't like... some of the pets will be dead those that are too slow to get stunned... will be killed by drain and evil/flash in no time, while getting chaosed (the enemy don't target chaos on stunned targets) i know that's why people use bloodpede and such but still not my personal style to let anything die.

my personal opinion is f.borg > m.borg. sorry. there are only two differences: (1) enhance vs protect, and (2) natural resist. i found protect is very important in most high-end fights while physical attack is not used. survival is a one big example. the new enemies from r17 on have very high dodge.
