Google Checkout Available Again

Dear players,


We are glad to announce that Google Checkout is once again available, so that players can now buy their Item Mall Cards there. And to celebrate we are holding an event from April 19th to April 29th EST (GMT-5), where all players can obtain an extra 10% of points when they purchase their Item Mall Cards in this way.


For more information about this event visit here please.


In addition, the end of April is quickly approaching so we will be holding a big sale for all of our valued players from April 23rd to April 27th EST (GMT-5). We will offer a 20% discount on all items (excluding the Power Pack) in the Item Mall.


We hope this sale will bring you many must have bargains and a few exclusive treats for you and your pets. Have fun!
