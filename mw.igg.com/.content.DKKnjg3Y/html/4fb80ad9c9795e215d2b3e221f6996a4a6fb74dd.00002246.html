Myth War II PK Tournament

Rules: The PK Tournament is conducted among individuals or between teams formed by at lease three teammates.
Event duration:
Individual Competition: Starts at 4:00pm August 23rd (Server Time)
Team VS Team: Starts at 4:00pm August 24th (Server Time)
Event details:
Individual Competition:
1.Tryout
Finding GM: GMs will announce the start to tryouts. Four GMs will hide themselves in four different cities. Four players who are the first to find the GMs and team up with them will be qualified to take part in the individual competition and will receive two 1.2M Exp Mystical Fruits and Fireworks apiece.
2.  These four players then go to City Hall and start the competition. Players and their pets are not allowed to use any skills, only physical attacks. The winning players from each round will continue to the next round of competition until a champion is decided.
3.  Players who use skills will be disqualified.
4.  GMs will announce who the final winner is in the system channel and release fireworks.

Team VS Team
1.Tryout
Finding a GM: GMs will ask players to team up with at least three players every half hour before the tryout starts. Four GMs will hide themselves in four cities. The four teams that are first to find the GMs and give each of them a rose will be qualified for team competition and get two 1.2M Exp Mystical Fruits and Fireworks apiece.
2. These four teams will then go to City Hall to start the competition. Teammates are allowed to use skills. The winning teams each round keep entering into the next round of competition until a champion is decided.
3.  GMs will announce who the final winning team is in the system channel and release fireworks

Prize:
 Champion: 100,000 Talent Points
Runner up: 50,000 Talent Points
