Exist 101

Soooo you nooblet always die at exist? fear not, I come to save u after reading this guide and understanding (hopefully u do meet the minimum requirements). This is how I do exist in ach, your server's strategies might differ a little less or be the complete opposite. Also I know best for cent, other races slightly less (hey guess what my main char is and I give u a cookie). Also this is for advanced ppls which I assume that meet the minimum...and hope to finish r15 with all limbs attached.

The trick to exist is to wear good res and high lvl protect 4. There's no hacks, hidden knowledge etc. Just wear good res and be protected.

OMG! what res to have?!?!
You'll need 2 big main res sets: melee (#1 priority) and fire. Both should be 65+ res so that with f borg protect u get over 100 res and attks do 1 dmg Wear criti weapon with more rate than hit if u dream of r15. Can use criti necky for r15 too, as long as armor + helm provide you enough melee. Also cent recommended to have 1 piece of chaos for r11, 12
Team suggestions
1 cent + 1 mage + 3 borg (at least 1 f borggy with good protect 4). Eventually u can replace a borg with mage...
Ppl usually don't use hummy in ach At least I never did...wanted to take a friend once but she went away If you do use hummy tell me what skills to use in what round as I absolutely have no clue. max round I survived with hummy was 9, since I always died at r10 no matter what until a guildie teached me.
[quote=Smokey]Humie and Fem borg faster than everything each round. Fastest fem borg naturally casting pro 4[/quote]

Skill requirements:
Cent: Heal 2 lvl 10+
Mage: Fire/Flash IV lvl 11 *not rly sure*
Borg: Drain lvl 9+ (all borg lvl 9 drain not recommended for higher rounds), Protect 4 lvl 10/11

Round 8

Resist: melee sets
Very very simple. Mage cast skill, borgs drain bee, cent heal and hummy do c3 on bee (if without set).

Round 9
*will make screenie*
Resist: 2 fire 1 melee
Spirit cats cast fire 3/4, skeletal stun, chaos, poison or melee, bee melee.
Rather easy if u know what ure doing.

Round 10
Here is where the fun starts. You'll start to rely on protect from now on. Hope the f borg isn't n00b or else you risk free tele.
Mob: 1 archer (Soulless biter), 3 skeletal, 6 spirit cat. Side notes: kitty got some res to mage skill if u really noob it will be a pain in the ass. Archer
biter does multi 3 or 4, skelly and cats same.
Res to wear: fire set. Some ppl choose 2 fire/1 melee but I like full fire as cent since

Round 11
With 6 skeletals, 3 archers and 1 snake demon melee set and a bit of chaos to cent is a must.
Snakey does drain 3 or 4 which hurts alot
With protect and melee set u should have no problems.
[quote=Smokey]humie uses Chaos 3 on snake, then each archer 1100+ speed = guaranteed outcast of snake[/quote]

Round 12
6 Archers, 3 snake demon, and 1 evil axewalker.
Again with your leet set and prot 4 the only threat might be a lil bana burn from drain but not a major problem. Poison axewalker if you can, but it's not a must.
[quote=Smokey]Again humie with the Chaos 3 to each snake demon, poison pets all of which hitting Axe with P3. See above with speed [/quote]

Round 13
This round is pure EVIL! 6 drainers! OMFGWTF! Thankfully the 3 axewalkers aren't gonna be a big pain either. Here the first char looking mob appears: Body mangler (m hummy non rb) which uses good chaos 4 and stun 4, sometimes melee. A poison pet is a must. A poison 3 from a pede first round will kill the hummy before he can bark. Also speed tankers are a must to take the drain dmg away from you. Keep cent away from drain at all cost. well...maybe not kill the team for the cent but make him slower than the rest.
Also this is where the 3 borgs come in play. 3 drain 4 + mage skill will nuke the snakes. If you want to kill them bfore they cast, their average speed is 1050-1100 speed. An agi borg or 2 really help. Press on the attack and hope for the best. Also replace cent chaos piece with melee. There isn't much time in the first turn to protect, and he/she needs to survive. Alternatively, your fastest f borg can cast protect on her 2nd turn but not recommended by me.
[quote=Smokey]Humie casting stun 4, poison pets hitting a P4 off (if 3 poison pets out each target different axe) +1 crit from a pet = dead humie kill off axes, 2x drain 2x poison 2x fire they should die, snakes will come out, 2 drains and fire they should be dead[/quote]

Round 14
Aaaahhh easy...somehow.
1 Death stalker using flash and evil 3 with consideralby high dmg, 3 hummy as the one in round 13 and 6 evil axewalker.
4 poison pets are a definitely must. Each one should target with poison 3 a hummy or the mage. In  round or two after being poisoned they should die. After you've poisones them all it's 80% done. All that's left is to kill axes. Can spam poison 4 on them to make the ownage faster.
[quote=Smokey]If male human cast frailty 4 try and run the humans out of mana use pets to poison the axes with the pets, Will go quick provided humies dont decide to stun . poison to humies, mage +1 crit from a pet and each of them will die . If male human drain + crit should do it [/quote]

Round 15
You will need crt weapon (alternatively necky too) like hell.
1 killer raven (m borg), does drain 4 and insanely high dmg. Immune to melee, magic, drain, poison (well almost immune) and over 3 mil hp. But hey, who said u can't kungfu and crit him?
3 death stalkers shouldn't be a problem as long as you poison 3 each one in first round.
6 body mangler?!?! omg! don't even think about chaos set! Melee and crit all the way! Did you notice exist mobs have limited mp? well these dudes aren't exactly with a big mp pool. Wait under your stun while they finish the mp and death stalker is poisoned to death. F borg should be first out of stun to cast protect. You may ask: OMG! 300k dmg from borg and it's all over! think again, with protect and melee set he will hit u 1 dmg. While the f borg is unharmed by the meleeing humans she should protect other teammates when they come out of stun. Human ownage time. After they all dead turn your attention to the raven doing criti to him with chars (protected chars ofc).
Here is where a hummy with good poison/frail shines in your team. A frail 4 first hound (supposedly you don't get chaosed and killed) and manglers will be finished sooner. Hgh lvl poison even better...hey you might even hit the borg with it! it will last 1-2 turns but what the heck it's worth it xD
[quote=Smokey]If your super lucky death hits first turn and you get stunned, by the time your out borg is dead.....if only it worked that way....Male human is frailing away, poison pets using 3 skill on mages..... Fem human poison, just have pets over poison the mages.... Frail 1 and Poison 1 tend to hit borg moreso than 3 skills..... In most cases your sitting and waiting for the humans to cast themselves outa mana....yes they do run out eventually.....fem human should be constant set to poison, male human constant frail, re-poisoning or re-frailing each time out of stun does help it along.....Having to crit the borg your looking at something like 22 crits to kill.

Humies do help....Blood party isnt needed so long as you are quick enough to outcast the mobs. 1100+ will outcast everything but the male borg at r15.  3 borgs is definitely faster with the drain, but humie is always nice to have, you'll need them for other things so be buddy buddy with them[/quote]

Round 16+17 guide
[quote=meaw]general tip for slow teams fighting multiple snakes i.e. when you don't agi borg / agi human but still want to go to r15 -> know your team turn order and pot to someone slower than you. potting yourself like nooblets is a suicide. say your f.borg is a blood build but still got drained dry. use fast pet or a whatever faster to pot her then protect iv up will save you a free tele.

on r13, fast pets + nuking + cent no shoe is one of the best strategy. but borgs need to be sure about mp (esp those with high drain iv). if your team fail to kill any of the snakes, you will be in trouble. if that happens, don't panic and try potting strategy above.

also a note on chaos iii snake. it does NOT always work. it's best to have max lvl 11, but even so it can miss. be sure you can handle if it happens.

you can spam r15 without human but r16+17 is not recommended without one. with human, r1-15 is not that much slower, but of course humans need to know what to do and the team must coordinate accordingly.  but after 17, humans are useless again. if you are looking to a smooth run passing r17, might want to plan to switch out human for other race (with the trick Smokey mentioned).

my take on 16 is:

6 f.mages. 3 m.borgs. 1 f.human.

new enemy: f.human is SUPER FAT. seriously. you can cap drain damage on her for many times and after it goes below max damage, it only goes down so slowly you want to cry. she has some drain resist too, that's what makes it slow. you might need like 20-30 drains before you can crit to kill her off. there are 2 ways to kill her. (1) luck on death. (2) drain festival. she does poison iv and chaos iv mostly. melee sometimes and it hurt. it's not like m.borg but it's still strong and have CRIT. so it's advisable to maintain high hp of your pets at all times.

what they will do: first know that you are likely to be drained in the beginning. you might be lucky some m.borgs might enhance iv. you might be unlucky it goes one-hit melee kill some important stuff. follows by f.mages spike stuff here and there (i.e. dangerous for squishy things) and then f.human goes poison iv and if unlucky chaos iv.

party: one human. one f.borg. one m.cent. one mage. another one can be anything but likely you will use a borg from previous rounds. human must max stun iv. squishy build is not recommended (besides mage obviously). everyone using reborn borg ssps will help a ton. but it's not absolutely needed.

resist: mage is full melee or melee/crit. usually this is leader so might want to wear it from beginning. cent use full chaos. borg and human use any combination of melee/crit/chaos. i recommended melee/chaos so with protect up you melee/chaos 100+. for weapon you can go crit.

first couple rounds to survive: need one human you want your human and f.borg to go after everything but the enemy f.human. something like 1-2 agi build at max level. some fast tank pets. priority: (1) protect iv up first turn (2) cast stun iv. since you can be drained up to three times, it's not likely you will have any mp to cast protect and stun. you need to use fast tanks to pot. if those pets unluckily die. human pots borg to protect or borg pots human to stun. at least get one thing done otherwise it's a high potential you will be screwed up in no time (this is why squishy build is not recommended as it increases chance of dying from mage sniping you). the cent should be slower than f.human. and heal or purge accordingly. now you will survive and can start killing things

to kill: after protect is up and your team is stable. i.e. replacing dead pets, etc. kill the f.mages with drain and poison while keeping borgs in stun (which means stun iv EVERY turn, it also helps to rotate target of the stun around. the target will stay 2 turns in stun, while other only 1 turn.). this should be fast. cent should counteract the f.human, which shouldn't be too bad as it will poison to override chaos from time to time. next is to stop stun iv and use hypno iii on single target. you should be able to keep 3  out of 4 things hypnoed most of the time. mage goes death iv. it's easy to hit at least one. when it hits, human stops hypno that one and let it die from death while mage try to death iii another one. with some patient you will kill most of them with death. then you can switch to crit the last borg or drain-fest the human while waiting for another to die out of death. this has been tried with lvl 10 death iv not perfect reborn, so it's quite reliable.

too lazy to write about higher rounds but r17 is pretty much the same strategy except it's much more complicated on the "survive first couple rounds" part. reborn borg ssp is a must for r17 tho.[/quote]

Info of even higher rounds mobs by meaw
[quote=meaw]r17 - m.cent. multi iv most of the times. sometimes melee. low hp. resist to all human skills. likely to be faster than everything besides full agi builds.

r18 - f. borg. drain iv most of the times. immune to all human skills. i would think this is the fastest monster in the game, or at least as fast as the m.borg. bad news is it always drains and can't be stunned or hypnoed.

r19 - f. cent. regularly bliz iv, speed iv, AND heal iv. sometimes melee. heal itself around 5 millions so it's super fat all right. resist to all human skills.

r20 - m.mage. if you manage to get this far, i don't want to spoil the thrill for you. oh no info

if you can get past r17, then 18-19 won't be a problem. since I tell you what they do, you can wear resist accordingly to not die. to kill them.. it's almost impossible without using death.[/quote]
