 I never understood why adding 1 agi stat point per lvl to cent was needed. Cents have good agi growth, their shoes give great speed bonus and they will be faster than any other race with similar gear and that have no(t too much) agi added. 2 sta/2 int cent is more than manageable for pvm provided you know what you're doing. Full speed cents might work for GW as they will out cast anything and cast speed on the party, thus gaining a cast advantage. But speed isn't everything in GW. Full int cents do great damage but the major downside is they only have 1 type of attack so gearing up against them is easy.

No one says anything about agi mages anymore. What's the point in doing more damage if the other mages kills you before you move? But there are drawbacks to everything, more hp, less damage and speed, more damage, less hp and speed, more speed, less hp and damage and prone to dying unless you have a suited array of pets in your party.

Also int on borgs is kinda tabu for some reason when it really should be str. Make str pets, not str chars, at least not for mains. There are so many drawbacks to adding lots of str that the satisfaction of killing one char/pet in 1 hit once in 10 battles won't make you feel very useful the other 9.

Having only speed humans can be a real drag if you're not fighting noobs, otherwise it's just fine and enjoyable. A sta human can be great for just about anything and if you have chaos resist, there is little they can do to stop you from castinging. Eventually.

Keep an open mind and work out what you need and what suits you. Maybe you might even (re)discover something that works better than the established guidelines. What's the fun of playing when you're listening to everyone and not to yourself?

 I do not see the point of recommending a full agi centaur. A centaur's purging skill is only useful once the enemy human had cast chaos/stun there is no point in having it to be faster then humans. If however, a centaur will be able to kill humans before they can cast, that is another story but making it full agi means it will have weak firepower. Yes, it will be interesting. However, you will have a hard time finding matching members and pets to party this centaur. While in human, their spells have a lasting effect which justify them being fast, as 1 single cast can last a few rounds, in a centaur, it is not the case. Anyway, a party of 4 agi based centaur can be a different story though, hahaha. Surely with all 4 multishot, blizzard, the human will be killed, unless, got forbid, he uses, melee resist, hahaha. But then again, you don't need a full agi centaur to be faster than a full agi human. A full agi mage and a full agi borg are both faster than a full agi human.

Yes, full int centaurs are great killers. Hahaha, I think I am one of the first, if not the first full Int centaur around and when I was building to be full int, people ask if I am crazy. Anyway, full int centaur have just the same HP as a full agi centaur.

In the end, it is all mix and match, really. Yes, why not a stamina based mage? Why not a full str borg? People telling about the need to make sure the centaur have enough stamina to survive, what about it needing to have enough MP to cast or other characters having enough stamina to survive? Surely, every members need to survive? Yes, a full sta mage can be made faster than a full int centaur anf if needed, the mage can actually pot the centaur a banana so that it can heal. Surely, that is logical and yes, a full stamina mage do have the ability to survive better compared to a full stamina centaur simply because they have better natural resistense to the most common killing skills, a mage's skill.
