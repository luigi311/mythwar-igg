They say the game is free, but in retrospect the game isn't free, it is masked as being free with the extra incentive of the item mall. In a way this game can rub players the wrong way when it comes to the item mall. Ever since joining this game well mw1 and onwards most updates are aimed towards using the item mall.

They do these events (or call them events) where they offer 30% - 50% off just to entice players into spending theie money, they could really care less about the players themselves the main concern is getting the money of players. It is very rare to see any "decent" event where you don't actually feel the need to use the item mall or go out and attempt to buy ingots\mp from other players.

Sure other games restrict you to certain equipment, certain areas however those games also have multiple methods of earning money f2p and p2p alike. With Igg and it's games the ability to make money is fairly low (to my knowledge best method in mw2 is to do duan which takes quite a bit of time) Sure you can also do guild quests and envoy quests but the gold you get is slim.

I use the item mall on few occasions, but I don't think that Igg should single out mall buyers and normal players, if they did it would just mean they only care about the money and nothing else.

Everybody should be entitled to something even if they don't use the item mall...
