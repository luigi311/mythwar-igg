Discover the Power of IGGems!

What is an IGGem?  IGGem is short for IGG-Gem. It's your key to purchasing power you've never had before. IGG will be launching a new credit system just in time for Christmas. When purchasing an IGG Point Card, players will get corresponding IGGems which can be exchanged for game credits . Furthermore, players can do shopping in the Credit Mall’s of most games or even buy some material items at low prices in the upcoming IGGem Store. If you want to check your IGGem, you can login IGGem System on December 23rd.

Players will obtain certain IGGems in accordance with the purchase of different IGG Points Cards. During the Christmas event period(December 18th,2008 to January 5th, 2009), players will get an additional 20% of IGGems as long as players purchase a Points Card. NOTE:We will launch a new IGGems exchange system which will enable you to convert IGGems into MW Integral with the ration set at 1:1. You can use them to take part in Fortune Bombing and  Auction Center.
