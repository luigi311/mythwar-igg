  Be Friendly to Newbie Event
Event Duration: 0:00 Server Time Nov 21 -- 23:59 Server Time Dec 10

Event Contents: GMs will disguise themselves as a newbie from Nov 21, 2007 to Dec 10, 2007. We will randomly log in newbie accounts (not the obvious GMs' accounts) and ask newbie questions in world chat (example, how much skill experience is needed for skill level 6, or where is xxx NPC). We will see who answers politely with the CORRECT answer and take down their IDs silently, without telling them that we are GMs right away. GMs can also create level 50 newbie and ask innocently for players to help them complete the quest for Skills III & IV. At the end of this event, these helpful players will be announced on the official website to go and claim their rewards from the GMs.

Rewards: 50 integrals for each CORRECT, POLITE answer, 1 Awesome Egg or 1 Devil Egg or 1 Miraculous Egg for helping to do the quest for Skills III & IV.

Myth War Online Team, IGG
