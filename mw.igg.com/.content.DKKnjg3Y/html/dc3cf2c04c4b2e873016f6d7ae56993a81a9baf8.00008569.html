Myth War “All Hallows Eve” Celebration

Dear players,

Halloween is approaching like a creeping ghost in a dark room! Here at Myth War, the Halloween Celebration is also coming! This time with an interesting mission to entertain you---"MW World Ghosts Cleanup" is waiting for your participation!

MW World Ghosts Cleanup

Background:

Recently, some vicious insects from the Darkness of Space have secretly entered MW World and are hiding in all the filthy corners of all the world. They are mosquitoes, centipedes, spiders and roaches. Their arrival has caused the spread of terrible plagues and diseases which are now becoming a huge threat to the people of MW World! Feel a cough coming on? In order to get rid of these unwelcome intruders, the elders in Woodlingor are calling up all brave warriors to search and fight against them. Players must first go to Desert City （265.344）, buy an Enchanted Whisker from the Broom Lord, then find the insects and dispose of them. At the same time, players may find poor harmless Furby Worms. If players can help it, they will be given more rewards.


Activity time: 10.31——11.6
                        Everyday 10：00--17：00， 18：00—23：00


Participation:

1. Find the NPC（265.344）in Desert City to buy an Enchanted Whisker, which allows players to search for the insects.
2. Players should team up, with a team consisting of at least 3 members.
3. Defeat monsters or complete relevant quests.
4. Get rewards!

GM Interaction online

Druggist's trick

On the day of Halloween, when zombies, skeletons and wolfmen are celebrating this yearly festival, the druggist (GM) cannot help but to participate in the celebrations with them. He will make good use of a special lotion costing him the whole year to develop.

Activity time: October 31st

Activity description: Druggist (GM) will send players to the terror zone (mob-killing map). Players will get magic rewards by completing the mission given by the druggist. If players don’t want to complete the mission, tell the GM that you do not want to participate in this activity. The GM will send you back. Great mission and wonderful rewards are waiting for you!

The Possession

Halloween is the time for departed souls to prowl for bodies to inhabit. So players should be very careful. Experienced ghosts may be waiting for you.

Activity time: November 1st

Activity description: GMs will become ghosts to give you magic presents, such as experience. You have to be online on the activity day to get presents. Don’t miss the chance.

Trick or Treat

Trick or Treat, give me something good to eat…like YOU!

Activity time: November 2nd

Activity description: On the day of the activity, GMs will select players at random and give them two pieces of news. One is good and the other is bad. Which one do you want to know first? Usually, the good news will be told you first, which is the GM will give you a present. And then the bad news... The bad news is you have to give the GM a present before receiving the GM's present. If you refuse, you can also get the GM's present by challenging them successfully.

Forum activity

Halloween Mask Design

Passionate music and gorgeous stage designs offer you a rare chance to escape away from the pressures of work and a boring daily routine. Just relax yourself on this haunted night. You can dress up in bizarre costumes to be a witch, princess or a vampire, and dance with strangers.
The Masked Ball is coming. Have you prepared your mask? Act immediately to design your own Halloween costume. Use your imagination and design your own Halloween mask which stands for the image of Myth War.

Activity time: Oct. 25th – Nov. 20th

Selection time: Nov. 20th -Nov. 23rd

Requirements:
1. The mask shall be related to Halloween
2. Make use of different colors and character images in Myth War to design the mask
3. Name the mask you design

Reward:
The first(1): Miraculous Egg + Saint Flask
The second(2):：Peculiar Whammy Egg + Intelligent Flask II
The third(2) ：Fantastic Whammy Egg+ Reborn Class Flask

Content and Rules:
1：All competitors should be MW players.
2：Contents of characteristic signature should be related to MW game. Contents should not be
referred to any sensitive topics, such as politics, extreme violence or sexuality. Otherwise the
competitors will be deprived of contest qualification.
3: Image Size:400X400 px 256KB(maximum) Format:JPG,GIF
4: All works must be originated by competitors and should be sent to appointed activity place
and titled with "MASK". Otherwise the competitors will be deprived of contest
qualification.
5: An official committee will be generated to select the excellent works.
6: The ownership of all works are reserved by IGG
