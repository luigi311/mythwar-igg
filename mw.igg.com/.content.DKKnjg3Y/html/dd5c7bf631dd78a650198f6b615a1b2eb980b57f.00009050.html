How to use pets?

Basic Usage
For every master, he/she can:
name their pets;
have the pets follow them in game;
distribute the remained attribute points for their pets;
set pets to combat with them in battles;
use the stone of loyalty to increase the loyalty of their pets.
resume their pets (normal ones only) into eggs and save them in the inventory or bank.

Follow
Open your pet stat window, choose a pet then click on the follow button and the pet will follow you. You can have as many pets to walk or fly after you as you wish. See:


Battle
Open your pet stat window, choose a pet and click on the battle button and the pet will join you when you are in battle. Remember, in every time you can only have one pet to assist you; so please choose the most capable one according to different circumstances.


When in the battle mode, you can select the way to let your pet attack, either by physical or skill attack. Or to use heal or mana potion to heal itself or the players.

Pets Control Panel:


Defense button: when the pet is used to defense the player it will help player to resist some damage from the monster.

Dodge button: when the dodge button is used, the pet will dodge from the attacker.

Change button: for changing pets during battle.

Train & Leveling
Like the main characters, pets can also realize level up, some can even get reborn, and in this sense it’s quite a good idea to find a good way to train them.

Normally, pets can level up by joining in battles. After each victory they will get certain experience points, every time when the points reach certain amount, the pets can get one level up, until reaching the maximum.
When the pet grows up 1 level, it will get 4 points which can be freely assigned. Owner of the pet can add the stats to how he wants it to become; that is to say, player can choose different ways to raise the pet by deciding how its stats points are added.



STA: Affects Health.
INT: Affects Mana.
STR: Affects Attack.
AGI: Affects Speed.


Since different pets have different outstanding features, so it’s better to know them well before adding the points, for the detailed features of the pets, you can refer to the Specific Information section.
