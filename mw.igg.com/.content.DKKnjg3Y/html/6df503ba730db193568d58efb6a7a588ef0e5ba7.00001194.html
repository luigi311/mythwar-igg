I took on the tremendous task of typing this in english. I did reword it quite a bit, but I tried my best to stick close to the original message.

Rule of thumb: It should never be easier to write your message than it is to read it.

Credit goes to platyping aka EluneGrace for writing this definitive guide

Centaur

Strength
Strength is nearly useless for centaurs. Its only use is to help your character meet equipment requirements. Newly reborn characters with low strength will not be able to wear the armor of dreams or meet certain equipment requirements.

Agility
Centaurs are naturally faster than other characters of similar experience. Therefore agility will only be needed for player killing.

Stamina
Stamina will increase your amount of health. People whom raise only their stamina could have over one hundred thousand health at level one hundred, but their amount of magic will suffer. They will have trouble casting Heal IV at levels ten and eleven. They will also be an easy target when frailed or drained. Having one hundred thousand health, but little magic will not help your party. People whom raise three stamina and one intelligence per level will do more damage with blizzard, but will only have a mediocre increase in their amount of magic. Adding an extra one hundred intelligence will not even increase your amount of magic by five thousand. Heal II at level ten costs five thousand three hundred magic, so  adding one extra point per level to intelligence just to increase your amount of magic will be a waste, since it will not even give you enough magic to cast one extra level ten Heal II.

Intelligence
Adding a lot of intelligence will give you the ability to do massive damage with blizzard. The lack of stamina will cause you to have little health though. Only build your character like this if you have great defensive equipment, or if you plan on using blizzard and not healing.



Borg

Strength
Strength is needed for equipment requirements and the armor of dreams. Strength is also useful for one versus one player killing matches. It is especially useful for male norgs with a high enhance skill, and even more useful for reborn norgs with berserk, critical, and pierce bonuses. However since player killing is just for fun and has no effect on quests or guild wars, most players do not add much strength.

Agility
The most advantageous speed for a borg is slower than centaurs and faster than mages. You want to be slower than centaurs so you can be revived and not miss your turn and you want to be faster than mages so you can drain them before they can use any of their skills. Borgs whom have had only agility added will work great in player killing matches and guild wars because they can drain their opponents before they can do attempt any kind of resistence.

Stamina
You need stamina to increase your amount of health, which will keep you alive. The amount of stamina that you should add is up to you.

Intelligence
Most borgs rely on level 9 Drain IV and don't increase their intelligence. If you plan on using levels 10 or 11 Drain IV, than you will need to add some intelligence or get some equipment that increases your amount of magic.


Mage

Strength
The only reason to add strength is to meet the armor of dreams requirement.

Agility
If you want to move before your opponents in guild wars or player killing, then you could add agility. Other than that the only reason to increase your agility is to use some rare weapons.

Stamina
While it is important to have a lot of health, every point you add into stamina will be one less point that you could have added into intelligence.

Intelligence
A lot of mages add only intelligence. This will cause them to do the most possible damage and have the high possible amount of magic, but their amount of health will suffer. They can even be killed by enemies repelling their own attacks. Others add three intelligence and one stamina per level. They will have more health, but have less magic and do  less damage. It is up to you to decide which you value more, the amount of health you have, or the amount of damage you do to your opponents.


Human

Strength
Strength is useful for player killing, and for meeting equipment requirements.

Agility
Adding only agility is great for player killing guild wars, and certain quests. You will be able to disable the opposing team before they can do the same to you. The human whom uses his skills first will be victorious in most situations. However there will still be some enemies that move faster than a human who adding only agility. Your amount of health will also suffer.

Stamina
Stamina is important as always since it increases your amount of health. Adding only stamina will give you a lot of health, but you will be slower than other humans and probably get disabled in player killing, guild wars, and high level quests. Even with the best equipment possible you cannot become immune to all disabling skills. Some add three agility and one stamina. They will be very fast, but not as fast as people who only add agility. They will have more health though, which will help the survive.

Intelligence
You will not need intelligence unless you want to be able to cast your high level skills even after being drained or frailed.
