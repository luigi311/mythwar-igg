Kung Fu Panda






Event duration：
From 4:00am July 24th,2008 to 11:59pm July 31st,2008


Event rules
Players level 30 and above
In every 24 hour period (from 12:00 midnight), players can complete a maximum of 100 quests, whether as an individual or in a family. Each time you complete a quest, you will be provided with a handsome reward.

Event introduction
Po is a genuine kung fu fanatic. He learned kung fu from the red panda Master Sifu and the tortoise Master Oogway. Finally he defeated the evil snow leopard warrior Tai Lung who escaped from prison and returned to threaten the Valley of Peace and became the true Dragon Warrior. It seems now that people in the Valley of Peace now can live happily forever. But the evil forces will forever return to threaten our peace. They are now going to the Rarus Continent and intend to wreak havoc there. Po and his two masters will come to help people living in the Rarus Continent and offer rewards to any participating players.

Event details
Event 1: Toil for gains:
Players can accept quests from Sifu at Luna's Palace (x 49, y58) during the event period The Gift Delivery quest The Killing quest The Quiz Show quest The Message Delivery quest Boss Fighting quest（Players can accept this quest to kill a Boss whenever they complete any of the quests above 10 times.）
Event 2: Homeland Defense
Evil snow leopards will attack the cities every hour and wreak havoc among people living there. They will appear everywhere outside the cities All participating players must work hard to defeat them.
Rewards
EXP
Talent
Material
Gem（lvl 4-lvl 12）
Deity Pet
