Mid-Autumn Festival Carnival!

 

Hello everyone,

Mid-Autumn Day Background:

For those of you unfamiliar with Mid-Autumn Day, it is an important traditional festival among Southeast Asian nations including, China, Japan, Korea, Vietnam, Malaysia, and Singapore. The festival falls on the 15th day of the 8th month on the Lunar Calendar. At this time, the moon is at its fullest and brightest, marking an ideal time to celebrate the abundance of the summers harvest. Traditionally, on this day, family members and friends will gather to admire the bright harvest moon, and share their happiness with tea and traditional sweets known as "mooncakes". It is always one of the most joyful days for people of this region!  
And this time, Myth War Online would like to bring an exciting activity to all players in Myth War World, to celebrate the pleasant day and share happiness with players around the world! The name of this activity is "Rescue the Moon Goddess"For the details, please read underneath.
Rescue the Moon Goddess

Activity Background:

Some insidious evil lord has gained control of the Wolfman Tribe by using magic and has kidnapped the Goddess of the Moon, who symbolizes romance and peace, and taken her to the dark cave. She is there now, guarded by snarling packs of Wolfmen.
With the Goddess of the Moon locked up, Mid-Autumn Day lacks the traditional bright moonlight it is known for and the whole world has fallen under a cloak of darkness. In order to launch an attack on the glittering city of Blython, the evil lord and his followers are sure to take advantage of the impenetrable twilight to try and sack the city.
  
They must not succeed!
Blython needs every brave warrior it can summon to rescue the Goddess of the Moon, frustrate the evil lord's plot and bring the moon back over the city to illuminate the darkness. Each player involved will gain a rich reward for killing every single Wolfman team.
When the players get rid of all the Wolfmen, they can rescue the Goddess of the Moon and receive one mooncake each from her as simple thanks for their bravery!

Activity Time:

The activity will be held at 9:00, 10:00, 11:00, 14:00, 15:00, 16:00, 20:00, 21:00 respectively from September 12th to September 16th.

Participation:

Go to Woodlingor and find the NPC Cave Guide (458, 788), then you'll be transported into the Wolfman Cave to enjoy the excitement!
