Promotion Event in Myth War for Christmas

Dear players,



Christmas is fast approaching, and to thank all of our players for their continued support of the game, we’ve arranged many exciting events. So without further ado let us introduce the Promotional Event Diary:



Event Duration: Midnight EST Dec 24th to 23:59 EST Dec 26th



Event Contents:We will be holding a promotional event. From Midnight, Dec 24th to 23:59 Dec 26th, all of the goods in the Item Mall (Except for the “Buy One Get One Free” items) will be 20% off. Don’t miss this opportunity to grab a bargain!



All players can participate in the activity.



May you a happy Christmas!
