Other Ideas

I would also like to see medium and large copper and gold ores in the item mall. Trying to make those ores costs too much redeem points for so small results. I spent 10k redeem points trying to make big ores and only came away with 4. That's a high price to pay for just 4 big copper ores. Imagine how much I will have to pay to get the gold ores.

I know a lot of people who have been playing the game since it started don't like the idea of adding good items such as +13 gems, medium and large ores, deity or saint pets, AoDs and such, but the fact is this game has become hugely a disadvantage for those who hasn't played the game that long. So I say put them in the item mall just to give others a chance to compete. Only certain people (special people) get the nice pets, AoDs and have now become so powerful no one can fight them. Yet, the game is supposed to be fair, but unfortunately not all the people are. Since the game is ruined already because of the costs of items and pets ingame that very few people can afford, why not allow them to get it through the item mall. Otherwise, increase the chances of getting such items in game. I like the idea of +13 gems in mall. That way I don't have to worry about paying 90+ mil for one +13 gem. People have become greedy in game and only those who have billions really have a chance of getting anything they want. Everyone else don't have a chance. So if people don't have enough game money to get these items why not allow them to use real money to get the same items. Then the balance of the game will be restored and anyone can become powerful.

Some people actually worked hard to get what they have and would disagree with what I am saying and I understand that. But many have scammed, hacked or was lucky enough to be given such items from friends that already had them and that's how they got to where they are. But not everyone is that fortunate. So I say put those items on the Mall IGG. If you want money that's the way to do it. Give the people what they want since the game has already lost its integrity. Sell all AoDs, deitys, and saint pets in item mall for 3000-10000 mall points. You can also sell resistances to counter crit, and drain. That should really lower the in game price of items making them more affordable.
