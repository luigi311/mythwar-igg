  The answer to the question of newly MW2
Dear players,
We have received a lot of concern and suggestions from players after revealing information of MW2.0 in advance. Surely, there are also some players questioning why we didn’t offer complete information about MW2.0. We want to thank those of you who pay close attention to MW. And we will also answer some of the questions and suggestions players presented as follows:

1.We are still negotiating with the game developer on some details of MW2.0, which has lead to the uncertainty of the exact release date of MW2.0. However, we anticipate MW2.0 will be released this winter. Regarding the schedule of MW2.0, we will release information about the introduction of the new edition in the notices section continuously. Please pay close attention to it.

2.Due to the difference of the current edition and MW2.0, we will have to adjust our forum and official website after releasing MW2.0.

3.We are still communicating with the game developer and testing whether or not we can or will transfer player data.

4.We have been also communicating with the game developer about whether to run the two different editions of MW simultaneously. How to maintain our servers and keep a balance between the two will be a big problem, which is beyond our ability right now. Look for updates about it as soon as the developers have decided.

5.Regarding suggestions on skills, we have submitted them to the game developer and also asked them to modify skills according to those suggestions. Just present your suggestions on game development for free.
6.Regarding various rumors about MW 2.0, please turn a deaf ear to all those except official updates and announcements from us. We will reveal information on the new edition to you as soon as it becomes available to us!

We hope more players will present more ideas or suggestions, including whether support data can be transfered or not. Thank you!
