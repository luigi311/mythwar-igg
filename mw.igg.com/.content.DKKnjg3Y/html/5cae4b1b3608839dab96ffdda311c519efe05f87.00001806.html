Centaur Race Issues

I think the Centaur graphics do not reflect the race itself. Both male and female characters have Elf attributes, not Centaur's.

I can understand the issue people had with the female being barebreasted in MW I but, to be honest, maybe it contributed to make it a bit more exciting and after a while, stupid comments disappeared.

In MW II, I think the female Centaur looks stupid, esp when she is running/walking: she is stooped and the flowing skirts around her remind me of the female characters in Dink Swallwood: quite primitive.

All other characters in the game are elegant, proud and stand tall, except for the female Centaur, and those impossibly pointed ears are just ridiculous, they look like rabbit ears.

Maybe we'd need better graphics for the Female Centaur.

Just my 2 cents.
