Hack Prevention

Hi, everyone. Here are some little things you can do to protect your accounts from being hacked and such.

Make sure your password is long and mixed with letters and numbers.
Never give out your password to anyone claiming they are from IGG. IGG will never ask for your password.
Do not download any programs/bots/hacks that supposedly help you play Mythwar better and/or easier.
Keep your account to yourself, share at your own risk.
