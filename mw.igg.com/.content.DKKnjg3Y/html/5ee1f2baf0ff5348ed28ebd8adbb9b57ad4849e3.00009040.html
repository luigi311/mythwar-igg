About Pets' Features

Generally to say, there are 6 species of pets, they’re Demon, Special, Flying, Undead, Human and Dragon. Each is featured by their ability and is relatively stronger or weaker when facing certain other species. So it’s a great advantage to keep multiple pets—when facing certain pets, you can choose their best predator.

Full Predominate: When a Full Predominate is attacked by a Full Predominated, the harm will reduce by 18%.
Half Predominate: When a Half Predominate is attacked by a Half Predominated, the harm will reduce by 18%.

As for individual pets, they’re also different from one another even they belong to a same species, for example:

Strength: determines the physical attack ability of the pet.
Stamina: determines the maximum amount of health the pet could reach.
Intelligence: determines the maximum amount of mana the pet could reach.
Agility: determines the attacking speed rate of the pet.

The stars and figure represents the talent of the pet in this aspect, the more stars and the higher figure they’ve got, means the pet is more worth to be trained in this aspect, and a transparent star equals to a half opaque star. If there is a yellow star, it means the pet is a genius in this aspect.

Growth Rate: It determines the increasing speed of several aspects of the pet, including Health, Mana, Attack and Speed, and this is the range that the normal pet originally can have.

Skill: like characters, pets also have skills and different pets have different skills.

Resistance: There are four kinds of different attributes and eight kinds of different conditional resistances, different pets have different physical resistance and magic arts resistance.
