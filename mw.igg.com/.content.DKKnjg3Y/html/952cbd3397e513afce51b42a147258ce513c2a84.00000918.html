Just copying website info, while it would seem to be going right to the source, has flaws- the web info on some pets is wrong, e.g. Aquarius: 10Sta, 6int, 9str, 9agi, not 13 sta. In resistances it also has 5/5 pierce, as well as 10 dodge, not 5 dodge.

Can't CTU Cyclops,  Earth Wolf can be CTU'd, Lizard Digger can't be CTU'd, Ogrewalker can't be CTU'd.

Bloodpede stats and resist: 1Sta, 3Int, 3Str, 5Agi. 100hit, 5 dodge, 20 poison, 10dark

Taurus has wrong resists: 100 hit, 10 Dodge, 5/5Pierce, 5/10 critical, 10/2 Combo, 30 Chaos, 10 Stun, 10 Anti-drain

Blue Chinchilla has a 0 in dodge (At least as written in stats, I've seen them dodge with that 0 as apparent rate.)

Leopard wolf has 6/10 beserk, not crit.

Teethor resists: 100Hit, 5Dodge,  10Poison, 20Ice

Check with someone who has the other constellation pets to correct your resistances there, as they are wrong. I think the diety/saint info needs fixing too, but I'm just going by the website
