Blessings for your father



DADDY,
If all these Kisses aren't enough,
There's two hands to help with garden stuff.
If all these hugs don't work their charm,
There's two arms to help keep you warm.
If this gift doesn't show how much we care,
We have REAL hugs & kisses to share!



June 15 is Father's Day! Still haven't prepared a gift yet? Well, we might be able to help.Take part in our Blessings for your father Event to express your gratitude

Event Content: Father's Day is just around the corner and it's time to show your gratitude to your dear daddy.Write a statement to express your gratitude to your father by replying to this thead(click here),The selected statement will be posted via system channel in game on June 15th .also we will select some lucky participants and reward Supper Rose to them

Event duration: From June 13 th to June 15th


Event Rules:


      1.The statement must be within 30 words. Participants must give the server and character


      2.Your words should be submitted within the designated submission period.


      3.The words must not contain any content that breaks the rules of the forum, or else the player will be disqualified.


      4.Any posts unrelated to the event will be deleted.


      5.The Event is open to any account holder.




Happy Father's day!
