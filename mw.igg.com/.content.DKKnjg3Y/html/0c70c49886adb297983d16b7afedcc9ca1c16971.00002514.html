Attention! Keep Your Account Safe!

Attention Players!  are two illegal and unsafe websitesthat have been spotted in IGG games. A number of players have had theiraccounts compromised by clicking on links to these websites linked fromwithin the game, so we ask all players to please be aware of how tokeep your account secure.

How can I know if a website is unsafe?
1. Generally speaking, illegal websites will askyou to provide your login details in order to access information ontheir site. Remember, the only website where you should login is www.igg.com.

How can I protect my account?
1. Firstly, do not click on any unfamiliar links inthe game, or provide your account details or password on any unknownwebsites.
2. If you need to log-in to your account, please visit the official IGG website (http://www.igg.com)and then login your account on the upper left corner of the interface.Type the address manually into your browser rather than clicking onwebsite links.
3. Ask IGG official Live Chat to make sure the unfamiliar website is illegal or not.
