Wedding Sign-up

Wedding Sign-up


Event Objects: All players taking part in the Closed Beta
Event Content: Maybe you have met your soul mates in Myth War. Maybe you are still struggling to find them. The marriage system in Myth War offers all of you a chance to have a romantic wedding ceremony. Improved marriage game playing and heartwarming wedding quests give you much fun to end your loneliness. All participants in the Closed Beta can take part in the group wedding ceremony held by our GMs. After the wedding, you can accept some special quests to complete together.


Sign-up Format:
your character name and ID
your mate's character name and ID


Event Time: 8:00 PM 18th June (GMT-5)

Participants must reply to this thread to sign-up Event,Thanks for you continued support and participation.
