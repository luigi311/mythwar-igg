Bonuses for Pay by Cash Users

Event Duration: August 22nd - September 22nd
Eligibility: Purchasing Myth War II Online mall points via Pay by Cash within the event duration.
Details:
The Open Beta of Myth War II Online has been released for more than 2 months. In order to give something back to those warmhearted players who have been playing and testing, we have decided to give bonuses to the players who purchase Myth War II Online mall points via Pay by Cash from August 22nd to September 22nd .

Mall points purchased

Bonuses

200 points

1,000,000 Gold

500 points

3,000,000 Gold

1000 points

7,000,000 Gold

2000 points

15,000,000 Gold
Notes:
1. Only a player who purchases Myth War II Online mall points via Pay by Cash during the event will get the bonuses.
2. A player who is eligible to claim bonuses should contact a GM via Live Support and tell the GM their character ID to receive the bonuses.
3. One of our GMs will mail the bonuses to the players designated character ID.
To purchase mall points, visit http://mw2.igg.com/shop/paybycash.php.
