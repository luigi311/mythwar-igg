31# A A A Post at 2008-6-24 02:22 AM  Show author
Thought long and hard about this and have read all the above posts a few times. Must say, most,not all, on all arguments have valid points.
Anyway, as most of you know, I'm a noob Reborn Hummy, backed up with a fantastic Glitter Tiger. I originally thought it would be good to take my GT to MW2 and after resetting it start from scratch but with the advantage of having a good pet to start out with.
Starting to change my mind now, although not necessarily for all the reasons (some tantrums) stated in previous posts.
I agree with one post that says you gain what you earn. I earnt my GT from mall event. Was fortunate enough to be able to afford a few mall points and was lucky enough to win the GT. But should that allow me to take it to MW2? I'm not so sure. I won it from a MW1 bombing event and not MW2. If they are seperate games then maybe all things should stay seperate.
Ok, so lets say I take my GT. What about another player who has played a lot longer than me, spent heaps more cash on the mall, but not been lucky enough to win top prize. Lets say he has a few AoD's, maybe ruined some along the way to get a decent one. Should it not follow when he sees me take my GT that he might want to take his/her AoD's. I am quite willing to take my GT, although in reality I will probably not. I can take 2 pets as ViP so  have the option of taking 4 altogether. I am even thinking about offering to transfer someone elses for them who might not be as fortunate to be a ViP.
How ever, after weighing up all the pro's and cons I want to say this. I am quite happy to lose the right to take my pet/s. I can understand the arguments from other players about it being a big and unfair advantage to certain players.
I like the idea suggested above about maybe a decent gr pet being given instead.
So why not a compromise. If IGG are going to reward mall spenders (ViP's) and don't forget the promoters as well, then reward with something thats specific to MW2 and not MW1. Maybe let them choose a new pet, lvl 1 from a certain list that excludes Dietys and Saints. Or maybe give them some in game gold or exp to help them get started. Ok, I know it still seems unfair to those that will start without anything. But you must remember that IGG is a money making business and mall spenders and promoters help fund the continued development and support to run both MW1 and MW2.
So maybe a rethink and then a compromise will be best for all.
How about IGG mail all ViP's and promoters and get a consensus from them.
Ok, so no answers here, but just a few more thoughts for the debate on this.

Give me a ball, I'll be sorted dudes.
