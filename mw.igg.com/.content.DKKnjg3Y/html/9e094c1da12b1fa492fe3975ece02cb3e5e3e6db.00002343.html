It costs you 20 points per turn. So if you purchase a 200 point card you get 10 turns at the wheel. Refresh page after each turn to reset it and also make a number or screenshot of any prizes if you wish to.
To redeem the prizes, log in your character that you used for the login page. Go to the mall promoter in Wood and select the option the claim reward. If you stay logged on and win more prizes, all you need to do is change servers and select option again.
Make sure you have enough room in your inventory to receive the prizes.

Icy pack not an item in MW2. There is a mall item for inti but it sucks and you can gain more from in-game events.
Best prizes on wheel upto now are the AoD, Gripesack and Hero Medal. Rest are low level items. The mummy ssp's are quite nice, but lots about now so value has dropped. Exp and gold is low and are best regarded as consolation prizes.
NB: I won the AoD btw, but lost it when bug froze my shop. nvm , s*** happens eh?
Gripesack is very nice if your fortunate enough to be in a guild with a mine.
And Hero medal is good if you have a Guild God, as it donates 8m to contribution which raises the amount of resist you obtain from it.
However, the Hero medal is incorrectly described in the mall and people(myself included) thought you gained resist from the use of the medal alone.
