1) Clear inventory as much as you can.

2) Open a new internet window. Go to this forum and enter the first topic (guide for being super powerful). Click the link there. The replies of the quest are negative but don't beleive them because they don't know our secret. Loot at the places of the npc's of the 20 run quest (don't be scared if you see the words flame ruins and you are a low lvl).

3) If you have items on your inventory put them at the bottom. Left click at the item then right click at the place where you want to move it. Now fill your inventory with teleport scrolls.

4) Record the first scroll at the grocessor. Then go to all the npc's where you can go alone, and record them with the teleports, at the order which they are written (first npc tele near the grocessor tele). The last npc has to be at the end of line 2 of the inventory. If you are too low for flame ask some1 lvl 85+ to take you there and back and promise him a small money prize. After recording all the scrolls you are ready to start.

5) Start the quest: tele to npc no.1. record an empty scroll and move it to the place of the used scroll. Then tele npc 2 and repeat... When you have only 2 lines of scroll beware! You have to tele back to the grocessor and buy all the scrolls. Record obe at the grocessor. Then continue. Keep your bag always open.

6) After many times completed the quest redeem the points, and wow! You are rich! In no time! Dont delete the tele to flame if you can't go there alone.

Compare it with the time time taken to do survival and see that it's really quicker money (not talking about captains or majors doing survival).
