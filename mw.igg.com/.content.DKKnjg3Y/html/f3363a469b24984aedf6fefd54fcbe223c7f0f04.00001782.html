My Impressions on MW2

Here are my impressions on the "new" version; MythWar 2

- I was very disappointed with the horrible resolution (still stuck at 800*600?).

- The same exact graphics, only very minor changes (if any at all).

- The interface and maps are somewhat confusing compared to MythWar.

- I liked the new Borg design, but the Centaur is no longer a "Centaur"? And the Mage/Human has no significant change in design.

- Weird characters apear everywhere, mostly in NPC names, pet names...etc.

Conclusion:

I quit MythWar like a year ago or so but I've always loved it, but MythWar2 is a dispointment, at least to me. I might go back to MythWar but it's highly unlikely that I'll continue playing/testing MythWar2.
