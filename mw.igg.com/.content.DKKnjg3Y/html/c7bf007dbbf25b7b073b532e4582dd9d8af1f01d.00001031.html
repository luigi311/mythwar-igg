How to Buy and Sell Gold Ingots

I'll try to explain.
Go to the trading zone in Bly' and click the npc. Silver ingots can't be bought or sold. This is just for Gold ingots
Sell:
If you want to sell, you choose the "sell" option. Lets say you have 200 ingots for sale, and you want to sell at 7m per 100.
Choose the amount as 2 (2 x 100) and the price as 700 (700 x 10k). They will then be put on sale for a purchaser to see and will be shown in the system as player ****? has offered etc etc.
This then has put them on sale for a period of time for a buyer to buy them. If after the time runs out, no one buys, they are returned to you.

Buy:
Select the buy option and choose how many you want to buy and at what price you are prepared to pay. Again, the amount is deducted from your account and if you are successful and someone who is selling selects your offer you will receive thier ingots and they will get your gold. Period of time still in force. No purchase and your gold is returned.

Buy/Sell:
This is the easiest way to buy and sell as its an instant purchase rather than an offer. You scroll down the ingots for sale and select the one you want and can purchase immediately if the gold is available in your account. Like wise for the opposite if you are selling and there is an offer to buy.

I'll make a graphic guide if peeps are still confused.
