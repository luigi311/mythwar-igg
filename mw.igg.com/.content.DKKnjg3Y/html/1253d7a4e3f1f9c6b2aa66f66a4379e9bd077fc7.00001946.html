Summer Sale Week

Summer Sale Week

Event duration:
From 9:30pm until 11:30pm June 23rd - June 29th

Event description:
Because we appreciate your constant support of our game so much, we will offer you a big discount on different items sold in the mall everyday from June 23rd to June29th. So open your wallet and join in the event.  You would regret not snatching that bargain when you had the chance!
Some of you may be concerned about whether or not Mythwar I will be closed down after the official launch of Mythwar II. But worry not dear players, we promise you that Mythwar I will not be closed but will run alongside Mythwar II. So that the players of Mythwar I can now enjoy the many privileges of playing Mythwar II.

If players of Mythwar I start to play Mythwar II between June 27th - July 6th and their characters progress to level 50 or above within the 10 days, we will credit each of their accounts with 100 silver ingots. (Every account can receive the reward once only.)

1. Players of Mythwar I can pick one of their pets (two pets for VIP players) from Mythwar I to play with them in Mythwar II. We will keep the level of the pets that the players have chosen unchanged but will not save the pets' resistance(Some Pets' growth in MW II are different from MW I). Once a player has reached a high enough level to own a pet, the GM will give the pet back to the player. If players wish to save their pets then they should visit our forum to register.

Note: One account just can transfer one pet and the account should have a character above level 80.

2.We will award 20% of all of the points that players have consumed within in Mythwar I (before June 30th,2008)into their new accounts in Mythwar II as compensation .
