IGG Adds Big Bling [02-16-2009]

Withthe increasing number of IGG players, we have offered more servers tomake sure our games run smoothly. However, our current serverfacilities are at their capacity limits, so we have decided to move allof the servers to a better IDC (Internet Data Center). This move willtake place from approximately 2:00pm February 17th to 6:00am, February 18th EST (GMT-5)during which all IGG games and official websites and forums will beunavailable. With this move we are not only just increasing the serverfacilities, but also the number of and quality of the servers. With allthis new hardware, players should soon be enjoying an even betterenvironment for gaming than before.
Stay tuned for updates.
Sorry for any inconvenience!
Meanwhile, the new avatar will finally be available on February 19th and we will be holding 2X Exp event from February 20th to February 22nd at the designated times.
Event Times
2:00am to 5:00am EST (GMT-5)
10:00am to 13:00noon EST (GMT-5)
3:00 pm to 6:00 pm EST (GMT-5)
8:00 pm to 11:00 midnight EST (GMT-5)
