Candy Hunting

With Halloween quickly approaching,you probably can't wait to say "Trick or treat?" ^.^  Well don't worry, we've got plenty of candy to offer, there's a catch however, you need to find them on your own. All the candy is hidden in some posts in our forums. You will definitely find the candy if you are careful enough. The candy you find will be your reward!  

Event Duration: October 20th to October 30th EDT (GMT-4)

Event Rules:
1. Visit all the game forums on IGG and find the pictures of candy.  
2. Post the links of the pictures to the IGG forum.
3. Your post should be hided otherwise it will be deleted.
4. Each player can take part in the event only once.  
5. Only Posts sent during the event count.

PS: Candy's general location

TOP Forum:  TOP Media Overview
AO Forum:  AO Media Overview
WL Forum: Announcement
VC Forum:  Tutorials & Guides
ZU Forum:  Event Zone
MW Forum:  Announcements
GW Forum:  Announcement
AB forum:  Announcement


Rewards:
After the event ends, our admins and mods will randomly pick serveral participants as winners. The number of the available links they send determines how many credits they will obtain.1 available link for 300 buzz credits, if you can find all the links, you will obtain 2400 buzz credits,
Note: buzz credits can be exchanged for Mall points.
