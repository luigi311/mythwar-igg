Borg Guide

At last! At my third guide....Please read my other guides named Human Guide and Mage Guide made by...Of course me....
NOTE:I made 6 Borgs and leveled them up to level 50 while using different builds to each Borg I used....I spent 2 Weeks on them.....With level III and IV skills....After that,Iv'e deleted them all.... ...I have tried all the borgs at duel with my agi-human named Antoinette.


Borgs-This time I made a borg out of my own self and account.....I experimented the race and studied every single detail I could get...And here are the informations Iv'e got:

Borgs:The male one has an ugly face.......They belong to a race with supernatural strength...Not much to tell about them

--------------------------Builds--------------------------
Ok....I experimented 6 builds......

1.2 sta 2 str-The first borg I made was with this build.Moderate HP,Nice melee attack,moderate Drain Damage....Not quite useful when level 50+ coz the leveling quests and maps when level 50 doesn't require melee attacks...A borg with this build could AFK for a long time without being noticed by the leader....

2.1 sta 2 str 1 int-I used this build on my 2nd borg.Uhhmmm...A brog with this build has a good Drain Damage....It provides a moderate mana pool for the MP-expensive Drain IV when level 7+.....I compared the 1st and 2nd borgs when they were at level 50....And this was what I have observed:

1st borg(2 sta 2 str)-With 9.3k HP at level 50...
2nd borg(1 sta 2 str 1 int)With 8.7k HP at level 50...

The 1 sta used just added 600 HP....

3.2 sta 2 agi-Everytime somebody asks in the Worl,"What's the best build for borg?" I always say 2 sta 2 agi....A borg with this build will have 300+ Speed at level 50 and can outrun a cent.This build is useful when duelling.I duelled a party consisting of 3 members level 50+ when this borg was still level 49.  I defeated them...The key element was cause I pawned there centaur first which was the healer....And next are their pets which could have healed the cent....This build would be so much useful during Guild Wars and Pking+Dueling...

4.An All over borg(1 sta 1 int 1 str 1 agi)-I have noticed that the Borg needs all the 4 stats...Int increases Drain damage,Sta for The HP,agi for the speed and 1 strength when at AFK    .Quite good build coz it was an all rounder....Good when PKing and leveling....But  will be pawned by an agi-human.

5.2 int 1 sta 1 agi-My hybrid build....I made in 2 int coz if you have noticed,Int increases Drain damage at a small percentage....The sta for the HP....and the agi for the speed when doing Skill training and Duelling.Not a guild build in my opinion.But provides a good mana pool when using Drain IV at quests with monsters that have long HP.(Drowcrusher,Othyugh[Wood Def])

6.1 sta 3 str-This build gives importance when used with Enhance.I trained this last borg with a ghost warrior as a pet.And with Enhance III used by the GW to the borg,The borg damaged 20k+ using melee.This borg nearly pawned my agi-human but thanks to chaos and hypno,My human was saved.


-------------------------Just suggesting----------------------

Centaur guide coming soon....(Give me 5 days or 1 week)
