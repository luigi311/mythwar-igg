FAQ

1. Q:  What is PayPal?
    A: With PayPal, means you have an online account that makes it easy to send money from a variety of sources (like your credit card or bank account) to a variety of recipients (such as an online store, like IGG item mall) – without sharing your financial information. So IGG will never view your credit card number or your banking information. See how PayPal helps you.

2. Q: Does PayPal Support Credit Card and bank account？
    A: Yes. Once you sign up for PayPal, you can send money to anyone with an email address using money from your PayPal balance or another funding option, there are several choices, such as a credit card, debit card, or your bank account. Recipients are then notified via email that they have received a payment from you.

3.  Q: Is PayPal secure?
     A: Yes. PayPal uses SSL technology to keep your information safe. In addition, when you send a payment using PayPal, the recipient won't receive sensitive financial information like your credit card or bank account number. This way, you don't have to worry about paying people you don't know.

4. Q:What is echeck?Why don't i get realtime buying capability by echeck?
     A: Echeck is a form of paypal payment. It occurs when the paypal owner only links a bank account to the paypal account. When a payment is made, the fund needs to be transferred from the bank account to the paypal account, and the payment is usually not cleared immediately. In order to avoid the delay of getting your points, you can either link a credit card account to the paypal account, or transfer some cash from your bank account into the paypal account, before you make a paypal payment.

If you use echeck in paypal, you will never get real-time buying capability, since your echeck is uncleared and will take a few days to clear. In this case, you will always need to file your purchase at the Paypal/Credit Card Purchase Page.
