Attaching Image to a Post/Reply




There are two ways to attach an image to a post.

1. One is using the attach image button on the message formatting options.



Using this method,you will be prompted to enter the URL of your image, thus, you need to upload your image to http://www.photobucket.com/ or http://imageshack.us/. When you have entered your image URL, you will see the BBCode for your URL in the message box(if you’re on the Discuz!Code tab), if you’re on the WYSIWYG tab, you will immediately see the image you have attached.




2. Another way of attaching an image is using the “Uploading New Attachments” option just right below the posting form. See attachment.



Using this method, you can attach images directly from your PC. You can upload multiple files by pressing the [+] link. Note that the image you’re going to attach will be placed at the bottom of your post. You need to submit your post first before you can place the image in its proper place. Meaning, after you submitted your post, you can "edit" your post and place your uploaded files on the proper place.
