I Introduction

So, you are 91 and want continue leveling. What are you going to do? Options are: Guildquest, Worn Diety, Survival or Drow Lord aka "Papa Drow".
The advantages of Papa are:
- not as boring as Guildquest
- not as expensive as Survival (considering the costs for experience scrolls and their availability)
- less need for various resistances / armors compared to Worn Deity and also better chances to find a party

The rewards are:
- for players lvl 90-109 400k++ exp and a chance to get G9 random gem. Pets gets 400k exp if its lvl is from 90 - 110.
- for players lvl 110+ 30 skill point to lowest skill and around 50k exp. 400k pet exp if leader lvl 90-109, else 300k.

IV Tactics

Party

=> any party-composition can do Papa, but the composition will vary the parameters of succeeding as well as the speed. Generally you can say that if you need more than 8-9 minutes / Papa, guildquest is more effective.

Normally each party has at least 1 member of each race, while taking more Mages / Borgs in increases speed, while taking more Humans / Cents in increases safety (recommended for "lower levels" with normal equipment and pets).
You can even do Papa in turbo speed with 4 Borgs and 1 Mage (all non reborn and no x-mas pets) if you know what you're doing.


Pets

When chosing pets you should consider that they will take aprox 30k melee damage from Swamps before you can get an Protect on them, if they are fast they will also hit by Drain before the melee. So, the poor man's choice are definetly big, slow melee-tanks.
Poison can deal a considerable amount of damage to almost all mobs in this quests while being much more reliable as crit-pets.
Importance of pet attributes (imo) :
Melee res / high sta >> Poison ability >> Chaos res >> Speed >> Crit / Melee attackers

Equipment

Things you want to achive:
1. stay alive
2. stay out of chaos
3. don't lose turns

There are many ways to achieve this, depending on what your party is and what your role in the party is.
If you're slower than the cent -> focus on your chaos res
If you're the the human that has to get Black preferably before anything else "happens" you probably want to survive at least a Drain and 1 melee from the Swamps.
The right equipment you have to figure out yourself, but a good general advice would be: 30 melee, 40-50 chaos and a Protect IV 10-11 should do the trick 90% of the time.

Tactics (itself)

This point will heavily rely on you guys and what you will deliver me as input.
I'll present 2 tactics I know that they are working from my point as fHuman / Borg / Mage

Tactic 1 - "Hyp"

Required / needed:
- Human with Hyp IV 9-11
- Mage with Flash III 10-11, Ice/Fire II/IV as high as possible
- Cent (I think even Heal IV 7 will do), doesn't even matter if male or female as long as he gets hit by Jungles Poison *hint*
- fBorg with Protect IV 10-11
- Borg with Drain IV as high as possible and has to be faster than the Mage
- 1 Poison pet, more = better

Procedure:
Human spams Hyp IV on Black (if second Human in party he can backup with spamming Hyp III on Black). Cent heals / purges if need. Borg Drain III on Winter, Mage Flash III on Winter, pet Poison III on Winter (that should do the trick), fBorg casts Protect IV.
In second round (even if Winter alive but poisoned) Borgs Drain III on Papa, Mage Flash III on Papa, pet poisons. When Papa dead: same game with Wild then Swamps (Mage uses Ice/Fire then). Try to keep Jungle alive while killing Black because his Poison IV can get you out of chaos.
Humans can try Chaos III Black optionally before start targetting him.
Important: If anything goes wrong and your Hyp fails: Wake Jungle, so that he can get you out of possible chaos!

Tactic 2 "Chaos"

- All party members immune to chaos (~40 + Protect IV 10-11)
- Human with Chaos III 11
- Mage with Flash III 10-11, Ice/Fire II/IV as high as possible
- Cent (male preferable)
- fBorg with Protect IV 10-11
- Borg with Drain IV as high as possible and has to be faster than the Mage

1st round Human Chaos III on Wild, Cent heals / purges if need, Borg Drain VI on Winter, Mage Flash III on Winter, pet Poison III on Winter (that should do the trick), fBorg casts Protect IV.
2nd round Human Chaos III on Black and alternates the following rounds between Wild and Black (if both dead Chaos IV or Posin IV "the rest"), Borgs Drain IV on Black, Mage Ice/Fire IV on Black, pet poisons if possible.
Until the end: kill, kill, kill and maybe tag Critters in.

Tactic 3 - by meaw

There is another possible strategy. The advantage of this is that, like hypno tactics, it doesn't require high level skills. But it's much faster than hypno tactics. Though it requires chaos gear, which you can borrow from friends (can't borrow skill points!). Wear chaos resist. having a male cent or snow bear come handy in case you got unlucky. The idea is to chaos IV on 3 melee attackers, drainer, and poisoner (which are conveniently the fastest five). The other 3 -- papa, winter, and chaos -- post no threat with their weak attacks and you being chaos resist. Then just nuke them out all at once with all you have while human keep using chaos IV (lvl 9+ recommended).

- Poisoning the ones that are not chaosed speeds things up a bit.
- Try to kill Black first by targeting the attacks on him.
- If your Chaos IV is 10-11, then on second round, you can chaos/hypno iii Black.
- If most party members wear good chaos resist, you don't even need to use protect. Can start off with drain IV.
- Dodge shoes are good


Thanks for reading, feel free to your Questions / correct my mistakes (I think I did my last papa 9 months ago...) and if you kindly provide a "new tactic", please do it in a format so I can just copy+paste it in - ty.
