Christmas Screenshot PK Contest

Christmas will be here before you know it and almost every IGG game has been wrapped up by the holiday atmosphere. However, which game has the most splendid Christmas scenery?!  Show us the most splendid Christmas screenshots from any of IGGs games!

Event Duration:  December 17th - December 31th EST (GMT-5)
Selection Duration:  Jan 1st to Jan  4th（GMT-5）
The winners will be announced and rewarded on Jan  5th


Rules
1.        All players are qualified to join this event without registration. The forum address is IGG Forum's Event Zone
2.        To participate in this event, players should submit screenshots that are related to Christmas and contain their in-game image.
3.        Each account is allowed to submit screenshots of 3 IGG games at most and the screenshots must be submitted in the Event Zone.
4.        Please fill out the "X-Mas + Game" in the post when uploading the screenshot.
5.        To avoid accounts being stolen, players are allowed to hide their character name in the screenshot and even decorate the screenshot
         a bit.
6.        All screenshots must be original.
7.        IGG reserves the right to use the player submitted screenshots as they like.


Rewards:
Extraordinary Xmas Screenshot (2)    100  IGG  forum Money
Cool Xmas Screenshot  (3)                  80   IGG  forum Money
Warm Xmas Screenshot  (3)                80   IGG  forum Money

Other participants can gain 20 IGG forum Money as the reward!

PS.
IGG forum Money can be exchanged to IGG credits in future!  
If you have any suggestions about the Name of IGG credits, please feel free to tell us. Please post your suggestions here
