Christmas Photo Contest (MWI and MWII)


As you walk along the tree-lined streets, the happy faces of excited children everywhere, it's impossible not to feel the spirit of Christmas in your heart. Why not try to capture that beautiful moment in a photograph? Take out your camera and send us a picture that best expresses the magic of Christmas!

Event Content:
Take a photo with a Christmas theme and add some elements of the MWI(or MWII) game ,then
show it in this thread. the post must include your  host server, character name and ID
Event duration: From December 17th to December 26 th EST(GMT-5)

Event Rules:
1. Post your photo (JPEG (.jpg) format, maximum file size 800*800) in the appointed section of our forum. It must be related to Christmas.
2.Photos containing offensive or any inappropriate content will be rejected.
3.Your photo should be original. If you upload a duplicate photo, only the first image sent will be accepted.
4.If your photo is used by IGG, you will win a reward.
5.IGG reserves the right of final interpretation and the ownership of winners' works.


Event Rewards:
Excellent Award（5）: 250 silver ingots+ 1*Saint ShapeShifting potion OR 200 integrals+ 1*Saint ShapeShifting potion
 Encouragement Award（15）: 200 silver ingots OR150 integrals
