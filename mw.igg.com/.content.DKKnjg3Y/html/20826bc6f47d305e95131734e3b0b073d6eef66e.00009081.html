All Time Fun

Starts from Dec 10th until 30th

Santa Claus will appear in certain places every 3 minutes. You could accept tasks by talking to the Santa Claus, and you will get the rewards by finishing them.

Rewards:
During the Xmas activities, many amazing gifts will be given out, and among them there are Enjoyment Card, Blessing Card, Santa Hat, Santa Stocking, Santa Bell and Santa Candy.
