Bugs Hunting

Are you anxious because you don't know how to spend your summer? Why not join in the Myth War 2.0 Closed Beta? Much fun awaits you in the mystical land of Myth War!

Bugs Hunting

At the period of the Closed Beta , all players can take part in the Bug Hunting event and have a chance to win Point Cards. Make sure to hand in all the bugs you can find during the event period.

Participation Ways:
Players can post replies with bugs they've found within the event duration

Event Rules:
1. All participants must post replies including a clear indication of the BUG. Otherwise, they will be disqualified.
2. It is much better for participants to give details about bugs, such as the map where bugs are found, a bug's coordinates, and a bug's type. If the bug is related to a computer system, offer us your computer configuration. Bugs found will be invalid if we fail to verify them because of insufficient information.
3. We will record every valid bug. If bugs of same type are found, the first post to submit them will be recorded. Don't repeat posting replies of the same bugs.
4. Participants can submit as many different unique bugs as possible in each of their posts to reply.
5. Bugs submitted are invalid after the event period expired. The event duration will be announced on our official website.
6. We will select 20 players according to the number and the quality of bugs they hand in and then present them with rewards.
7. We will release winners' forum usernames after the end of event. Winners must send private message to the appointed board manager to leave your account and contact information. We are not responsible for any losses resulting from incorrect contact information.
8. IGG reserves the right to final interpretation.
Event Rewards:

A ticket to the Olympic Football Tournament

The top 10 players can obtain 500 Silver-Ingots for each.
Players ranked from No.11 to No.20 can obtain 300 Silver-Ingots for each
