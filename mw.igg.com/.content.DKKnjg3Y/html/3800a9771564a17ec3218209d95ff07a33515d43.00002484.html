Christmas Activities Quick Glance

Everyday Event
Event Duration:0:00(server time) on Dec 20th to 11:59 pm (server time) on Dec 30th
1.Santa will appear at a random spot every 3 minutes during the event duration, and  players can accept the quest by talking to Santa.(the quest is repeatable)
2.Players need to pass the quest among certain NPCs, and no battle will happen.
3.The last NPC you meet will offer you the rewards when you complete the mission.
4.All players can participate in the event.
Everyday Rewards
Event Duration:0:00 (server time) on Dec 20th to 11:59 pm (server time) on Dec 30th
1.Players can find the appointed NPCs to redeem rewards every 24 hours in each server. Each ID is only allowed to redeem rewards once every day.
2.There are some ordinary rewards and special rewards. The amount of special rewards is limited.
3.Participation: Level 30+ players  
Christmas Celebration
Event Duration: 0:00(server time) on Dec 23th to 11:59 pm (server time) on Dec 25th

Players can participate in the events above according to their own schedules.
Players need to make a team including at least 3 players. They need to find the appointed NPCs and complete all the quests. You are supposed to kill many monsters, and sometimes NPCs will give you a quiz or some entertaining game to play.  
After completing the quest, you will be offered rewards
Participation: Level 30+ players  
Event Rewards:Enjoyment Card, Blessing Card, Santa Hat, Santa Stocking, Santa Bell and Santa Candy. The Best rewards are Deity Pets and Christmas Pets.
Puzzle Treasure
Players should collect all letters including "M E R R Y C H R I S T M A S"

Event Duration: 0:00(server time) Dec 5th to 11:59pm (server time) Dec 31st
Event Contents
1.Players can find the map trader to buy an ordinary treasure map in Woodlingor (407，302). The map will take you to the place where the treasure is hidden. When you find the place, you should use the map again, and then you will obtain the treasure.
2.Advanced Treasure Map: The map fragments are composed of the 10 letters, M, E, R, Y, C, H, I, S, T, and A. M E R R Y will make up of one advanced treasure map, and C H R I S T M A S will make up of the other treasure map. The way to find advanced treasure is the same with the way to find ordinary treasure. But the rewards are more attractive, you will have chances to obtain Dream Equipment and Christmas Pets.

Rewards
1.Ordinary Treasure:Copper Box, Iron Box, Silver Box, Fragments of Treasure Map (Collect all fragments to obtain advanced treasure map), Gold, Experience and so on..
2.Advanced Treasure：Santa Hat, Santa Stocking, Santa Bell, Santa Candy, Advanced necklace or Dream Equipment, Christmas Pets and so on .
