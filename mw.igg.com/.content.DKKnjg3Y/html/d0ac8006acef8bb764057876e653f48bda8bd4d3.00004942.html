Alps Sent Candidates.

Well what can you say about those that have been selected? I think those who have been around should sit back and remember how our server was a year ago. Think about what AA and Serenity had to deal with when it was just them against the world. They were always fair even if they had to take action against someone. That can't be said for the majority of the candidates that we are faced with right now. Mad and Serenity are doing a great job of handling everything but they do need some help. We have slid back to a year ago and only have two against the world again but this time we have 5 times the world population. So when you pick who are going to be the ones that we have to go to with a problem, think about who is fair and honest. Don't pick your friend just because he/she is your friend or because they are reborn and can kick your bum if you don't. Think about if you were a new person on the server and needed someone, would this person be who you went to with confidence. Havok is one that I would trust. Wuschi is right up there in the top as well. Most of the other's I don't know personally. Akville is a really nice person and I see her helping when she is online, different time zone from me. Just stop and think before you put someone in that we all have to try to trust. Some are very one sided in their way. Their friends get to do and say what they wish and everyone else has to follow the rules. Is that really who you want to run roughshod over us? You may not be one of the friends once the power trip hits. Peace and love to all of our family in Alps.
