Dragon's Teeth - a reference to an episode in Star Trek where the name of the race Borg comes from. We also have Dragon Tooth Dagger in game.

A'loa Glen - it is an island full of ancient magic in the fictional series The Wit'ch by James Clemen, which is one of the early series about magic and it features the main character as a female mage (witch) along with other dark mage enemies.

Los Angeles - a human land. I just like the sound of the name and its meaning "the city of angels"

Ziz's Realm - Ziz is a mythical creature who protects the sky. He is one of the three great creatures along with Leviathan and Behemoth. Ziz's realm is literally the vast sky. He presents all other creatures in Mythwar besides the four major races.
