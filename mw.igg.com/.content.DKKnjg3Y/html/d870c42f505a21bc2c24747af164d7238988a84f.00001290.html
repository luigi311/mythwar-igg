Mods on Probation Recruited

Dear players,
In order to maintain a more harmonious forum for our players, we are currently recruiting some probationary  Mods. If anyone is interested in becoming one of these probationary
Mods, then they should read the following requirements and rights to gain a clearer understanding of the overall scope of the position.

Application Requirements:
1.  Already a forum member.
2.  Have been online for more than 1 week.
3.  Have posted a lot of posts (no spams)
4.  Usually helping other players to solve problems.
5.  Familiar with the game, and enjoy Myth War Online.

Probation Period:
After one month of work on the forum, you will be qualified to apply to become a formal mod.

Probation Obligations
1.  Reply to players' posts
2.  Maintain a harmonious forum
3.  Encourage more players to join our events and promote the games popularity.
4.  Communicate with forum members frequently and maintain a good relationship between you and excellent members.

Probation Rights:
1.   When players make a mistake in your section,they should be given a warning
2.   Advertisements, promotions,spamming should be deleted immediately.
3.   Your section event should be verified by the administrator.

Notes:
If you break the rules as below, your qualifications will be cancelled.
1.   Continue to abuse other players after being given a warning
2.   Haven't logged into the forum or posted for more than 1 week.
3.   After being given a warning, you continue to take revenge on other players when they complain or otherwise abuse your position and powers.
4.   Please remember the Probation obligations and rights are not limited to the ones above as there are more obligations probation mod will be introduced to once selected.
