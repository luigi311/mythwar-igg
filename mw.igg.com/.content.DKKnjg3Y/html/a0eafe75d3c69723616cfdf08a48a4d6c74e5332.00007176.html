Beware - Counter Attacks
*******************************************************

Throughout the game, I saw many times in quests where pets, newbies and sometimes even experience players (mostly STR built) get killed and free tele in quest due to monsters’ counter attacks.

Note that counter attacks are dangerous especially from Killer Bees in Existent quest and Cleaver Killer in Drowcrusher quest. If your characters or pets has combo upon counter attacks, the outcome of counter attack would be worse.

Example :
Character attack : 20k Monster counter : 20k
Combo : 3x Counter : 3x
Maximum damage : 35k Maximum damage : 60k

It is advisable to avoid using physical attack at monsters with high rate and damage in counter attacks.

Reversing the role, I have not find any good reason to put counter attack diamond into weapon. I may get myself killed as I counter the monster attack. I will likely consider one day may be just to look nice doing those counter attack moves.
