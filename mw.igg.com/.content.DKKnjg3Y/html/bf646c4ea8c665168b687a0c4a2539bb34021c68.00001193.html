For those who keep saying 3/1 sta or full sta built best for cents, I ask u the following questions.

If it is for extra mp, then i tell u 100 int at low int level = peanuts MP, not even enough to cast a heal II 11. So why did u waste 100 points in INT rather than investing in full sta. COS HP per sta and MP per INT increase as STA/INT level increase. IE 100 additional sta at 500 sta will give more HP than 100 additional sta at 200 sta

For full sta cent, yes, u are fat. U have like 120k hp or even more. Big Deal? Mp less than 35k, cant even cast 2 times heal IV 11. On the other hand, a 2/2 or 1int/3sta cent can have 50k/50k or 30k/60k hp/mp. On the other hand, with my high INT, I can cast 3 or even 4 times heal IV 11 which obviously u cant. I can heal in hurricane even under 3 drain IV, which obviously u cant again. I can kill seal mobs with my high blizzard damage while u are just an extra carefare in charge of healing, which a good party doesnt even need to heal.
