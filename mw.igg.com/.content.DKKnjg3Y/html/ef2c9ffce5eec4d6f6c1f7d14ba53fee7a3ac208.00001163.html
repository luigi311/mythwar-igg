Dear Players:
IGG is pleased to announce that we will be adding the Taurus Pack to the Item Mall this week. The new Taurus pet featured in the Taurus pack is also totally different from the previous lovely Aries. Which one do you like?
Taurus Pack:It contains a Taurus egg and 6 other articles. When you redeem it from the NPC, there should be at least 7 blanks in your inventory.   
     Taurus
  GrowthRate:1.250,Skill:Fire,Resistance:Drain/Chaos/Stun

Icy Crystal Pack:It contains 45 Icy Crystals and you can take them out to use them. It will increase your participating pet's intimacy by 200 points.

Dragon Bone:Can be used directly to increase an in-battle pet's attributes by 6 at random and the growth rate by 0.01. Limit 4 usages per pet.

Note: Stone of Moon will reset it.

Dragon Eye:Can be used directly on a pet to increase it's growth rate by 0.1. Limit 1 usage per pet. Note: Stone of Moon will reset it.

Gold Card:Contains 10,000,000 gold, which can be withdrawn once or several times.

Stone of Dreams:After giving it to the Pet researcher (338,390) outside City Hall at Blython, the pet's level will be reset to level 1. The growth rate will be changed (up or down at random).

Stone of Curses:Can be used directly on a pet. After using, it will delete the resistance effect of the in battle pet and renew its natural resistance.

Total items: 7
And also, the price of this pack is 1997 points. Who will be the first and lucky player owns the fantastic Taurus? Let's see!
