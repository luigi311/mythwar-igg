  News
Anti-illegal-trading & Anti-cheating

 

Dear players,

It has been reported that some players have spread some sites for the illegal trading in our games and forums. If you are caught doing this then the official will automatically freeze your account until you can justify your actions. In addition, links to such sites often have some virus lurking behind them, so you'd better avoid them, to protect your accounts from being stolen. A small number of players have reported that their accounts had been stolen recently. And after deep investigation, we find that most of those players who claimed this had dishonest intentions. For example some of them were found to be using cheat programs so that "Trojan" applications for stealing accounts were installed onto other people's computers. Other ways to be a victim of fraud are by sharing your account information, neglecting to protect your account information and using a password that is too easy to guess.  To help combat this; the Official has put together the following tips to help you ensure the security of your account:

1. It is recommended that you don’t use assistant software so-called with powerful functions whilst playing Mythwar. Once you use assistant software to play or so-called botter, the account name and password you enter will be very likely to be sent to a remote server（Not the IGG server）. Then your account may be stolen at anytime.

2. It is recommended that you do not to log into any unofficial website or link that is not authorized by us. All official sites are under the domain name: http://mw.igg.com or www.igg.com, so be cautious when you login to non-official websites.

3. The official will never ask players for their account password or any other account information. Some players may try to use a username with “IGG or GM” in front to cheat in game. And once you reveal the account name and password to him/her, then your account may be stolen within a short time. So please don’t reveal any account information to others.

4. Players need to enter their account name and password to log into the official forum. However, this is not the main reason that accounts are stolen. But don't fill in the mailing address used for registering game accounts on the forum, to avoid being attacked by a hacker.

5. Don't reveal account information to others or share your account with others because it may lead to unnecessary trouble or could even make you lose your entire account.

6. Don't download any software or so-called update game clients that they are not on our official website.

7. Don't believe any mails that they are not from igg.com. Some cheaters used so-called vip.com to trick players into giving out their account and password information.

8. We strongly recommend that you only purchase items from the official Item Mall. Real money trades are expressly forbidden and you may get easily duped doing this.

 9. For security reasons, the answers to password questions should be complex. Simple answers are easily guessed and may result in the theft of your account. If your account has been stolen, please link to http://www.igg.com/getpass.php

The official wishes to remind players once more not to reveal account information to others or to become lured by assistant programs with allegedly with "powerful functions" to protect your own computer. Any assistant program used whilst playing Mythwar may bring unnecessary negative effects and may also bring you trouble with your account security!

Please look after for your account and resist cheat program in order to enjoy Mythwar for longer!

There is more satisfaction in knowing that you have earned your rewards than cheated someone out of them!
                                                

 

Mythwar Online Team, IGG
2007-12-21
