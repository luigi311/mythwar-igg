Q) How to redeem the first pet?
A: In sky passage find the pet redemption NPC. Talk to the NPC and he will randomly give you a pet. If you are not satisfied with the given pet you are able to release the pet and re-pick until you are satisfied with the given pet.
2007-02-28
Q) Is it normal for a pet to level up faster than the main character?
A: Yes, this is very normal, as the main character is allowed to keep a few pets. Pets will always be able to level up faster than the main character.
2007-02-28
Q) How many pets can a character keep?
A: Every main character can keep up to 8 pets and there will be an increment of 3 pets after every reincarnation. The advantages of keeping multiple pets are: a) being able to use different pets for different battles; b) being able to trade your pets with other players.
2007-02-28
Q) How many races/classes are there?
A: There are 4 main classes.
Human: Humans possess the deadliest physical attack skills.
Male Human: Wanderer.
Female: Thief.
Centaur: Centaurs possess superb ability and arrow attack skills.
Male: Archery.
Female: Healer.
Mage: Mages mainly use magical attacks as their form of offense.
Male: Wizard.
Female: Witch.
Cyborg: Their vast knowledge of technology is their main form of power.
Male: Destroyer.
Female: Destroyer.

Q) Where are the places you can buy heal or mana pots and armor?
