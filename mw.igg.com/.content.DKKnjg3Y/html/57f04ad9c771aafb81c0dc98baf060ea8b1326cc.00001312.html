2# A A A Post at 2008-4-18 05:57  Show author
If you mean being reborn as a Mage if you were a Cent as a non-reborn, then it would probably be a bad idea. Firstly Cent's have the highest speed/AGI growth rate. A Cent rb to a Mage would mean a fast Mage.... which would mean a hit-prone Mage, which is not good. Mages are generally not supposed to be fast(for most non PvP quests). Other bonuses as a Cent rb to Mage would include skill bonuses, like Chaos res from Purge Chaos, which is about the only useful bonus. Other bonuses would be a str bonus from Multi Shot(Mages are not supposed to be physical attackers generally, so str would be absolutely useless), HP bonus from Heal(this is alos useful, but won't make much of a difference) and, even more Speed from Speed. Which means Cent to Mage would be a bad idea(normally). If you want the useful bonuses from Cent, then make a Human and rb him to Mage, that would mean res to all Human skills, as well as extra HP without the unnecessary str bonus, or the suicidal speed bonus. Although these are just my thoughts...
