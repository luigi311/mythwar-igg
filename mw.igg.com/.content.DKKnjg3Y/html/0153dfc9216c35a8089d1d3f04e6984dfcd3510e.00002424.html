Pet lairs are in the same locations as they were in the first Myth War game.

Demon Lair - Desert City (Can catch various monsters there).
Evil Lair - Demon Square (All random encounter monsters cannot be caught).

You can still kill spirit beasts in both areas though, if you're only after an eye kicker\bubble crab Demon Lair is the place for you.
