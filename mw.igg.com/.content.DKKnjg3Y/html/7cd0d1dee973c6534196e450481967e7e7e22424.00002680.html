During the Chinese New Year, the mysterious Thearchy World will open, and if you're brave enough and would like
to give it a shot, you can find the wonderful treasures left by the Gods there by then!
From Feb 6th to Feb 10th
Everyday between 09：00-12：00 ; 14：00-17：00 ; 19：00-22：00
Players above level 20 are qualified to participate in it.

1. Enter the mysterious world:
One hour before the opening of the Thearchy World, system will broadcast the notices to remind you. After the
opening of the Thearchy World, players can go and find Fortune Angel in front of the Church of Blython at
(160,544) to enter the mysterious world.

2. Hunt for treasure:
The Thearchy World consists of two parts—SierraMounta and Paradise, players can enter the Paradise only after
defeating all the enemies in the SierraMounta, strong team spirit and great team work is the most important
necessities!
In this World of Gods, you'll confront with interesting things one after another and there will be lots of challenges
as well as countless treasure!

Plenty of rewards (perhaps the most ever) will be given out in various forms!? Including lucky points, Guild
accumulations, treasure box, lottery, Hong Bao, Blessing Card…

You can have the rewards directly or exchange the points for the rewards, and among them, there are Dream
Equipment, Deity Pets, precious stones, hundreds and thousands of Exp, intimacy points…
You can redeem your rewards from the 6 NPCs in the Church of Blython.
