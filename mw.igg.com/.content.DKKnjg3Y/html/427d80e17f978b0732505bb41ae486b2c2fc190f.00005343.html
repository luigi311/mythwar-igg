It is very usefull, to do Santa Quest with callculator. Many questions can't be done quickly in memory, but with proper use od callculator you'll be able to solve them in 2-10 seconds. FRUITS QUESTION
Fruit sell out one fifth of the fruits and buy in X kilogram fruits. Now the total weight of fruits is one sixth more then the original weight. How heavy the original amount of fruits is?
X-kilograms of fruits
Answer=X*30/11
Calcullator:
1)Write down X
2)Press "Multiply" (*)
3)Write down number 30
4)Press "Divide" (/)
5)Write down number 11
6)Press "Equation" (=)



NEW APPLES QUESTION
There are A & B apples. Put X from A into B, than A and B is the same. Put Y from B into A, then the number of A is as twice as B. The question is how many apples of total?
X-First number
Y-Second number
Answer=(X+Y)*6
Calcullator:
1)Write down X
2)Press "Add" (+)
3)Write down Y
4)Press "Multiply" (*)
5)Write down number 6
6)Press "Equation" (=)

Each person has a rice bowl. 2 person share a soup bowl, 4 person share the dish bowl....
Answer has to be divisible by 4. If there are 2 or more answers divisible by 4 choose the one on the top.
Calcullator: (If you're unable to do this SIMPLE division in your memory)
1)Write down first number
2)Press "Divide" (/)
3)Write down 4
4)Press "Equation" (=)
5)Check wheter answer is a complete number if not, try next possibility.



CLOCK QUESTION (bugged)
An old clock beat once at exact time. The time of beating is the same as the exact time. And X beats needs Y seconds. How many seconds will it take to beat Z o'clock?
X- Number of beats
Y- Number of seconds that X-beats take
Z- Hour
There are two formulas here:
Answer=(Z-1)*(Y/(X-1))
Answer=(Z-1)*((X-1)/Y)
Usually the first formula (ideologically proper;P ) is right, but if you'll see it gave you the answer you can't match, use the second one. One of them is always right so don't worry. P.S. Sometimes you'll get fraction like 6,777777 or something- round it up to 6,8.
Calcullator:
1)Write down Y
2)Press "Divide" (/)
3)Substract 1 from X (beats) in your mind
4)Write it down
5)Press "Multiply" (*)
6)Substract 1 from Z (hours) in your mind
7)Write it down
8)Press "Equation" (=)
For the second formula switch point 1 with 3 and 4

SMALLEST NUMBER QUESTION
The summation of four continous natural number is X, so which one is the smallest?
X- Summation
Two possible solutions, choose the one that is easier for you:
Answer=(X/4)-1,5
Answer=(X-6)/4
Calcullator:
1)Write down X
2)Press "Divide" (/)
3)Write down number 4
4)Press "Equation" (=)
5)Substract 1,5 from result in your head.



SUBSTRAHEND QUESTION
One subtrahend is X, and the difference is as Y times as the subtrahend. The question is what is the difference.
X- Substrahend
Y- Multiplier
Answer=X*Y
Calcullator:
1)Write down X
2)Press "Multiply (*)
3)Write down Y
4)Press "Equation" (=)



REMAINDER QUESTION
The first figure is X, the second figure is Y. The next figure is the summation of the former two. In this way, what is the remainder of th 10th number divides by 3
X- First figure
Y- Second figure
Answer=Y%3
for those not knowing %-modulo sign:
Answer=remainder(Y/3)
(yes, you dont need X at all! This is little math trick;) )
Calcullator: (if you can't count it in your head...)
1)Write down Y
2)Press "Divide" (/)
3)Write down number 3
4)Press "Equation" (=)
5)Press "Add to Memory" (M+)
6)Press "Clear" (C)
7)Write down Y
8)Press "Substract" (-)
9)Press "Memory Recall" (MR)
10)Press "Equation" (=)
11)Press "Memory Clear" (MC)




FIVE FIGURES QUESTION
The mean of five figures is X. Arrange them from small to big. The summation of the first 3 figures is Y. The summation of the last 3 figures is Z. What is the figure in the middle of the 5 figures?
X- Mean
Y- Summation of first 3 figures
Z- Summation of last 3 figures
Answer=(Y+Z)-(5*X)
Calcullator:
1)Write down X
2)Press "Multiply" (*)
3)Write down number 5
4)Press "Equation" (=)
5)Press "Add to Memory" (M+)
6)Write down Y
7)Press "Add" (+)
8)Write down Z
9)Press "Substract" (-)
10)Press "Memory Recall" (MR)
11)Press "Equation" (=)
12)Press "Memory Clear" (MC)
