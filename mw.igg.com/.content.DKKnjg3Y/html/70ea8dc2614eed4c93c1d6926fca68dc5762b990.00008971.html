Cruxis Assult
Date:2007-07-05 Author:FiReBaLLER
    Welcome. To the third story of my series. I really don't know how long my series will last. But I will make this story long to speed up the process. Ok, in the last story "Derris-Kharlon War" we find Mythrica and her team trying to reborn bidz. But things don’t go there way when they take a wrong turn and meet more of FireBaller's companions. Hopefully after this story I will only have 2 to make this a five story novel. "Cruxis Assault" is a hard story. I took me a while to come up with something. Remember, im basing this stuff off Tales OF Symphonia. In this story you will get to meet a few new characters. So now I will shut up and start with "Cruxis Assault". Please enjoy.
    
     "Cruxis Assault"
    
    **one day in wars against Los_Paladins**
    bidz: ....
    
    mythrica: something wrong?
    
    Pascal: yea, you don’t look to good...
    
    bidz: im fine, I think its just my head, I think I can hear a voice.. come to think it sounds like fireballer...
    
    darkstar: ohh my theory was right! I knew this would happen, I did a scientific research after we got back from sewer, fireballer is trying to talk to you.
    
    bidz: well, he can bug off that pest.
    
    darkstar: ummm bidz, since you are the chosen and fireb and talk to you in your head, he can severely hurt you head, make it worst. You might want to talk to him.
    
    bidz: fine, but only for a second.
    
    ***fireballer comes out of bidz head in a smoky faded look in the form of him***
    
    darkstar: even more impressive! he can not only talk in your head but appear in-front as us as well! AMAZING!
    
    pascal: dude, why do you act like a smarty pants, really.... You always have to be the brained around here.
    
    fireballer: ummm guys.....
    
    darkstar: MAYBE becuase I go to school pascy
    
    fireballer: guys....
    
    pascal: ATLEAST I act responsible!
    
    darkstar: you call yelling at me responsible!?
    
    fireballer: .....GUYS! *uses FireIV* *they burn!*
    
    Fireballer: ok, now that I have your attention im here for bidz... As I will take the chosen as the new body for me since you guys destroyed me. Even though I was happy. Yes, I was tired of livening, doesn’t mean I wanna stop it.
    
    bidz: no way!
    
    fireballer: that’s not all, I want to help you guys up against the Los_Paladins Guild, you guys have them for wars and there a big strong hold.
    
    mythrica: he’s right. we have no choice but to take him.
    
    **war begins. Fireballer is im mythrica's team with mythrica,fireballer,bidz,Griggyman, and darkstar.**
    **battle begins**
    
    darkstar - chaos
    mythrica - Ice II
    fireballer- "guys let me handle this."
     - big bang! "you just might die!"
    fireballer - "I stand a full light of the heavens who command thy, to open the gates of hell. Come forth divine lighting! THIS ENDS NOW!" indignation!
    
    **they win battle** Los_Paladins guild fled the battleground and legacy wins.**
    **after war is far from over bidz comes to talk to fireballer**
    
    bidz: you ok? aren’t you cold? I mean its snowing.. you look cold... something up? looks like something happen from the past that I don’t know about. mind sharing?
    
    fireballer: ...On that day. Blython had a record snowfall. It looks just like the city of woodlingor.
    
    bidz: what are you talking about all of a sudden?
    
    fireballer: Ah. just a story about the past like you said. I suddenly felt like talking about it.
    
    bidz: well. if you want to talk.. im all ears.
    
    fireballer: I was so excited to see snow for the first time. and I made a snowman in the garden with my mother. Then suddenly. the snowman feel apart. Red snow began to fall.
    
    bidz: red snow?
    
    fireballer: yes. it was my mother's blood. she was murdered. as she fell she grabbed my shoulder and told me "you should have never been born." Look bidz I don’t want to talk about it... it bring back bad memories..
    
    bidz: ok.
    
    **the alarm goes off. mythrica gets on the microphone and announced**
    
    mythric: this is your leader! Legacy! we are under attacked by Cruxis Guild. Get into your parties! Fight back!
    
    freballer: looks like I can trust Kratos now..
    
    bids: fire! how could you!
    
    Fireballer: thank you. thank you very much. *grabs bidz and teleports away*
    
    **Cruxis cannons start blowing down the legacy's HQ.** and its destroyed...**
