Challenge Limit, Challenge Yourself

How can your life be meaningful? It's a hard question, isn't it? There are probably no 100% correct answers, but one thing is sure we should challenge ourselves as we live, and try to be the best you. We will be holding an event to offer you an opportunity to challenge yourself.

Event Duration:
3:00 pm to 5:00 pm , September 6th  EDT(GMT-4)

Event Contents:
Our GM will post an announcement through the system channel when the event begins. To participate in this event, players should go to Sunset and register with GM  at near NPC: Existence. Special attention should be paid that this challenge event is only for individuals not groups, if you participate in this event with others, all of your qualifications will be canceled. You are supposed to finish 12 rounds of survival challenge on your own, good luck!
If you can make it, you can not only obtain a lot of Exp but also 1 AOD+1  

Event Reward: AOD+1
