Item Mall Halloween Event Advanced

Dear players,

In order to give more opportunities to players, the Item Mall Halloween Event we announced on Oct.18th will be held from Oct. 20th to Nov.8th ahead of the schedule time.  


During this event, Chocolate, Icy Crystal Pack and Oceanic Stone Pack will be released. In addition, Change Lotion and Devil’s Blood will be sold 50% off. At the same time, we will give player with highest consuming records of buying point cards from Oct. 20th to Nov. 8th the Saint Beast-Ocean Loong based on the statistics we compile. Player ranking No.2 will be given a Deity Pet. Player ranking No.3 will be given a pet with growth rate of 1,300 (Christmas pet is not included). Players buying point cards through Paypal can not participate in this activity. The Winners List will be published at official website on Nov.9th when prize will be issued.

The ranking of this event will be listed on the official website and renewed at 0:00 every day. Players can check your rank directly to ensure the fairness of the event.

The event time will be EST, please check your local time.


Notice: Other Halloween Events time will not be affected.


Happy Halloween!
