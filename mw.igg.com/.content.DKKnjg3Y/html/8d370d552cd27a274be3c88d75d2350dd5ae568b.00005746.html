 hmm. my ranking is like this:

1. berserker
2. glittering tiger
3. flame phoenix
4. dark dream
5. holy dragon
6. chaos wisp

don't know ocean loong and stone tortoise, so i don't include it in. but HB is below any saint and deity to me as the most important thing is to be able to add +30 crit rate. HB has 20 extra hit rate but that doesn't come any close to outweighing +30 crit rate. at least, in my opinion.

and from personal experience, these pets all have high HP (building mostly sta, that is).. so high that you wonder what's the point is the HP going any higher than it's now. of course higher HP harder to die.. but it barely dies anyway. so it boils down to natural resists that matter much much more.

making any of these as a tanker is a waste because there are a lot of other pets to do the job. and if it's an attacker, then crit is a must. or you can opt to berserk but that makes the use limited because of low hp. plus it's harder to resist crit. many things have melee resist rending berserk less effective.

if you think PK, then flame phoenix and dark dream will be higher because of their speed, but in general their speed work against them because of human skills. since we make it attacker, you won't have any resist to human skills, not enough anyway.

berserker is very optimal for offense. it's not too fast to be affected by skills but not too slow to lag behind in a long battle. high attack + high str growth, so it comes first on the list. glitter tiger better sta and str but no 160 hit rate. how sad.

3 and 4 are actually tough choices.. may even tie. dark dream has extra dodge rate (i think?) and poison is very useful. but FP growth is better in all areas. HP is rather important here because they are fast and absorb a lot of damage.

then basically what follows is whatever you can add +30 crit. holy dragon has protect, which is nice.. but pet's protect is not that good and using it on itself won't do anything much. chaos wisp doesn't have any single-target skill, which means you will always wake up the fastest enemy if you use skills.. kinda funny.
