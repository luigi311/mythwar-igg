IGG Journalists Recruited

If you have insightful eyes, ears that hear the faintest whispers and fingers that don't mind typing, we would like you to join us as an IGG Journalist! This is a very unique opportunity for you to share your feelings about games, problems encountered, epic battles waged, fierce PvP, and funny stories. If this has excited your eyes, ears and fingers, then start by filling in an application form now!

Post your application according to the format below in the Event Zone of our Forum
Quote:
Forum Name:
Age
Occupation (Student or Office Worker)
How long are you usually online
Application Motives
Future Work Plan(s)
Post an article to sign up
The articles should be original. Quote's can not exceed 50% of the article.  

Requirements
1. Love IGG games, Love IGG forum
2. Have insightful opinions on news and enjoy writing  
3. Forum level should be higher than member. No limit to character level.


Sign-up Duration：February  2nd  to March 11th
Ideal candidates to be journalists will be announced on：March 17th

All IGG players are qualified to participate in the event.




Journalist Rules

 Responsibilities
 1. You should hand in your news drafts on time if it will be late, there must be a valid reason. If you get stuck on other matters, you should let us know in advance, so we can rearrange the schedule and responsibilities.

2. You should hand in 3 to 5 news drafts every month, and there are no limits to the type of articles handed in.

Rewards:  
You will obtain a certain number of forum money according to your journalist level and the quality of your news.
If your news is considered as essential or is used by IGG officials, you will obtain extra rewards.

Journalist Levels
 Intern Journalist
Staff Journalist
Senior Journalist
Editor

Section Name and News Types
 Section Name: News under the Sun
News Types:    Event Report, Anecdotes, Game Stars'Interview, Current Affairs  

Notes
If journalists can't finish their assignments, their news drafts don't meet our requirements for 2 consecutive months, or they break forum rules severely, their press qualifications will be cancelled.
