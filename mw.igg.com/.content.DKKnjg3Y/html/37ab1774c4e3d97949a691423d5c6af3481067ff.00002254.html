Big Sale ! Storm Approaching!

It's been 2 months since the Myth War II Open Beta began, and our players' enthusiasm has totally surpassed our expectations. To give something back for their continued support, we will be holding the " Big Sale!Storm Approaching!" event. Bring your friends to have fun with you !
From August 27th to August 31st, there will be a big Item Mall promotion. The prices of selected items are now 50% off. This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity, you can't miss it.
The items are as followed:

Source of Life

Obtain 999,999 reserved health and restores full health after combat when used
Source of Magic

Obtain 999,999 reserved mana and restores full mana after combat when used
Super Source of Life

Obtain 9,999,999 reserved health and restores full health after combat when used
Super Source of Magic

Obtain 999,999 reserved mana and restores full mana after combat when used
Great Pine Bag

Use to redeem 30 bottles of Great Pine. Each bottle can restore 15000 HP and MP.
Whammy Egg

Player will receive a pet egg when used
Whammy Egg

Open it to obtain a pet egg of the Guard, Leopard Wolf or the Howling Beast, randomly.
Fantastic Whammy Egg

Open it to obtain a pet egg of Demon Punisher, Crazy Phantom or Capilla, randomly.
Peculiar Whammy Egg

Open it to obtain a pet egg of Crazy Guard, Stone Giant or Mosquito, randomly.
Full-mob Card

Encounter twice as many monsters as members of your group when used
Hero Medal

Donate 8 million gold for the guild and get most awesome resistance within 30 days after used.
Rings for Espousal

A couple of rings that have witnessed the love of lovers.Teleports you to your love(r).11 changes
Home Upgrader

Upgrades your home to an exalted house.Lasts 30 days.
Nexus of Agility

Used to increase Agility by 200 temporarily, can fight for 5 times
Nexus of Power

Used to increase strength by 200 temporarily, can fight for 5 times
Nexus of Magic

Used to increase intellect by 200 temporarily, can fight for 5 times
Nexus of Life

Used to increase stamina by 200 temporarily, can fight for 5 times
Holy Beast SSP

Contains 2 shape shifting potions of Glitter Tiger, Flame Phoenix, Tortoise and Ocean Loong respectively
Rebirth Shapeshifting

Contains 8 Vials of shapeshifting potions which will change you into a reborn class at random
Bag of Survival

Contains 10 Survival Scrolls. You and your pet will receive the same number of Exp as the gold obtained after completing the Survival quest with the Survival Scroll.

Envelop Bag

Redeems 10 cute envelopes when used. Every letter sent will use up one envelope.

Chamberlaine Invite

Allows you to employ a chamberlaine which can be used for depositing pets and also serves as bank after used
