Olympic Inspirations


Olympic symbolize the unity and the strength of the people around the world.
We will be holding an Olympics event in the forum to offer a chance for players to show their creativity. Abundant rewards await you!

Event Content: Design pictures with the theme of the Olympics and Myth War.

Event Rules:
 1. After the event begins, participants can reply to this post with pictures you’ve designed.
2. Your post should contain your server name, character ID and character name.
3. Your picture should be relative to both Olympics and Myth War.
4. We don’t appreciate pictures bearing rude or impolite messages and we don’t think anyone else would either. Remember these kinds of messages are NOT allowed.
5. All pictures should be original. If there are 2 identical or mostly identical pictures, the one sent first will count.
6. All participants should obey the event rules. Otherwise, they will be disqualified.

Event Duration: From April 30th to May 9th (GMT-5)

Selection Duration: From May 9th to May 12th (GMT-5)

Event Reward:

1st place: (1) Any Deity Pet
2nd place: (2) Any Chinchilla or Any Aod+3
3rd place: (3) Any Christmas Pet or Any Aod+3
