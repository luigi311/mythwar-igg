8# A A A Post at 2008-7-28 12:07 PM  Show author
I caught loads of pets in closed version and found my experiences were as follow:
It is totally random.
ok I'll elaborate. I didn't find any difference in ctu a higher lvl pet than myself to a lower lvl one. I also didn't notice any remarkable difference in the ease of ctu if I weakened it 1st.
Also adding status effects didn't seem to improve my chances. And also capturing a "Chief" pet didn't make it any better in gr or build to a normal one.
I also tried to watch the mobs atk and and hp when in battle then capturing it to see if build was related to the behaviour/atk/hp of the one I caught. I did notice that if I caught a high atk Tauren then it was likely to be str based, and same goes for a higher hp one being sta based. But in all cases gr was totally random.
Back to adding status and higher lvl pets.
While I have stated there is no difference in the chance rate of ctu or whether it is lvl affected, you must realise it is easier to catch a lower lvl pet than a higher lvl pet, due to the battle alone being easier and less strain on your pots and own pet.
Also, it makes no difference whether you have a pet in battle or not, and I strongly suggest when trying to ctu a higher lvl pet you have you pet in battle to pot you mp/hp.
I'm hummy, and I think hummies have easiest chance to ctu due to the status effects.
As stated above, hypno makes for a easy time. Just sleep it, ctu a few rnds, re-sleep and repeat. Keep pet potting mp and its just a mattter of time.
I caught high lvl Tauren when I was lvl 30 in Ashes Pit 4 using this method. And other than the difficulty of the battle, found no difference in trying to ctu,say a big foot in Cursed Abyss.

Anyway, this is just my experience upto now. Don't mean I'm right.
