Can you hold on Until the End?

In order to help you pass the time during the maintenance, we will be holding an event while our servers are down.  

Event Flows:
 The aim of the game is to spell new words by using the characters of the words that we offer. You can copy the words from the last post and take turns to add words to the list. You must add at least one word but three words at most for each post. 5 qualified participants who posted the last 5 works will be rewarded. The winners list will be announced at the end of the thread after the end of the event.

Note: Words with obscene/vulgar/offensive meanings will not be accepted.

For Example
The words we offer:          "Myth War"   
The first post could be        " my"   
The second post could be  "my, math"   
The third post could be       "my, math, may …"      
………                                        .
……….                                         

Reply Format : "answer" server name, and character name.

Start Time: 1:00pm (GMT-5), April 24th        
End Time:  4:00pm (GMT-5), April 24th   

Rewards: Each Winner will obtain 1 Icy Crystal Pack

ps:this thread will be open at 1:00pm (GMT-5), April 24th     

Please read the instruction carefully
