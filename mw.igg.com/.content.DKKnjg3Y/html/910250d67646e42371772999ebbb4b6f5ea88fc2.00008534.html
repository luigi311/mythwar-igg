The 5 Soulstone Quest

Index
I) Soulstone of Light
II) Soulstone of Darkness
III) Soulstone of Fire
IV) Soulstone of Ice
V) Soulstone of Wind

Level Requirement: 5
Other Requirements: None
Quest reward: Title Wanderer, Soulstone of Light

Activating the Quest: Head to Elders Zone At Woodlingor(366,77) and talk to the three elders in order.
First talk to Elder of Dawn and listen to his stories and you'll earn 60 experience points.
Secondly, talk to Elder of Sight and listen to his stories to receive 120 experience points.
Talk to Elder of Night and listen to his stories. He'll tell you about the soulstones and whereabouts you can seek them.
Check your quest log to make sure you've done everything right. Your quest log should now contain this.

Now head to the Bar at Woodlingor(133,555).
Talk to Barkeeper and choose the option "I'm here to find out some information"

Tell the barkeeper "I heard someone here knows about the soulstone!"

Choice 1:Give the barkeeper 50 Gold for information.
Choice 1 Part 2: Give the Bar keeper another 100 gold for the information.
Choice 2: "Tell me or I'll be nasty" will put u into a fight.

Choice 1 or Choice 2 will lead you knowing more information about the soulstone.
The barkeeper now will tell you about Drunkard Fenny
Check your quest log and make sure is updated like the example picture.

Now head near the Revival Arena to look for Drunkard Fenny at coordinates 531,791.
Now you'll have three options to pick from.
Choice 1: "What are you doing here?"  Gives you an answer of what's drunkard fenny doing around this area.
Choice 2: "I want to know about the soulstone." He will only exchange the information for a bottle of winter melon wine.
Choice 3: Same as choice 2, he'll tell u violence will not solve the problem and request you to bring him a bottle of winter melon wine for the information.
Check your quest log and make sure is updated.

Now head back to the Bar at Woodlingor(133,555).
Talk to the barkeeper and tell him "I want to buy winter melon wine"
Barkeeper will then ask you why, answer him "Drunkard Fenny wants it!"
Barkeeper will now ask if you're sure you wish to waste money on a drunkard like fenny.
Choosing "Yes" will allow you to buy the Winter Melon Wine from barkeeper for 1000 gold.
Choosing "No! But can you help me?" will trigger a sub quest to find worm skin from Capilla/ Green Capilla for 5 gold each.

Either choice will lead you to buying the Winter Melon wine one way or another.
IMPORTANT: Make sure your inventory is not full while buying the Winter Melon Wine.
Check your quest log to make sure is updated and you have a bottle of Winter Melon Wine inside your inventory.

Now head back to the area near Revival Arena and find Drunkard Fenny(531,791).
Use the Give Function (Alt+G) and give the Winter Melon Wine to Fenny.
He'll now tell you that Gambler John has the soulstone.
Your quest log should now be updated like so.

Head to the Woodlingor Trading Zone area and walk right to see Gambler John(233,117).
Tell John "I'm here to find the soulstone!"

You'll now be given two options:
"Ok, Deal!" will start a gamble with him. If you lose the gamble, you'll be required to bring him a Mushroom Spore from teethors or poison Teethors to try again.
Guessing the correct answer on gamble and he'll tell you the soulstone is with Deserter
"Give me the soulstone or else I'll kill you!" will start a fight, and one more after. He'll then tell u the soulstone is with Deserter
WARNING: The fight is not easy and is not recommended for lower level players. You can ask for help for a more experienced player to help you with the fight if you do not wish to gamble.
You'll receive 3000 experience points per person if you fight with john.
For example, you + another two players helping u fighting john will earn the whole party 9000 experience points each.

Now check your quest log and make sure is updated.

Head to Tree of Life 2 to find Deserter
It is advised you save a teleport scroll at Woodlingor if you haven't done so. Teleport scrolls are for easy traveling from one place to another. The save points are decided by you. Click on the teleport scroll and press use once to save the coordinates of where you're standing. Press use again to teleport to the spot you saved.
Note: If you got lost in Tree of Life, please refer to maps at the forum for the portal coordinates or kindly ask someone to bring you there.
Warning: If you're a lower level player that is challenging this quest, you'll be required to ask some experienced players to help you battle Deserter as it does hurt your Hit Points to an extend where you cannot solo.
