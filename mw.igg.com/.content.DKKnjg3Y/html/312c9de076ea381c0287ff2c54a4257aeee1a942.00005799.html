  Report for replicating bug fixed
Dear players,

Recently, we have received many problems that players reported by many means, including official BBS, unofficial BBS, mails and other contact means. The problems are focused as follows:

1. Accounts got hacked: Account info got changed and items got stolen.
2. There are still bugs existing in the game.
3. There are no new content added to the game, such as new events, maps, quests, etc.
4. Cheaters may still copy items and money in the game.We will answer these questions roundly next:


1. As for the problem that accounts got stolen, we have investigated deeply and issued a notice on account security on here and also posted a post on here.

However, we still need to dispel some players' misunderstanding for most players thought it was the faultiness of official safety precautions about accounts that results in the account loss after they visit official web or official BBS. In fact, our official web and official BBS are safe. Actually, most accounts are stolen because of the following reasons we will mention, especially being deceived, which is beyond our ability. We ask for your understanding and expect all the players to look out for account safety

a) Some cheaters send e-mail or messages to players in the name of us official and ask for account information.
Official Prompt: Official e-mail is ended with @igg.com and we will never ask for account information by any means.

b) Some people defraud players of their trust by publicizing fake information on unofficial BBS or in the game, and then induce players to visit unsafe webs and download unsafe programs. For example, they promulgate fake videos to teach players how to copy items and ask players to download cheating program which has been added Trojan or other viruses in it.
Official Prompt: We hope you can play in the normal way without using any third party program. We will ban the accounts that ever use cheating program or take advantage of game bug. It is recommended to scan your computer periodically. Besides, please don't log into any unofficial webs with IGG account info to assure account safety.

c) Some people defraud players of their trust by many means and then ask for sharing accounts and steal important account info.
Official Prompt: Keep account info secret and don't share accounts with others.

2. Most players point out that there are still bugs existing in the game. They also submitted various new event plans and good advice to improve Myth War. We really appreciate these players who love IGG and Myth War. We are very sorry for no new content added and some game bug. Some players find that Myth War 2.0 have been released in China but IGG still hasn't taken action. This is because of the following considerations:

a) It needs some time to consult with the game developer on details about issuing the new version.

b) The new version game is quite different from the old one still needs some improvement. And we are waiting for a steadier and better edition.

c) The data of new Myth War are quite different from the old ones. It was still impossible to transfer all of the original data completely, that is, players are unable to take their level, pets, items to the new version and they need to level from the beginning like a newbie. However, Myth War Team of IGG never give up. We are always asking the game developer to solve this key problem.
We IGG never abandon Myth War. We never stop communicating with the game developer either. And we have received permission of the game developer. In this period, the game developers are trying their best to improve the new edition and data transfer between new edition and the old one, which leads to the delay of bug modification. We IGG will release the new Myth War in the next season. You can learn information about Myth War II ahead of schedule in the preview website of Myth War II.

3. At last, what we need to emphasize is that the bug for coping items and gold has been fixed in Myth War 1.0.48.35. Please do not try to use any third party program to copy items or gold. We can't help to find the accounts that may be stolen by this back.


The reason why we mention so many things above is that we expect the players who love IGG and Myth War not to be suborned and deluded by cheaters or liars any longer. We do not hope to hear any account be stolen any longer. We are in expectation that you won't lose trust or confidence in IGG and Myth War because of some fake information or rumor spread by cheaters or liars. We sincerely hope that all IGG players can find the games you are interested in on IGG and enjoy yourselves. We will do our best continuously to improve the services and the quality of the games.


Mythwar Online Team, IGG
2007-07-10
