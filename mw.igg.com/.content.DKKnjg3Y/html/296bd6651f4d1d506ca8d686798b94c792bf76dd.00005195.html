new item idea

Lets Face it no matter how hard we try there is never enough room in our inventory for all the needed items, more so for the non reborn. You carry sets of gears. Fire, chaos, melee, int(spamming) not to mention all your fruits, and nonsense. In no time your bags are full and your bank is full. Always tossing things around, making mules and then searching all mules to find one thing. Why not save the hassle and make mini backpacks/bags? Small slot bags that can hold certian items, such as equips, gems, pots and so on. even a 4 slot bag till save a whole lot of room. So you can put in any item and take it out at any time and make it to where its not binding. you can sell them trade them(only if empty of course) It would be alot easier if we had more storage space, then always tossing items to make room for the new. and make them small enough price to finish off our mall points.
