  [Guide] Mercenaries
First of all, I have found very little next to none usefull information on the forum about mercenaries, yet many questions.
It seems to me almost none do know much about these additional fighters, or those who does, doesn't want to share.

So here it is

The mercenary will help you to get additional experience points and items.

You can go to Mercenary Chief (Blython 148,605). That is just east of teleporter.

The mercenary need batteries to start fighting.
Those can be bought at any groceror or the Energy Seller (Blython 153,609) next to Mercenary Chief.

More batteries = more health for the mercenary = he will easier survive hence bring back the items and experience for you.

---
When you talk to the Chief you can check your current mercenary points, what are those you ask?
They are an indication of how strong your mercenary are. More points, the stronger, the less health it loses when battling and the more items it can bring home.
(I have yet no hard numbers on when special things occur like it being able to go to next area etc.)
---
When you choose to activate your mercenary you will be able to choose which area you want it to fight in.
You can choose from:
Sunset Plains(beginner)
Outcast City(advanced)
Badlands(expert)
Bone Desert(master)
---
Sunset Plains(beginner) will be your area of choice for a very long time. I have ATM 3000 pts and still there.
---

When that is done the Chief asks you to set a fighting style.
You can choose from the below:
Reckless
Offensive
Normal
Defensive
Cautious
Sorrowless
---
I have only been using Offensive, Normal and Defensive. Learning that offensive is a much better choice.
Actually I felt that the mercenary died much earlier on Defensive, not finishing the battles but just taking damage.
---

Next thing to set is the time you want it to fight:
1 hour
2 hours
5 hours
---
Keep in mind that the longer the mercenary stays out, the more health it will lose. So if you want it to survive use more batteries.
---

Now with all settings done, the Chief will ask you for the batteries to replenish the mercenaries health.
Those can as above said be bought for example from the Energy Seller just next to the Cheif for 2000 gold each.
You can recharge the mercenary with as many batteries you like, each battery replenishes 3600 health.
Note: The batteries are consumed when you recharge the mercenary,
so even if it has health left when time is up you will not get it back.
---
***
---
Now the mercenary is out there fighting and you can go back to what you were doing.
But if you want to, you can still keep active by monitoring the mercenaries progress with a Communicator.
Tooltip:
Communicator
Borg mercenary setting control device
Item Level: 1
Race: All Races

You can buy these in 20packs at the Item mall which is located on the homepage.
Communicator Bag
Intro - Contain 20 communicators and players can know the mercenary
chief's action when they employ the mercenary chief to fight, and get all the reward from the battle.
Point number - 80 Point

The communicator will let you manage the mercenary meanwhile it's fighting, you will be told some about the events that happened to the mercenary since you last checked it, how much health it has left and you can choose to change combat style. Using it once an hour will bring you more experience, I don't know how much.
-
Erendis Said in another thread:
And you get 25 % more exp if you are using communicator every hour.
However, communicator from item mall can only be used once.
-
The communicator can though be used as many times as you like, simply deposit into the bank.
If you still have it when you go to talk to the Mercenary Chief, he will take it from you.
-
Some also say you need it to get the items, that is not true.
Edit

This info is outdated, and does not go hand in hand anymore. Will try to update.

Without using the communicator I get 15,000-25,000xp and a top of 20 items. (this is not confirmed, but what I have noticed lately.)

Edit Note
***
This is a thing I found browsing the Item mall:

Time Machine

Intro - Mercenary gets double exp and skill when used during the quest.
Point number - 50 Point

I assume it do just what it says, I have not used it myself as I have no mall points.
Just to clarify and save some people eventual questions. When you launch the mercenary that is actually a "quest". As why it is described to be used during the quest, simply means while the mercenary is still out fighting.
***
End of Edit Note

---
***
---
When the mercenary returns some information will pop up about what happened during the fights, those will also go away pretty soon. If it lives, it will gain 10 mercenary points for each hour out fighting (10 for 1 hour, 50 for 5 hours). The Mercenary Chief will tell you how much experience you got, and say "Items over level x will not be given to you." Whereas x is the amount of items you will gain. But if it did not survive all you get is minor amount of mercenary points (1-4).
So far I have only gained mana/hp fruits of different kind. (More merc points/longer time = more items a go)
---

I have got between 0 and 21 items each time.
The items I have got from Sunset Plains is:
Wild Lychee +7000MP
Graded Pear +4000MP
Graded Banana +5000HP
Medium Red Potion +40% HP
Red Salad +8000HP
---
I am not sure if other items will be handed out from other areas, as my mercenary has not surived anywhere else yet than in Sunset Plains.
---
Now, how cost effective is all this, is what most people ask themselves I guess. At around 800 points i were using 20! batteries to survive 5 hours offensive in sunset plains. At 1200+ I was down to using a sure 9 and still trying to reduce. I used 1 battery less each round, just to see when it dies. Around 2000 points using less batteries, 7 is enough for survival.
At around 2500 I started using 4 batteries only, and at 3000 points still doing that.

Ranging from 1,2 or 5 hours depending on what you feel like.
