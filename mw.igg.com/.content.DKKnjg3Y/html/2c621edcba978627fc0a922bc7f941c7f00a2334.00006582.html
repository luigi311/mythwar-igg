Dear players,

Since our "Fortune Bombing" event has received so much attention and participation from everyone, the Myth War team is glad to announce the new rewards for this event so as to give you something back for your continuous support. We hope this time you can find your favorites among the new ones listed below:

The Top Prize: Stone Tortoise or Ocean Loong

Unexpected Prize: (Dark Dream or Howling Beast) and Any One Armor of Dream+3(Ordain Warden, Heavenly Armor, Diadem of Serenity)

Lucky Prize: (Any one of Christmas pets or Sand Giant) and Any One Armor of Dream+2((Ordain Warden, Heavenly Armor, Diadem of Serenity)

Surprising Prize: 1.3 growth Intelligent pet

Honorable Mentioned: 50 Fortune Bombing integrals

The adjustments will come into force at EST 0:00 (GMT-5) Jan. 1st.

Enjoy and have fun. ^-^
