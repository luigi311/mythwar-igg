Dear player,
Have you ever participated in our events which GM hosted, like PK, hunting and so on. Do you fully enjoy yourself in these events or not? Imagine that if you are the promoter who is responsible for the promotion of the events, how are you going to plan them and put them into practice? Now IGG provides you this opportunity to design online events suitable for yourself. Remember, don’t lose chance to show your originality talent and share your joys with us.

Time :21st May-28th May

Rule:
FYI, here is an Event Scheme, please start to design your own schemes. When finished, post your schemes on the forum with the title of “event+topic” as a flag. Remark: the event should be online event which is not need to amend game program

Rewards:
You will obtain 300 points from Item Mall, even the chance to operate the schemes into practice with GM as long as your ideas adopted by IGG official.

Criteria:
Player’s popularity for the events schemes will be considered as an important aspect. Further more, if any two idea schemes happen to fall into collision, official will pick one of them according to the real situation.

PS: For players who love this game and willing to work as event promoter, please complete the Form, then send to event@igg.com
